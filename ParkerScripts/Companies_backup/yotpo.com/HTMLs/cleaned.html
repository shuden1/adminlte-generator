Yotpo’s Recruiting Process

Yotpo’s most important asset? Our team of dedicated, creative, and smart people! While the exact recruiting process varies by location, below you’ll find the most common steps to expect.

01

Application and Resume Screening

Expect a confirmation email once your application is received. Our team reviews every resume typically within a week.
02

Initial Phone Screen

Your Talent Acquisition Partner will learn more about you, your goals, discuss compensation, and tell you more about Yotpo.
03

Hiring Manager Interview

This call is either an in-person or virtual interview with the team leader. Use this time to ask questions and learn more about the role.
04

Practical Exercise and Presentation

Some roles may involve a practical exercise to help us evaluate relevant skills, such as coding, problem-solving, writing, etc.
05

Meet the team

Meet additional leaders, business partners, team members, and HR. Each interviewer assesses different aspects of your skills.
