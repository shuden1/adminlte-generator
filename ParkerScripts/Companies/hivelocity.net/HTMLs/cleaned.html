Data Analyst

Hivelocity is on the lookout for a Data Analyst to pioneer their Business Intelligence team. This role centers on transforming data into actionable business insights. As a Data Analyst, you’ll be involved in the entire data analysis lifecycle, from requirement gathering and data wrangling to building data models, crafting visualizations, and presenting data-driven stories. You’ll need experience with SQL and proficiency in creating visualizations with business intelligence tools. Key responsibilities include complex SQL writing, learning specific data sources to become a Subject Matter Expert (SME), leading data analytics projects, and creating reports, models, and dashboards in Superset. You’ll engage with various stakeholders to understand business needs, develop documentation, provide training, and participate in Agile processes. The ideal candidate holds a degree in Science, Analytics, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Finance, or Economics, with experience in SQL, BI, and analytics. Familiarity with dbt, MySQL, Superset, Git, JIRA, and agile methodologies is advantageous. The role offers a base salary between $65,000 – $75,000 with annual bonus, based on skills and experience alignment with these requirements.This offer is a fantastic opportunity to be a part of a rapidly growing company with great benefits, rewarding challenges, and a flexible schedule.

Key Responsibilities

  • Able to write complex SQL including multi-table joins, grouping and aggregation, common table expressions, and conditional filters
  • Learning to serve as a Subject Matter Expert (SME) in one or more data sources and its related Business Subject
  • Lead four or more departmental data analytics projects from identifying sources in the beginning, to delivering complete dashboards that tell a story with the data
  • Demonstrated proficiency in creating reports, data modeling, and dashboards in Superset or similiar BI tool.
  • Perform adhoc reporting, data extraction, and analysis

Qualifications:

  • Degree specialized in a Science, Analytics, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Finance or Economics.
  • Strong experience with SQL.
  • Basic level knowledge of data warehousing, analytics, ETL concepts & best practices.
  • Scripting experience with Python and dbt a plus
  • Basic understanding of Agile, Kanban, Story points and other agile development techniques.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: From $65,000.00 per year
