Work in tech by the sea, with Amazon here in Swansea.


Recently acquired by Amazon, Veeqo is going places fast and we are looking for the best talent to join us on our amazing journey. We are a friendly, kind, and modest bunch, who work very hard but value healthy balance, family, friends and all the good stuff that makes us smile. We also have some incredible benefits - and for those who love dogs, we have a few in our Swansea office!


A career at Veeqo truly is like no other. If you’re ambitious, smart, and hungry to be part of one of the most exciting tech companies in Wales... we want to hear from you.
