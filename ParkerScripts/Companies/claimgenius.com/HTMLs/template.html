Claim Genius welcomes you to join us

Claim Genius is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology startup company with a niche in image analytics. A career with us entails developing modernized, state-of-the-art AI-based solutions to make complex insurance-related processes simpler for our customers. We strive to provide elegant solutions to solve all inconveniences of automotive insurance claims.

What We Do

Claim Genius’s patent pending technology is bringing forth a new comprehensive automated approach for automobile claims providing critical information at the first notice of loss (FNOL). We provide:

  • Instant AI car damage assessment
  • Instant recommendation of repairable or total loss

Claim Genius’s vision is to make “Touchless Claims a Reality”.

What to expect from a career with us

When we grow together as an organization, you will get to handle rich, current AI Technology. We believe a career truly flourishes by tackling challenges that spark innovation and problem-solving. If you also believe in the same philosophy, you’re in the right place.

Build your career in AI

At Claim Genius, you are part of a high-performance team that focuses on driving innovation, critical solutions and building next-gen technology that will disrupt the automotive insurance industry. If you are excited about massive scale, quality, performance, craftsmanship, and want a fair challenge and a leap forward, we think you are a right fit. Come join us at our Nagpur or Hyderabad Office and kickstart a career in the field of Artificial Intelligence.

A career with us also makes you eligible for some sweet perks:

  • Competitive Salary
  • Flexible Hours

  • Cash Bonuses
