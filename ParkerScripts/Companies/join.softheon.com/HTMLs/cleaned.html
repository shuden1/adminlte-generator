Our Mission and Values

Powering what's next in healthcare. Our mission is powering growth to make healthcare more productive, intelligent, and successful.

"Work to Make a Life, Not Just a Living"

We believe in providing you with all the tools and resources to remain connected to our vibrant, fast-paced and ever-changing community.

Environment

Softheon cultivates an environment for success by encouraging all employees to follow their passions and ideas. Tools like WorkTango allow our employees to send well-deserved recognition to redeem for gift cards!

Culture

We work hard, but we play hard too. Engagement activities, holiday celebrations, and volunteer opportunities are frequent. Friendly competition heats up during our annual charity Olympics event!

Benefits

On top of our partnership with Holisticly, our package includes:

 401k matching, medical, dental and vision, life insurance, referral bonuses, spot bonuses, and a generous PTO policy.

Growth

If you can dream it, you can do it. From LinkedIn Learning access to tuition reimbursement, we’re committed to fostering a continuous learning culture and supporting your professional goals.

Wellbeing

Inhale...exhale. Our Employee Assistance Program offers employees and their family members confidential, 24/7 support at no cost to help balance home life, relationships, health, and work.

A 100% Remote Workforce!

Our employees are located in 33 different states throughout the United States. We are a 100% remote, work from home workforce for employees based in the United States.
