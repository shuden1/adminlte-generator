Team

Our Design Department includes UX/UI and Marketing teams with different responsibility areas.

The UX/UI team designs products from concept ideas to gamification mechanics and interfaces. They constantly monitor innovations in the market and collaborate with developers to improve user experience.

Marketing Design Production creates all our brands online and offline communications. The team designs all kinds of ads like banners, videos, animation, landing pages, websites, billboards, exhibition stands, merch, and more. The website you read at the moment is made by the MDP team.

We value experience, interest in design and technology, and pay attention to critical thinking. It is essential to ask the right questions to the stakeholder, apply exceptional internal standards, view the analytics, and give the idea a good overall shape. But most importantly, there must be a spark between you and us!
