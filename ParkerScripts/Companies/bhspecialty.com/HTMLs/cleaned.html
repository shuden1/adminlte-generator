Not Your Usual Organization

BHSI first came to market in 2013 — an industry “newcomer,” out of the gate with one of the industry’s most experienced management teams and the strong balance sheet and capital of Berkshire Hathaway. Since then, we have been moving swiftly ahead, in lockstep with customer needs – building an enduring industry leader from the ground up.

We have excess & surplus underwriting capabilities, a hunger for large and complex risks. We are in a number of admitted markets, and expanding, steadily and strategically, to serve customers’ needs domestically and internationally.

Our culture is liberating and fast paced: We are purposefully non-bureaucratic and perpetually entrepreneurial, centered on doing what is right for our customers, our teammates, and our business.

High Expectations, Vast Opportunities

We are looking for individuals who share our devotion to the task…who show both an ability to excel and an excellence of character. People who want to do the heavy lifting — and find unprecedented opportunities in reach. People whose integrity and work ethic fit proudly under the Berkshire Hathaway banner.

At BHSI, we value diversity of backgrounds, experience, and perspectives. We are looking for individuals from every corner of our industry and the industries we serve and varying levels of experience.

BHSI offers a comprehensive benefits package. We are an equal opportunity employer.

Up for the Task?

At BHSI, every day is a chance to do something new. Drafting innovative policy wording. Bringing game-changing service capabilities into the fold. Walking the talk of best in class claims handling. It is all in a day’s work. As part of our team, you will fuel BHSI’s growth, shaping our future and that of our customers – collaboration by collaboration, innovation by innovation.
