Security alert

We are aware that organizations not affiliated with GoodData have been advertising non-existent job openings with our company. In recent weeks, these fraudulent campaigns have increased in effort and severity.

All current, official GoodData job openings are listed on our job portal at gooddata.com/company/careers. All communication with candidates is performed from @gooddata.com domain email address. Especially, we do not run any *gooddata.shop or *gooddata.life domain. Any offers received from sources other than our official gooddata.com domain are fraudulent, and we are by no means affiliated with such organizations. This includes communication through applications such as WhatsApp, Viber, Telegram, Facebook Messenger, and non-affiliate Facebook groups. If you have engaged with any of these fraudulent organizations regarding a job opening with GoodData, report it to your local law enforcement authorities immediately and alert our security team at security@gooddata.com. We take this fraudulent activity very seriously and are moving forward with all possible steps to bring such activity to an end.
