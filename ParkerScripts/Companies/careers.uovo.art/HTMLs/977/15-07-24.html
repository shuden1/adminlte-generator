UOVO sets a new standard in collections care

Founded by collectors who sought a new standard of art storage and services, UOVO is dedicated to providing pre-eminent logistics solutions for art, fashion, wine, archives, and collectibles. Headquartered in New York with locations across the continental United States, UOVO is operated by a team of industry professionals with decades of experience.

UOVO, pronounced “woh-voh,” translates to “egg” in Italian and reflects our central mission to protect fragile objects.



Why UOVO?

At UOVO, we pride ourselves on delivering best-in-class solutions for our clients while fostering a company culture that is inclusive, collaborative, and motivating. We are committed to building a first-rate, diverse team by recruiting from a variety of fields. We are always seeking talented individuals across departments to join our dynamic team.

The Benefits of Working at UOVO

We are a hard-working team, but we also understand that everyone has a life outside work. At UOVO we promote a healthy work/life balance by respecting personal boundaries and offering a comprehensive benefits package that includes medical coverage, generous paid leave, 401(k)  with competitive matching, paid parental leave, an ongoing employee recognition program, and other benefits.

Time off

At UOVO we understand the importance of a balance between work and time to recharge, travel or simply take a break. UOVO offers paid time off, which increases with tenure and recognizes all federal holidays. In addition, all employees are granted one floating holiday day per year.

Plan for your future

UOVO knows the importance of saving for retirement and wants to ensure all team members meet their retirement financial goals. To this effect, UOVO offers a 401(k) match equal to 100% of employee contributions into the plan up to the first 3% of eligible pay. 

Healthcare

UOVO offers excellent national healthcare, including 100% employer-paid medical, dental, and optical options.

Competitive referral program

UOVO employees are encouraged to refer qualified and like-minded individuals to join our team. In the event of a successful referral leading to recruitment, UOVO will offer a referral award.

Growth opportunities

The world of UOVO is growing. In addition to being the national art and fashion storage brand of choice, we aim to be the employer of choice. We are committed to creating meaningful opportunities for our people; making sure all open positions within the organization are clearly communicated, easily accessible, and encouraging of internal applicants.

A team that celebrates its people

At UOVO, we like to show our appreciation for our team’s hard work and dedication, with employee recognition programs that allow team members to nominate their colleagues for awards and prestigious prizes. We also seek to foster long-term career growth and celebrate meaningful milestones, offering a special reward at every five year anniversary known as the UOVO Milestone Award.
