Life at fuuse...

We pride ourselves on our vibrant culture, collaborative atmosphere, and the strong sense of community that defines our workplace. But don't just take our word for it, take a look what some of the Fuuse team have to say...

Meet Tom our Partnerships Manager

Working at Fuuse presents a multitude of challenges as we strive to fulfill a broad spectrum of tasks for our clients and further the growth of the company. It's rewarding to acknowledge our positive impact on business growth, our client satisfaction, and our contribution to the planet's journey towards Net Zero.One of the most significant benefits of working at Fuuse is the genuine flexibility it offers. If you're more productive at home, you're granted the freedom to work there. Personally, I enjoy being in the office to interact with colleagues who are also friends. 

Meet Rob our L&D Manager

I have loved my first two years working at Fuuse. When I started, I was told the story of the business and the difference it wants to make, and I instantly felt a part of a work family with a passion and drive to offer a service that's truly needed in the transport sector.

I was trusted from the beginning to bring my knowledge and expertise to improve and develop the people who work here now, and also making it a desirable place for others, as we grow and expand.

 

Meet Nataliia our UX Lead

I joined Fuuse at the end of 2023 having never worked in the EV world before. My colleagues are so thoughtful and patiently helped me to get up to speed. We are ambitious and hardworking, and together we will build a greener future.

The leadership team is approachable and encourages us to have a voice to empower innovation. I am really proud that we are evolving and building better products than ever.
