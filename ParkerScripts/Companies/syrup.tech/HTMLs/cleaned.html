AI with a Purpose

Did you know that the textile industry contributes approximately 10% of global greenhouse emissions? And unfortunately much of what is produced is wasted. In fact, it is estimated that for every 3 or 4 articles a brand wants to sell at full price, they have to make 10. The excess is waste — often sold at a deep discount and sometimes even discarded.

Syrup has set out to make commerce less wasteful by enabling merchandise planners to buy the right amount of inventory and ensure that it gets to the right channels and stores to be sold. Our AI uses advanced machine learning models to accurately predict demand, enable more precise pre-season merchandise buying, and optimize inventory in-season. The result is improved revenue for our customers — all while making the apparel industry more sustainable.
