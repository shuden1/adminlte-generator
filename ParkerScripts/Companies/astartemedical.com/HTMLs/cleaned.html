Careers

Astarte Medical is the only precision medicine company using software and predictive analytics to improve outcomes during the first 1,000 days of life. With an initial focus on preterm infants, Astarte Medical supports feeding protocols, practice and decision-making in the neonatal ICU with a suite of digital tools and diagnostics designed to standardize feeding, optimize nutrition and quantify gut health. We have compiled a proprietary dataset combining feeding practices, longitudinal development and the population of the gut microbiome. Utilizing machine learning on this unique dataset will allow us to develop a digital diagnostic tool, the first to quantify a vital sign for the current health of the gut and a predictive tool for the gut heath state in the near-term future.

If you’re interested in connecting and learning about potential opportunities, you can reach out to us at careers@astartemedical.com.

 

Technical Business Analyst

Company DescriptionPlease only apply to this position if you have the ability to work full-time, are in theGreater Philadelphia area, are a US Citizen, have your Green Card or Long Term Visa.

Astarte Medical is a clinical intelligence company using software and predictive analytics to improve outcomes for children with medical complexity. Astarte Medical supports standardized care protocols, care planning and decision-making in acute, complex care settings with a suite of digital tools and diagnostics designed to optimize care and outcomes for vulnerable patientpopulations when there is great opportunity to impact long-term health. Astarte Medical’s first tool, NutritionIQ®, is an EMR-integrated, clinical decision support tool addressing critical, unmet needs in the neonatal ICU (NICU) like malnutrition, suboptimal growth, and racial disparities in care. With a national focus on health equity, studies continue to reveal disparate health conditions in women and babies of color. We are expanding our product portfolio to include NutritionIQ_CHD, real-time clinical decision support solution that enables Cardiovascular ICU clinical care teams to standardize pre and post op enteral nutrition guidelines and care plans for children undergoing surgery for congenital heart defects. To learn more, please visit: www.astartemedical.com.

  • Preterm infants face significant challenges, which include: (1) 50% Growth Failure, (2)Greater Risk of Allergy, Asthma and Obesity and (3) >40% Neurodevelopmental Delaysor Disabilities.
  • The NutritionIQ platform utilizes a comprehensive and proprietary set of high-fidelity clinical data that integrates feeding protocols and clinical information to provide real-time decision support for clinical teams.
  • The NutritionIQ suite of products provides actionable information in real time, enablinghospitals and clinical teams to standardize care protocols, and provide more equitablecare for all infants.
  • NutritionIQ is the only enteral feeding protocol audit and monitoring solution thatmeasures adherence to a hospital’s protocol, either as a historical snapshot or ongoing,and correlates it to preterm infant outcomes.
