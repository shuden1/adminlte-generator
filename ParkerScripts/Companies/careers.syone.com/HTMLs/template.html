OUR CULTURE

WHY JOIN US?

Syone’s Culture strongly focuses on people's development, inspiring everyone to be their best. We’re fast-paced and obsessed with doing things the best way possible, on a respect and transparency base that starts with our people and extends to customers, partners, and everyone around us.

Sure we have challenges, sure we are demanding, and sure there are many potential improvement points for our company, but we can assure you that we work on them every day and that you'll be invited to be part of the solution, learn to do better, to be more resilient, stronger than before, and happier, of course.

RECRUITMENT PROCESS

THE 4 STAGES

1 - Application Review  We will review your application and get back to you if you’re a match with what we are looking for.

2 - HR Interview  We will analyze your professional experience and your motivations for a new professional project. In this interview, you will get to know Syone and our job offers that best fit your profile.

3 - Tech Interview  We will put you in contact with our tech experts where your capabilities will be tested in practice.

4 - Our Offer  We will present you our value proposition for you to become a Syoner.

LEARN MORE

Syone brings innovation to the global stage by partnering with top-tier companies to execute mission-critical projects and accelerate Digital Transformation. Our expertise helps organizations enhance their agility and stay ahead of the curve.

We are proud to be a recognized Competence Center for Open Source technologies, partnering with industry leaders like Red Hat, Elastic, Camunda, Couchbase, SUSE, Gitlab, Embarcadero, Syntevo, Zabbix, and Microsoft. Our expertise in these technologies sets us apart and allows us to deliver exceptional results for our clients, helping them stay ahead of the curve in their digital transformation journey.

Our experience spans across borders and industries, having collaborated with over 500 clients in more than 15 countries.
