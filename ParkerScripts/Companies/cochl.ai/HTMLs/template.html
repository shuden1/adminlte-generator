Who we are looking for

    • Self-motivated
    • We’re looking for self-motivated professionals who can define the problem rather than passively do the given tasks.
    • Creative
    • We make new services with new technologies to make a positive impact in the world, so creative minds are important.
    • High standards
    • Lowering the bar to complete the task quickly isn’t what we do. We keep the highest standards from invisible and fundamental things.
