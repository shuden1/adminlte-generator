About Us

At driveblocks, a start-up based in the “Werksviertel” in Munich, we develop the Mapless Autonomy Platform for automated and autonomous driving in the commercial vehicle sector.

Our technology is based on the latest transformer network architectures and  explainable sensor fusion algorithms to create a digital representation of the environment for next gen AD systems. In contrast to existing technologies based on so-called high-definition maps, this eliminates the need for costly pre-mapping and constant updating of these maps in the cloud.

We are a growing company with a strong commitment to building a best-in-class product based on our in-house technology. Our team combines a strong technical foundation with a clear focus on delivering long-term value to our customers. We strive to incrementally improve our product in a fast-paced environment. In addition, we believe that a culture of learning and personal growth is the foundation of an innovative deep-tech company.

Your Background

  • Degree in computer science, informatics, engineering or related (Bachelor, Master, or PhD).

  • Strong technical foundation and experience in at least one of the following fields: On vehicle code development, embedded deployment, implementing safety related functions in automotive dev. processes, deep learning, computer vision, probabilistic sensor-fusion

  • Experience in Robotics, driver assistance systems and / or autonomous driving.

  • Experience with modern C++, Python and embedded software engineering for real-time applications.

  • Experience with modern software engineering tools, including version control, continuous integration and testing as well as cloud-based development processes.

  • Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment with complex technical challenges and requirements.

  • Willingness to deep-dive into new challenges and make our product a true next-generation software platform for automotive applications.

  • Strong team-player and the ability to communicate complex technology in a clear and straight-forward way to match customer and project expectations.
