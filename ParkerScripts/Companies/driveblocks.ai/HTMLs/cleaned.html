About Us

driveblocks, a start-up based in Munich|Werksviertel, develops the Mapless Autonomy Platform for automated and autonomous driving in the commercial vehicle sector. The core technology is a combination of transformer neural networks and geometrically interpretable sensor-fusion to create a next-generation perception software suite. In contrast to existing technologies based on so-called high-definition maps, this eliminates the need for costly pre-mapping and constant updating of these maps in the cloud.

We are a growing company with a strong commitment to building a best-in-class product based on our in-house technology. Our team combines a strong technical foundation with a clear focus on delivering long-term value to our customers. We strive to incrementally improve our product in a fast-paced environment. In addition, we believe that a culture of learning and personal growth is the foundation of an innovative deep-tech company.

Your Role

  • Responsible for the full development cycle of one or more software components within the Mapless Autonomy Platform.

  • Identify software requirements in close contact with customers and technical experts and design appropriate algorithms and solutions.

  • Implement high-quality embedded software ready for real-time deployment on real vehicles.

  • Ensure the fulfilment of performance, safety and quality requirements with software and simulation testing as well as deployment under real-world conditions on our development vehicle.

  • Provide our customers with hands-on support for deployment of our software on their vehicles – including on-site testing and application engineering.

Your Background

  • Degree in computer science, informatics, engineering or related (Bachelor, Master, or PhD).

  • Strong technical foundation and yearlong experience in at least one of the following fields: Deep learning, data-driven algorithms, probabilistic sensor-fusion, safety requirements and engineering.

  • Experience in driver assistance systems and / or autonomous driving.

  • Experience with modern C++ and embedded software engineering for real-time applications.

  • Experience with modern software engineering tools, including version control, continuous integration and testing as well as cloud-based development processes.

  • Apply and extend your technical expertise - Work on the forefront of technology

  • Open work environment - Challenge yourself and define your own focus

  • Ownership of your work – Take responsibility for the full value chain

  • Company shares via virtual employee stock option program

  • Flexible working hours and remote work options (up to 50% of your time)
