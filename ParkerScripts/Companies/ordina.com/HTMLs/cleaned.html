Social Responsibility

Diversity & social responsibility

We strive for a diverse workplace, as we believe diversity accelerates growth. Diversity when it comes to gender ratio, cultural background, people who experience barriers to the labor market and LGBTQ+ colleagues. One of our main goals is that by 2030 at least 30% of our employees are women (up from 20% today).

At Ordina, we have a number of active communities, working to promote a diverse and inclusive organization. We are working with them to increase awareness, mutual understanding and acceptance, with the goal of bringing diversity and inclusion into our organization's DNA.

We want to give everyone equal opportunities; in the labor market and in terms of access to digital resources and knowledge. We mainly focus on local initiatives and get our people involved in making a difference here too.
