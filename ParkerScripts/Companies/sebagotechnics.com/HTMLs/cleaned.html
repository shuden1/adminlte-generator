Careers

Sebago Technics is one of Maine’s leading land development consulting firms. With an emphasis on quality, innovation and results, we are looking for motivated professionals to join our employee-ownership team.

This is a great opportunity to work on a diverse array of projects and be an integral part of our employee-owned company. We offer an excellent work environment, competitive salaries, and great benefits. We work hard at providing opportunities for professional and personal growth while working for our collective success. Sebago Technics is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action employer.

We are hiring for the following positions:

*If you are interested in becoming an employee-owner at Sebago Technics, but don’t see an applicable position listed below, please fill out the form on our Contact Us page, and we will be in touch!

Project Managers & Senior Project Manager

We’re hiring Project Managers & Senior Project Managers in any of the following disciplines: Civil, Traffic, or Transportation Engineering.

We can’t wait to see what you can bring to the table!

Project Manager for our Traffic and Transportation Division

We are seeking Project Manager for our Traffic and Transportation division. Picture yourself around a table with other engineers, surveyors, landscape architects, and CAD designers who enjoy working together to design high-level projects within a dynamic and continuous conversation.  At Sebago, no one works in a silo; everything we do is shaping, and we do it together with great intentionality.

Title Researcher & Administrative Support

Sebago Technics is seeking a Title/Deed Researcher to join the Survey and Geomatics team and help maintain our standard of excellence. The researcher will have an immediate and lasting impact on our operations, guiding us toward positive outcomes with comprehensive deed research, document creation, and database maintenance. The ideal candidate will have experience in a Land Surveying firm.
