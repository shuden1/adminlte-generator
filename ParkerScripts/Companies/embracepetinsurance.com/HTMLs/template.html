Our claims team is the cat's meow

Claims adjuster

Attention Registered Veterinary Technicians: If helping pets is your passion but the clinic setting isn’t for you, we have just what you’re looking for. Our claims team processes thousands of claims per week and helps our policyholders get their pets the best care.

Claims admin

Processing claims couldn’t happen without our beloved Claims Admins. This position ensures that claims are set up correctly, that we have what we need to process them, and does the follow-up to get what we need in a timely manner.
