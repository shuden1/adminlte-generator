Our claims team is the cat's meow

Claims adjuster

Attention Registered Veterinary Technicians: If helping pets is your passion but the clinic setting isnt for you, we have just what youre looking for. Our claims team processes thousands of claims per week and helps our policyholders get their pets the best care.

Claims admin

Processing claims couldnt happen without our beloved Claims Admins. This position ensures that claims are set up correctly, that we have what we need to process them, and does the follow-up to get what we need in a timely manner.
