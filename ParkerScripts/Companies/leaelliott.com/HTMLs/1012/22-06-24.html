Careers

Life at Lea+Elliott is exciting! Our experienced, professional staff of industry veterans apply their skills to designing and procuring the best possible transportation systems for clients worldwide.

Lea+Elliott’s major resource is a highly professional staff of experienced and recognized specialists, who are constantly and passionately striving to advance and improve transportation technology and services, while adhering to proven safety principles. If you are a team-focused professional, who strives for creative excellence and will commit to providing outstanding customer service, we want to hear from you. Email your resume to jobopenings@leaelliott.com

 

Job Opportunities

Transit Systems Project Manager

Lea+Elliott is seeking a qualified Transit Systems Project Manager to manage an Automated People Mover (APM) project in Singapore. The Transit Systems Project Manager will be responsible for overseeing transit systems engineering and planning, including preliminary design, detailed design review, report preparation, system operation and alternatives analysis, procurement specification document preparation, and construction and testing oversight for a range of transportation systems. Responsibilities will also include interfacing with and supporting other project managers to achieve overall project goals.

Qualifications

  • B.S. in Engineering (Civil, Mechanical, or Electrical preferred)
  • Registered Professional Engineering (PE) license preferred
  • Minimum 15 years of experience in the transit industry or related field
  • Experience working in a consulting environment a plus
  • Experience with alternatives analysis, systems/facility design interfaces and technical specification or contract writing is preferred.  Specialized experience with transit vehicles, controls, power distribution, and/or communications systems is a plus
  • Willingness to learn and engage in multiple disciplines, work as part of a team, effectively delegate, balance multiple assignments as necessary

If you are interested in a long-term, growth-oriented opportunity, we are interested in hearing from you!

Please respond to jobopenings@leaelliott.com with cover letter, resume and references.

Lea+Elliott is a transportation consulting firm that works with owners on the planning, design, procurement, implementation and operation and maintenance of rail, bus and automated transit systems.
