IMPORTANT NOTICE!

We have been recently reported numerous fraud incidents, in particular in India, using our name and brand attributes. It was identified that swindlers under our brand are running hiring campaigns in India, Bangalore, and then asking training fees from misled people. In the light of this, we want to outline a few important facts: ⚠️ WE HAVE NO OPERATIONS IN INDIA, including representatives or employees. ⚠️ WE DON’T HAVE any open positions in India, both on-site and remote, and don’t run any hiring campaigns. ⚠️ WE NEVER CHARGE any fees for training or other onboarding activities. Should you be contacted by anyone claiming to work for Artelogic from an email domain other than artelogic.net, as well as ask to pay any kind of fee for whatever reason, you’re likely being affected. Please ignore these attempts as fraudulent and warn anyone you know who may also have been targeted.

6 Reason To Join Artelogic

  • We ensure your professional growth
    We are a squad of artists who constantly upgrade their professional level. Artelogic assures a variety of opportunities for this: compensation for courses and training, mentorship program (in role both mentee and mentor), and support in professional certification. We implemented the career trek for every team member that allows you to plan your career in a very transparent manner
  • We respect your identity and recognize your achievements
    We accept your personality as is. Neither age, gender, beliefs, or racial affiliation - only your expertise and team's spirit matter. Working in Artelogic means your achievements will be recognized and your failures will be accepted with tolerance as an opportunity for growth.
  • We go beyond the wireframes and stereotypes
    We provide solutions, not lines of code. Therefore we inspire creative thinking and are open to all innovative ideas. If you look for the opportunity to use all of the benefits of Agile methodology and are oriented to results rather then procedures, Artelogic is the right workplace. We respect your creativity and courage.
  • We provide the comfortable environment and open-minded management
    The comfortable working environment is paired with transparent and easy-to-follow company policies. Our office is designed to be a safe, healthy, and smart working space. We respect the work/life balance and provide extra benefits like health insurance, parental days-off, mental and legal support. We are a company with a strong feedback culture and believe that open and transparent communication plays a crucial role in our success.
  • We trust you
    We are a team of mature professionals who are empowered to make decisions on their own. We cultivate the leadership approach in management and believe that leaders need inspiration and trust rather than control.
  • Need more knowledge?
    Artelogic University allows you to develop your engineering carrier in a very flexible manner. We provide courses in technology stacks like Node.js, PHP, .NET, for free for everyone who has a minimal tech background and needs to upgrade his/her skills. The savvy mentors and real-case studies allow you to get enough knowledge for getting an offer from any of the software companies.
