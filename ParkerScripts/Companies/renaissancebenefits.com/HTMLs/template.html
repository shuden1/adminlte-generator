Careers

Renaissance works hard every day to provide an ancillary benefits experience that stands out. That means having passionate employees to match. Renaissance seeks qualified candidates with an interest in joining an insurance carrier that isn’t afraid to challenge the status quo in how ancillary benefits are provided. We want “big thinkers” and employees who feel empowered to bring ideas forward that are in the best interests of our customers.

Candidates will find competitive salaries and comprehensive benefits packages alongside training and educational opportunities for career advancement in a variety of career paths.
