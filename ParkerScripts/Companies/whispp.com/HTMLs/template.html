Speech Technology AI Developer & Researcher

Junior/Medior level – Full time – Leiden

Working at Whispp means being part of a highly innovative and impact driven tech startup with a creative international team. We enjoy the collaborative atmosphere and nice lunches at our office in the startup incubator www.plnt.nl in the city center of Leiden.

Responsibilities

Research and development of state of the art speech analysis, conversion and synthesis models

Requirements

  • Master’s in AI, Data Science or Computer Science. PhD is a plus
  • Background in Deep Learning, automatic speech recognition and audio synthesis
  • Proficiency in Python and experience with PyTorch
  • Experience in building the pipelines for AI development

Nice to have

  • Experience with Digital Signal Processing (DSP)
  • Experience with low latency models
  • Experience with C++

Please send your application and resume to Joris Castermans, founder and CEO of Whispp joris@whispp.com or 06-51234840.
