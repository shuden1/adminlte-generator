Careers

bidadoo is growing and we are looking for professionals who are passionate about heavy equipment and/or have auction experience.

We'd love to hear from you!

bidadoo is a global online remarketing company with Auction Service Centers across North America and bidders and buyers from around the world. For the past 20 years, bidadoo has become the most trusted company buyers and sellers turn to for online auction services of used capital equipment on the world's largest auction marketplace, eBay. We work with some of the largest equipment owners in the world including Sunstate Equipment, Herc Rentals, H&E Equipment, JCB, Genie/Terex, JLG, Komatsu, John Deere dealers, The Boeing Company, City of Seattle, and many municipalities and government agencies. With eBay's million's of registered users globally, bidadoo provides equipment sellers access to the world’s largest online bidder and buyer base through our long term partnership with eBay.

bidadoo is seeking energetic, self-driven, customer focused, experienced individuals to fill positions throughout the United States.
