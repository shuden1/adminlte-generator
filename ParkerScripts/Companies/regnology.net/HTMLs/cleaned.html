Shape the future of RegTech

Regnology is a leading international firm with an exclusive focus on regulatory reporting; uniquely positioned to bring greater data quality, efficiency and cost savings to financial institutions, regulators, and tax authorities. Our objective? To bring safety and stability to the financial markets.

To accomplish this, we are looking for people who want to be part of developing future-oriented solutions in a flexible environment while enjoying creative freedom, continuous development, and a wide range of opportunities to accelerate their careers.

Why Regnology?

People are our strongest asset, and we recognize the benefits of working in diverse teams across genders, generations, abilities, skill-sets, and cultural backgrounds. Our way of working is about ensuring the learning and development of our workforce in an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome and safe. We believe in continual improvement through learning and innovation by investing in our people, listening to our customers, and providing space for creativity and growth.

We promote:

  • Work-life balance

    With remote working and flexible hours so that you can find your own balance between your career and personal life.

  • Career growth and mobility

    With the opportunity to take on further responsibilities, participate in international projects, and move to new roles within Regnology.

  • Learning and development

    With access to courses, training, and certifications during which you can explore new technical and functional areas as well as improve existing ones.
