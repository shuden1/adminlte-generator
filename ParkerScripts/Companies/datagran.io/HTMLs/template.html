F/T Chief Artificial Intelligence Officer

Data Gran, INC., seeks a F/T Chief Artificial Intelligence Officer for their New York, New York location.

Responsibilities:‍

Responsible for identifying opportunities to improve the product and customer experience across machine learning based solutions.
Building and designing the architecture of the products.
Managing a team of developers and data scientists to solve the customer problems and building the product providing strategic and tactical insights based on the technological developments.

Requirements:

A U.S. Bachelor’s Degree or a Foreign Equivalent Degree in Computer Engineering.
5+ years of progressive, post-baccalaureate experience in Management and Software Development required.
