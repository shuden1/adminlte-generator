Welcome to Healthcare2U Careers

At Healthcare2U, we are dedicated to transforming the way healthcare is delivered. We believe that access to quality healthcare should be convenient, affordable, and focused on the individual. If you share our passion for improving healthcare and want to make a positive impact, you’ve come to the right place. Join us in our mission to create a healthier and happier world.

Who We Are

Healthcare2U is a leader in delivering nationwide Direct Primary Care to manage the 13 most prevalent chronic conditions for employees at organizations of all sizes and structures. We offer membership-based, no-claims healthcare™ alternatives for businesses and their workforce. We are breaking down barriers to accessing board-certified family care physicians and internists through our Private Physician Network (PPN).

Our Mission

We ensure that organizations of all sizes and structures have unlimited, nationwide access to affordable and consistent primary and urgent care provided by Healthcare2U Physician Services; and to promote healthy living by detecting, treating, and managing acute and chronic conditions before the onset of serious illnesses.

Why Healthcare2U?

1. Meaningful Work:

At Healthcare2U, your work directly impacts people’s lives. You’ll be part of a team dedicated to improving the health of individuals, enhancing their quality of life and making healthcare accessible to all.

2. Innovation:

We are at the forefront of revolutionizing healthcare delivery. Join us to be a part of a dynamic and innovative environment where you can make a real difference.

3. Career Growth:

We believe in investing in our people. We offer opportunities for professional development and growth within the company, giving you the chance to build a fulfilling career.

4. Team Collaboration:

Our diverse and talented team collaborates to achieve our mission. You’ll have the opportunity to work alongside professionals who share your passion for healthcare.

5. Employee-led activities:

To promote opportunities for collaboration among employees, an employee-led Joy Division organizes events such as group lunches, volunteer opportunities, and holiday parties.

Our Culture

Our company culture is built on a foundation of integrity, compassion, innovation and dedication. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and supportive environment where every team member is valued and encouraged to thrive. When you join Healthcare2U, you become part of a family that shares a common vision of improving healthcare.

Benefits

We offer a competitive compensation package, comprehensive healthcare benefits, retirement plans and various perks and discounts. Your health, happiness, and well-being are important to us and we strive to provide a supportive and fulfilling work experience.

How to Apply

Please send your resume and a cover letter to Hiring@healthc2u.com. Be sure to include the position title you are applying for in the subject line and explain why you would be a great fit for the role. We look forward to hearing from you.

Join Our Team

If you are looking to advance your career in a mission-driven organization that values innovation and teamwork, Healthcare2U is the place for you. Together, we can make a difference in the world of healthcare and create a healthier future for all.

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Equal Opportunity

The employees at Healthcare2U make a difference. Here, we believe that celebrating the unique backgrounds, perspectives and abilities of every employee makes an impact not only for our company internally but also for our clients, customers and community. We are an equal opportunity employer and believe that the inclusive atmosphere we build together will enable every person to grow, contribute and thrive. We will ensure that individuals with disabilities are provided reasonable accommodation to participate in the interview process, to perform essential job functions and to receive other benefits and privileges of employment. Healthcare2U will comply with all laws and regulations.

Join us in our journey to transform healthcare. Apply today to start your exciting career with Healthcare2U!
