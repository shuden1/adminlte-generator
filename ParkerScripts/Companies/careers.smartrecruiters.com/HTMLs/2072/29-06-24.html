Phoenix American

Since its beginning, Phoenix American has focused on serving the needs of the financial services industry. Gus Constantin, President and Chief Executive Officer, founded the company as the sponsor of equipment leasing investment funds in 1972. Phoenix Leasing funds were highly successful for over 20 years and from that experience, several related businesses emerged:  Phoenix American Financial Services, Phoenix American SalesFocus Solutions, Phoenix Leasing Portfolio Services and Phoenix Cable.  Today Phoenix American companies provide a broad range of business and technology services including: business software applications, application hosting, investment fund administration, fund accounting, transfer agent services, managing agent services, back-office outsourcing, tax and financial services and cable television systems.

Phoenix American Financial Services

Phoenix American Financial Services provides full-service fund administration, fund accounting, transfer agent and back-office outsourcing functions for alternative investment funds. The company is the developer of the STAR-XMS transfer agent system, the most advanced fund administration system available for alternative investments. Client firms include alternative investment funds of every structure, scale and asset class:  private equity, venture capital, non-traded REITS, BDCs, commodity pools and private real estate funds.  Additional services include:  AML/KYC, OFAC/FinCen compliance checks, printing and fulfillment services, investment document review and compliance, investor accreditation services for both individuals and institutions and telephone call center support.  Phoenix American also prepares and delivers K-1 and 1099 tax forms for entities with activities in all 50 states.

 

Phoenix American Financial Services - Aviation ABS

Phoenix American is the industry leader in managing agent services for asset-backed securitizations (ABS) in the commercial aviation leasing industry.  For over thirty years, the Phoenix American family of companies has created lease and loan portfolios and has securitized six of its own structured transactions. Directly involved in the aviation ABS market since 1993, Phoenix American manages securitized aviation assets representing over 1400 aircraft and engines appraised at over $36 billion.

Our long history of success attests to Phoenix American's peerless industry knowledge, skilled and seasoned professional staff and solid relationships with the major players in the securitization industry.

  • Aircraft Lessors
  • Investment Banks
  • Investors
  • Trustees
  • Rating Agencies
  • Auditing and Accounting Firms
  • Law Firms
  • Appraisers

Phoenix American SalesFocus Solutions

SalesFocus Solutions provides asset management firms with state-of-the-art data visualization and sales analytics to facilitate intelligent decision making through its Marketing And Reporting Sales (MARS) product suite. MARS transforms transfer agent and financial intermediary data into actionable market intelligence and allows firms to perform sophisticated business analysis, and trending.  MARS provides firms with a competitive advantage with advanced analytics and data visualization and the ability to drill-down into graphical views so sales teams can find new actionable business opportunities.  SalesFocus Solutions' clients include banks, asset management firms, insurance companies and real estate investment trusts.  MARS has been utilized by some of the world's largest financial services firms.

 

Phoenix Leasing Portfolio Services

Phoenix Leasing Portfolio Services, and its Phoenix Leasing Bank Services division provide lease and loan portfolio management services, accounting, tax, sales and marketing services to banks and leasing companies.  

Phoenix Leasing Portfolio Services and related Phoenix American entities have sponsored 31 limited partnerships, including 28 public limited partnerships. These partnerships raised equity of $940 million from over 100,000 individual investors, investing over $2 billion in equipment and collateralized leases and loans throughout the United States.   

Phoenix Cable

Established in 1986, Phoenix Cable and its affiliate PCI One own, operate, buy and sell cable television systems throughout the eastern United States. They currently own and operate the franchise for the cable television system serving the United States Naval Station at Guantanamo Bay, Cuba.
