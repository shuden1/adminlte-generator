The Kensington Tours Advantage

Kensington Tours offers personalized, private-guided journeys in more than 100 countries worldwide. By combining passionate, experienced talent with industry-leading technology, we have become the premier provider of unforgettable, one-of-a-kind travel experiences. To create unforgettable and truly unique travel experiences we rely on four essential principles:

•  Personalized journeys designed uniquely for our clients
•  Expert planning by people who care and have deep in-country knowledge
•  Private local guides to deliver personal attention and authentic experiences
•  Unbeatable 24/7 support
