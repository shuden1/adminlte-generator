At-Bay

We are a fast-growing cyber and specialty insurance startup based out of Mountain View, CA. We’re an insurance company built by cybersecurity experts, leveraging our security expertise to better understand digital risks and thus create better insurance products and services. We help clients by providing both risk transfer (the insurance policy), guidance on their security issues, and active risk management (helping them stay safe).

At-Bay has raised over $50M in funding, backed by Acrew Capital, Khosla Ventures, Lightspeed Venture Partners, Munich Re Ventures, and Shlomo Kramer. We are currently 50 employees, split between Mountain View, San Francisco, New York City, Atlanta, and Tel Aviv, with a plan to double our headcount and increase our span across the US by year-end.
