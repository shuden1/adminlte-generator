About Zwift:

Zwift is the fitness company born from gaming, and we’re on a mission to make more people, more active, more often. The Zwift platform delivers an at-home training game that connects cyclists and runners in over 200 countries across the globe! Launched from the sunny shores of Long Beach, California in 2014, we’ve grown to have a distributed workforce across the world.

Some reasons we think you will love it here:

At Zwift, we want to inspire each other, build on each other’s ideas, and celebrate the wins together. We all play a role in the success of Zwift, and it takes Watopians (that’s what we call our employees!) across the globe to make it all happen. Love cycling? Awesome. Haven’t ridden a bike since you were 8? Cool. Never learned how to ride a bike? We’ve got the best teachers if you want to learn. If not, no sweat. We celebrate the value of different perspectives. That’s how we grow and become an inclusive game, company, and community. 

We are a passionate, energized, and innovative team. We like bikes. We like running. We admire a healthy work/life balance too - offering “Wednesday Wellness” to all Watopians to prioritize wellness for your body and mind.

Our Values

Our Values are at the core of what we do, and we aspire to bring them to life in the ways we work, play, and connect. Our Company Values are:

  • Zwift For Everyone
    • We aspire for Zwift to be a safe and welcoming place for everyone. We seek to understand different perspectives, demonstrate empathy, and value diverse skill sets.
  • Focus on Progress
    • We pursue opportunity and take calculated risks. We align on outcomes, iterate to improve, and tolerate imperfection in favor of progress.
  • Always Level Up
    • We constantly look for growth by developing our skills, capabilities, and execution. We remain curious, learn quickly, and embrace change.
  • Elevate All Teammates
    • We are one team. We grow, celebrate, and achieve more by working together, recognizing all contributions, supporting team decisions, and teaching others.
  • Make It Fun
    • Mixing work and a sense of play is our magic recipe. We cultivate connection and trust while finding the fun in all we do.
  • Activate Our Mission
    • We are passionate about achieving our vision together. We aspire to be a community prioritizing fun experiences, innovation, and well-being.

The Future of Work at Zwift

We have thought long and hard about the Future of Work and what that will mean for our Watopians. COVID-19 provided us the opportunity to look outside of our normal markets for talent, and that is something that we will continue to do as we embrace this next chapter of work. We are excited to welcome people to our teams from all over the world, and we're thrilled with the diversity of perspectives that brings.

Zwift is excited to offer a default 2+3 hybrid working model (two days in office and three days at home each week) for those that are near one of our offices. We consider each role individually to determine what the role, work, and team require – if there’s a specific need for you to be in the office more than the standard 2+3, it’ll be included in the job posting. We want to hire the best people, regardless of location – if you aren’t close to an office, we encourage you to still apply. 

We care about you as our candidate, so let your Talent Acquisition Partner know if there’s anything we can do to support a smooth interview process. We are always looking for ways to level up our candidate experience – Always Level Up in action right there!  

If these Values and Future of Work principles align with your own behaviors and thoughts, check out our current opportunities and come join the mission to make more people, more active, more often.
