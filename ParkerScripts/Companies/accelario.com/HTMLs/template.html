Administrative Manager

Accelario Software Inc, an early-stage startup and the provider of the Accelario Continuous DataOps platform – the VMWare of Databases, seeks an Administrative Manager. You will be managing all administrative aspects of the company, reporting to the company CEO.

Job description:

  • All employee’s welfare, wellbeing, and constant feedback to management
  • Employees development programs follow up (done with their manager)
  • Company offices manager (Israel mainly, other locations globally)
  • Company website admin
  • Company marketing and sales events coordinator
  • Travel facilitator and IT equipment manager / approver, team expenses reimbursement, team vacation coordinator.
  • Management and employees’ annual meetings.

 

Requirements:

  • The successful candidate will have the suitable background, relevant experience, high skills of relationship and leadership capabilities. Fluent English and a relevant track record are essential.
  • Compensation includes participation in the company employees stock options program, above market average salary and hybrid working facilities (mix of working from home and office), in 60% capacity (3 days a week)

Senior Architect

Responsibilities

  • Own and evolve the technology architecture.
  • Work closely with senior management (VP R&D, CTO, and Director
    of Product) on the product roadmap and the technology
    architecture requiredto implement the vision.
  • Drive the definition and implementation of an advanced
    architecture of the technology and products to address growing product
    challenges, such as scalability, availability, redundancy, etc.
  • Research and implement new technological changes to improve the
    potential and competitive edge of the platform and products.
  • Develop some key complex components of the platform.

 

Requirements:

  • 7+ years software development experience, at least 4 of which
    for enterprise infrastructure products (databases, enterprise
    data management, storage, resource virtualization, etc.).
  • 3+ years in a technology architect role for enterprise
    infrastructure products. Demonstrable impact on both the
    technology environment architecture and the software architecture is a must.
  • 3+ years in a dynamic and fast-growing environment (e.g.
    startup or a growing innovative group in a bigger company).
  • Solid experience with enterprise infrastructure technologies – a must.
  • Demonstrable experience designing advanced architecture for
    enterprise infrastructure products.
  • Hands-on experience with Java.
  • Experience/good understanding of CI/CD processes and test data
    management methodologies.
  • Independent and self-starter.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Fluent in English.
  • BSc in Computer Science/Software Engineering (or equivalent).
