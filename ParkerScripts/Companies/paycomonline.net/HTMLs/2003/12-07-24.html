Florida Peninsula Insurance Company is a premier homeowners insurance carrier in Florida, with Corporate Headquarters based in Boca Raton, Florida. We currently have more than 250,000 policies in-force with more than $1 Billion of in-force premium. The FPI Family of Companies includes Florida Peninsula Insurance, Edison Insurance, Florida Peninsula Claim Services, and The Windward Insurance Agency. One of our most important commitments is managing our financial stability in spite of the challenges of the Florida homeowners market. 

At FPI, our team members are the heart of our company and we actively appreciate, recognize, and reward them for their efforts. We offer competitive salaries, an outstanding benefits package, a relaxed work environment, and career development. Many positions offer flexible work arrangements including remote, hybrid, and onsite, depending on the role. This is a casual, family-oriented work environment with a long-term focus on shared success. 

Our Mission is to be the insurer Floridians choose to protect their homes by providing an exceptional customer experience.  We are committed to; A customer-focused and collaborative culture, Continuous innovation, Engaged team members with diverse perspectives, Long-term financial stability, Service to our communities, and Integrity and transparency in all we do. 

We are growing, innovating, and creating a great place to work. 

Florida Peninsula Insurance Company is an equal opportunity employer. All applicants will be considered for employment without attention to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, veteran, or disability status.
