Want to Be a
Part of Our Team?

Here’s everything you need to know about what it’s like to work with us.

Our mission to actively connect people to their next great opportunity is powered by industry-leading technology that’s always getting smarter. We improve the job seeker experience for millions of people every month and help businesses of all sizes find and hire the right people quickly.

We’re growing rapidly in pursuit of this mission. And whether you want to work with us remotely or in one of our offices, we’re always looking for talented people to join our team.

What Are
Our Core Values?

We’re a diverse group who each bring our talents
to the table while collaborating on our mission.

We are a bold group of doers and problem solvers. We’re always curious to find better, smarter solutions. And we never settle for subpar. We use our best judgment to keep pushing forward.

We’re confident in our skills and highly knowledgeable in our specialized areas. But we’re also considerate to each other. Although we are experts at what we do, we have no egos. We’ve got each other’s backs. We communicate openly and honestly. And we give credit where credit is due.

We love what we do, and we believe in it. It’s what drives us. That’s why we take ownership of our responsibilities and teach others when we can. But we think beyond our individual roles. We think about how, together, we are making a real difference.

What’s Life
Like at ZipRecruiter?

We’re passionate about what we do—which is why we work so well together.

Meaningful work

We help millions of job seekers find roles that match their skills and experience every day. You will literally be changing people’s lives. Helping others is in our DNA.

Enjoy the journey

We celebrate wins and embrace opportunities to learn. We value getting to work together on something we believe in. Come be a part of the team.

Inclusive teamwork

Join a team where diverse perspectives and experiences help us be better every day. Work with smart people who are excited to tackle interesting problems.

We champion diversity and
inclusion in all aspects of our work.

We are committed to nurturing an environment where every person feels welcomed, valued, heard, and respected. Whether you prefer to work from home
or in a hybrid environment, we have options for you.

Remote

Don't live near one of our offices? No problem. Many of our positions can be performed remotely.

Santa Monica, CA

Home to our headquarters, this spacious office boasts a beautiful rooftop workspace and is just a 10-minute walk from the beach.

Phoenix, AZ

A quick drive from downtown Phoenix, our office is conveniently located on Mill Avenue, which features dining, shops, nightlife, entertainment and more.

Palo Alto, CA

Our Bay Area office is just a short walk from Caltrain (our commuter rail) and is nestled in the California Avenue shopping district. Recharge by exploring one of the nearby hiking trails or grab a bite at one of the local cafes or restaurants.

Tel Aviv, Israel

Home to a team of engineers, product managers, and data scientists, this office has some of the best views in Tel Aviv. It’s also located next to the largest enclosed culinary market in Israel—Sarona Market.

London, United Kingdom

Based in the heart of the capital in London Bridge, our office is surrounded by world-famous restaurants and landmarks such as Borough Market and the Shard.
