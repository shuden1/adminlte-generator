Life at Cohley

What makes us special

Cohley builds software that enables brands to connect with influencers, photographers, and videographers to produce authentic and engaging content that propels the brand’s growth.

Cohley is built by innovators and team players who want to change the way brands create content - all the while having fun doing it.

Oh, and what’s with the fish references, you might ask? Cohley is an incorrectly spelled spin-off of Coley, a species of fish. Cohley's legal name is Goldfish Social Inc., and yes, we do in fact have a fish tank in the office.
