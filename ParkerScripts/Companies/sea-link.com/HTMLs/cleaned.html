Freight Forwarder Sales Executive (Air & Ocean)
Sealink International Inc.
At Sealink, we believe there is a better way to do freight. Logistics is often a complicated and confusing process, but it does not have to be. We focus on simplifying shipping for customers by bringing freight & its visibility online. We use technology to offer enhanced visibility to your shipment, making it easy for you to browse rates, make bookings, and pay invoices online.
Sealink International Inc. operates in several locations throughout the United States and employs over 100 dedicated staff members. We exported over 115,000 TEUs in 2020. We were recognized among the top fastest growing companies in the US by Inc. Media FIVE times – 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2021.
Are you an experienced Sales Professional with a strong background in selling various modes of transportation, particularly international air and ocean shipments? Sealink International, a renowned global logistics company, is seeking a talented Freight Forwarder Sales Executive (Air & Ocean) to join our dynamic team. If you are passionate about sales, possess excellent communication skills, and thrive in a fast-paced environment, this is an exceptional opportunity to advance your career.
With our innovative and convenient approach to freight, and our unwavering dedication to our customer’s success, we are excited to welcome new customers to our family and help them grow with us. This is where you come in:
Responsibilities:
  • Identifying and prospecting potential clients in diverse industries
  • Building and nurturing strong relationships with existing clients, understanding their unique requirements, and delivering tailored solutions.
  • Conducting comprehensive market research to identify emerging trends and potential areas for business growth.
  • Developing and executing effective sales strategies to meet revenue targets and increase market share.
  • Preparing and delivering compelling sales presentations, proposals, and negotiations to secure new business opportunities.
  1. Previous experience as a Freight Forwarder Sales Executive (Air & Ocean) in a similar sales role, with a focus on various modes including international air and ocean transportation.
  2. Demonstrated track record of consistently achieving and surpassing sales targets.
  3. Excellent knowledge of the logistics industry, including a deep understanding of various transportation modes and their intricacies.
  4. Strong negotiation and communication skills, enabling you to build and nurture relationships with clients at all levels.
  5. Self-motivation and goal orientation, coupled with the ability to work both independently and collaboratively as part of a team.
  • Great benefits.
  • Flexible schedule.
  • Growth opportunities.
  • In office gym and golf access.
  • Paid time off.
