Career

At Fraser, we know that our people are our greatest asset, which is why we actively search for the best talent to join our team of sales brokers, charter brokers, charter managers, and support staff.Discover our current list of job opportunities below! Fraser is proud to be fully Great Place to Work certified.
 
 
 

TECHNICAL SUPERINTENDENT

Location:  AthensThe Technical Superintendent is the Company’s and the Owner’s representative in the day-to-day operations of the vessel. She/he has to communicate and continuously improve the relationships by providing excellent support services to the Client(s), tailored to the yacht Owner’s expectations.With an in-depth knowledge of yachts, operations, systems, engineering, refits, upkeep, and personnel management, the Technical Superintendent performs concentrated work under pressure of multiple vessels and tight deadlines.
