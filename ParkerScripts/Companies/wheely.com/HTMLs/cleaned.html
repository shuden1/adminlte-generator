Our Vision: LUXURY-AS-A-SERVICE

At Wheely, we’re a luxury brand on the outside, but a technology company on the inside, building the world’s first Luxury-as-a-Service. We believe that time is the ultimate luxury and that modern engineering and design, combined with the industry’s highest standards of service, can unlock an unparalleled experience for our customers.

More than 40% of our team works in product & engineering, and both Wheely founders are technical. We are also unapologetically design centric. It’s not about A/B testing one hundred shades of blue, but crafting the perfect shade. We also take a privacy-first approach and believe that where people travel, and who they travel with, is at their discretion. We have refused government requests to hand over journey data, and are currently developing bespoke technology to put our clients’ movements beyond even our own reach.

Backed by leading global investors, Wheely is poised for the next phase of our journey. Over the next 5-10 years, we plan to offer a full portfolio of luxury services and expand into more international cities, building on our success in London, Paris, and Dubai.

Why join Wheely?

At Wheely, we don’t just offer jobs; we offer careers filled with potential, growth, and impact. Here’s what you can expect when you become part of our team:

Ownership

We believe in empowering our team members with a high degree of ownership and trust. We trust you to bring your best self and your best ideas, and we provide the support and freedom you need to make them happen.

Growth

When you join Wheely, you’re not just filling a role; you’re taking charge of your career path. Personal and professional growth is at the heart of our culture. At Wheely, you’ll find endless opportunities to learn, expand your skill set, and take on new challenges. Whether you are an engineer, designer, or luxury service specialist, we support your career development with mentorship, training, and opportunities to work on various projects across different teams.

Impact

The work you do at Wheely won’t just be confined to the office. We are redefining the luxury industry on a global scale, and the impact of your work will be felt externally, influencing how people perceive and experience luxury. From creating an exceptional in-car experience to developing privacy-focused technology, your contributions will play a vital role in shaping the future of luxury as we know it.

Join us for a Meaningful Career

When you join Wheely, you become part of an ambitious, forward-thinking team that values innovation, growth, and building a successful business for the long term. We look for passionate individuals who are eager to contribute to our mission and vision. If this sounds like you, explore our current openings and take the first step towards a meaningful career at Wheely.

Benefits
