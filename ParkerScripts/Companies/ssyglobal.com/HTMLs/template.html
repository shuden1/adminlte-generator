LNG Shipping Analyst

Location

London

Salary

DOE

Sector

LNG Research

SSY boasts a proud legacy which spans over 144 years. In that time, some of the biggest names in ship broking have worked for the company. Indeed, it's fair to say that our company has helped shape the broking industry that we know today.

Due to our continued growth, we are now looking for an LNG Shipping Analyst to join our LNG Research team in London.

SSY is the world’s largest independent broker. We are objective and autonomous with the scale and coverage to service the most demanding clients and volatile markets across the globe. As a privately owned partnership, we are focused entirely on the needs of our clients and are agile and impartial in the way we do business. SSY operates from 25 offices across the globe and employs over 500 people, covering all the major shipping and commodity locations, and providing a round-the-clock service.

Job Description
We are looking for an LNG Shipping Analyst to join our LNG team, as part of a team of analysts sitting alongside the LNG brokers. The research team works collaboratively with the brokers to provide comprehensive services and insights to a wide range of clients, including Shipowners, LNG producers, national and major oil companies, oil, gas and energy traders and financial institutions.
The job involves statistical and written analysis of the LNG shipping market, focusing on the drivers of the underlying commodity trades as well as monitoring the development of the global fleet. This is a very exciting time to join our LNG desk, which is expanding rapidly in the face of a growing sector.

Responsibilities:
• Updating, monitoring, and developing statistical databases that are used for research publications, client services and consultancy reports
• Writing for weekly and monthly reports
• Leverage LNG and shipping market understanding to develop research materials and insights
• Preparing slide decks for client presentations
• Working on ad-hoc internal and external requests
• Collating market data and processing/interpreting datasets into reports/presentations
• Working closely with the broking teams and other analysts to support specific needs

Skills
• Excellent analytical and numerical skills and ability to manage and analyse large data sets
• Strong attention to detail, organised with reliable time management skills and a methodical approach
• Clear and concise verbal and written communication skills required to work with a global team
• Ability to work within a deadline driven environment with a strong work ethic and self-motivation
• Able to prioritise work according to deadlines
• 0-2 years previous shipping experience and/or gas and LNG markets
• Proficient in MS Office, particularly Excel, Word, PowerPoint & Publisher
• SQL or other programming language knowledge would be advantageous, but not required

Benefits for working for SSY
• Holidays: 25 days pro-rata from January to December
• Staff Life Assurance: 3 times your pensionable salary
• Pension: If you contribute a minimum of 3% of your pensionable salary, SSY will contribute an additional 6.5%
• Subsidised Gym Membership

Closing Date for Applications: 9th July 2024

Please note: This employment is subject to you being able to obtain the right to work in the UK.

• Issue broker comm invoice and follow up outstanding broker comm invoice (if any).

• Excellent communication and interpersonal skills. • This trainee position involved a fully in-house and hands-on training, with the candidate committed at least 1 year in the operation, post-fixture and claims department before mutually discussing further career projection with the supervisors.
