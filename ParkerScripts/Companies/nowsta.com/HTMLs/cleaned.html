How we work

It’s not all ping pong tables and free lunches. We’re traveling a path that’s rarely straight and is often fraught with peril.

When we encounter obstacles, when things are really tough, and when the dynamism of this hyper-growth start-up gets to us, we rely on one another. When we’re confused, we ask questions. Even when we’re afraid. When we receive feedback, we say thank you. Even when it stings. When we need to go the extra mile, we push ourselves. Even when we’re tired.

We do all of this and more because we care deeply.It takes a special constitution to flourish in a scale-up environment. Our work isn’t easy. And we embrace that.If you’re interested in putting in the effort to build something amazing, we’d love to support your candidacy

Where we work

We’re a remote-first company that works how you work. Whether you thrive in a remote role or love the structure of an office, working at Nowsta is rooted in flexibility. Choose to work from one of our hubs, entirely remote, or a mix of the two. At Nowsta, we care about results, not where you get them.

Who we are

We’re a quirky, innovative and passionate team with absolutely zero ego. We know it takes a special constitution to flourish in a start-up environment and, at Nowsta, the work is just as rewarding as the relationships we cultivate.
