Project Manager▼

Location:Yerevan, Armenia (On-site)
Deadline:2024-05-17
Term:Full Time
Level:Middle

Job description:

We are seeking an experienced Project Manager to join our team. 

Job responsibilities:

  • Project planning: Develop detailed project plans that outline the scope,  timelines and resources.
  • Communication: Acting as the primary point of communication between the project team and stakeholders, managing client relationships.
  • Quality Assurance: Ensuring the project deliverables meet the agreed-upon standards and stakeholder expectations through continuous oversight.
  • Team Leadership and Development: Leading and coaching project team in Agile frameworks and motivating, supporting them to succeed.
  • Problem-Solving: Identifying and resolving issues that arise during the project, requires quick thinking and decision-making to keep the project on track.

Required qualifications:

  • 2+ years of experience in a Project Manager and scrum master role
  • Excellent written and verbal English communication
  • Excellent knowledge of Scrum techniques and artifacts
  • Having experience working on international outsourcing projects
  • Deep understanding of software development processes, tools, technologies

Full Stack Developer▼

Location:Yerevan, Armenia (On-site)
Deadline:2024-06-30
Term:Full Time
Level:Middle

Job description:

We are seeking a talented Full-Stack Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in full-stack development with a focus on PHP, JavaScript, and modern frameworks such as Laravel, React.js, and Vue.js. You will be responsible for developing and maintaining web applications, ensuring their performance, responsiveness, and scalability.

Job responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain web applications using React, Vue, Laravel, and Node.js.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve issues and bugs on time.
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends.

Required qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, or a related field.
  • 2+ years of experience in full-stack development.
  • Proficiency in PHP and JavaScript.
  • Experience with Laravel, React.js, and Vue.js.
  • Knowledge of Node.js and its frameworks.

Additional information:

If you are passionate about web development and eager to work on innovative projects in the Web3/blockchain space, we would love to hear from you! Please send your resume and a cover letter detailing your experience and why you would be a great fit for our team to careers@yogurt.llc or use the form to apply.
