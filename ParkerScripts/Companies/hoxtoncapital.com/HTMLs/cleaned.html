Careers

Working For Hoxton

Hoxton Capital Management is looking for talented individuals to be part of our expansion. We are looking for individuals who believe in excellence. We specialise in hiring people with the drive to succeed and the will to implement the discipline required. We focus on nurturing and providing our team with an environment conducive to learning and growth. At Hoxton Capital, you are amongst the industry’s best, which presents a great learning opportunity and an inspiring, positive working environment.

What does Hoxton Capital do differently?

Our recruitment process is based on quality, not quantity. For existing advisers interested in moving within the industry, we look for a proven record of ethical practices, solid technical knowledge and a bank of existing clients who can vouch for your reputation.

What do we specialise in?

At Hoxton Capital Management, we focus primarily on pension advice and lump sum investors.

Would Hoxton Capital be right for you?

If you are looking for a long-term career in the industry and constantly strive to improve your knowledge and qualifications, then Hoxton could be your place. We look for individuals who take pride in their work and understand the value of reputation.

Benefits and Rewards

At Hoxton, your professional development and success are our priority. Our Financial advisors and business development managers are the best in the industry, and we ensure they have a clear pathway to success.

There are several ways we achieve this

  • We cover the cost of your qualifications.
  • We provide ongoing technical training.​
  • We support you with marketing and lead generation.
  • We provide world-class sales training.

All of this takes place in a relaxed working atmosphere surrounded by your like-minded multinational colleagues.

Hoxton Capital offers industry-leading training and development. We cover the costs for exams, including UK level 4, level 6 pension exams, and series 65 for the US. This ensures all our advisers are trained to the highest ability. We help our advisers with anything they need, and our centralised marketing and lead generation team endeavour to provide a continuous flow of inbound enquiries.
