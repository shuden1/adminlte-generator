Working at Zoo

(verb) /work·ing ət zü/

We’re a globally-distributed, remote-first team with the flexibility to maximize our impact and manage our schedules in ways that are most suitable for us.

We operate autonomously while fostering team collaboration via our weekly team syncs, lunch & learns, gaming sessions, and our various Github repos and Slack channels.

We’re empowered to take ownership, express opinions, and make decisions, and we see our team’s impact come to life everyday.

We work hard and we nerd snipe, and we do so with a genuine sense of camaraderie.

Our Values

  1. Tool building

  3. Conquer

  4. Non-toxic candor

    We promote and deliver transparent communication to create productive psychological safety.

    We are accountable for our work and deliverables, alongside the needs of ourselves, our team, and our customers.
