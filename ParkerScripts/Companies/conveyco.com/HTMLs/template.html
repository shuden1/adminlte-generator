We like working with good people.

As Conveyco continues to grow, we hire people who value collaboration, initiative, client respect, and a strong work ethic. Be sure to reference the position to which you are applying.

Controls Engineering (Automation Systems Engineering) Level 2 (Location Flexible)

This position is responsible for setting up, programming, checking, and modifying electrical control panels, circuitry, and equipment. Adhering to established guidelines of the Conveyco Technologies controls engineering department

To apply for a position at Conveyco, please submit a resume and cover letter to careers@conveyco.com. Be sure to reference the position to which you are applying.

Application Engineering and Estimation – Controls Systems (No Travel)

The individual will act as the technical liaison between sales and the internal support staff supporting the project through all aspects of proposal development, including engineering, RFP development, supplier qualification, and the estimation process. The application engineer’s role demands a high degree of operational expertise along with a strong mechanical engineering background.

Individuals should be comfortable and experienced in interfacing with client executive leadership teams and supporting or leading alternate design reviews and evaluations.  AE’s are expected to have a broad working knowledge of various manufacturers equipment, so they can lead conversations and develop designs that offer solutions that are appropriate and cost-effective.

To apply for a position at Conveyco, please submit a resume and cover letter to careers@conveyco.com. Be sure to reference the position to which you are applying.

Project Engineering (Pre/Post Sale)

Performs engineering design evaluations and works to complete projects within budget and scheduling restraints. Reviews industry policies and procedures to ensure data accuracy, security, and regulatory compliance. Provides technical support for the engineering process. May require a bachelor’s degree or its equivalent and 0-4+ years of experience in the field or in a related area.

Has knowledge of commonly used concepts, practices, and procedures within a particular field. Relies on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job. Works under immediate supervision. Primary job functions do not typically require exercising independent judgment. Typically reports to a supervisor or manager.
