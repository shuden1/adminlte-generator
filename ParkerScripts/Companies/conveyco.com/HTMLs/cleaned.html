Join Our Team – Great Career Opportunities

We like working with good people.

As Conveyco continues to grow, we hire people who value collaboration, initiative, client respect, and a strong work ethic. We offer a competitive salary and benefits package, and a great work environment. To apply for a position at Conveyco, please submit a resume and cover letter to careers@conveyco.com. Be sure to reference the position to which you are applying.

Right now we have the following career opportunities available:

Controls Engineering (Automation Systems Engineering) Level 2 (Location Flexible)

This position is responsible for setting up, programming, checking, and modifying electrical control panels, circuitry, and equipment. Adhering to established guidelines of the Conveyco Technologies controls engineering department

To apply for a position at Conveyco, please submit a resume and cover letter to careers@conveyco.com. Be sure to reference the position to which you are applying.

Application Engineering and Estimation – Controls Systems (No Travel)

The individual will act as the technical liaison between sales and the internal support staff supporting the project through all aspects of proposal development, including engineering, RFP development, supplier qualification, and the estimation process. The application engineer’s role demands a high degree of operational expertise along with a strong mechanical engineering background.
