Benefits

100% Remote

Flexible Schedule

Don’t miss your morning gym session or evening parties. As long as the work is done, you’re free to adjust the schedule to your needs.

Guidance and Mentoring by Motivated Leaders

Your growth is our growth. That’s why you’ll have an experienced mentor by your side to help you grow faster and expand your toolbox.

Coverage of Fitness Activities, Professional Meetups & Streaming Services

Do yoga, go swimming, never miss a new episode of your favorite series or a new music album – with our support. We also encourage self-education so we promote going to professional meetups & conferences.

Internal Workshops & Knowledge Sharing

You don’t always have to look for new knowledge and skills outside of the team. We at Stormotion love to learn together – whether through peer code reviews or internal workshops.

Awesome Team Retreats and Parties

Work hard, play hard. Stormotion anniversaries, New Year celebrations, laser tag outings, board game parties, Halloween movie nights and many more – we never miss an opportunity to get together and share some happy moments.

Memorable Anniversary & B-day Presents

They say, the best gift to someone is your time. Thus, we dedicate our time to come up with meaningful, creative and useful presents for our teammates. And branded merch is our specialty 😉

Online English Speaking Club

We work with customers all across Europe, the US and Australia – and we want you to feel confident communicating with them. Also, it’s a fun hour to get together with other team members, chat, laugh and discuss life beyond work.

Recruitment process

1

Recruitment screening

You'll chat with our recruiter - to find a match between your skills & knowledge and our role. Here, we'll also ask you a few questions in English.

2

Tech-Task & Technical interview

Depending on the role, you'll firstly receive a technical task with a 2-day deadline or invitation to LiveCoding challenge and after that, will be invited to a Technical Interview. This will be a video call with your future Team Lead, who'll share feedback on your Test Task, ask some technical questions to deeply understand your background.

3

Interview with co-Founders and Quick Decision

A short video call (15-20mins) to know whether we're on the same wavelength in terms of our values and cultural fit. If there's a match - we're happy to make you an offer and welcome you to Stormotion!
