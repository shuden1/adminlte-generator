Join Our Team

As an OutSystems partner our people are what makes us great. They are all experts in agile, lean development using OutSystems low-code platform. Our team is comprised of Product Owners (Engagement Managers), SCRUM Masters (Technical Leads) and Developers. Everyone has deep experience delivering both web and mobile projects and most are certified at one or more levels by OutSystems. Interested in learning more? Account Manager | France
Paris, France French and English
View Details View Details

As Account Manager, you will have the unique opportunity to manage a high-growth territory, selling both Do iT Lean’s best in market services and the #1 rated enterprise low-code platform OutSystems. You will focus on extending Do iT Lean’s successful services to the French market. You will be responsible for the entire sales cycle – from initial contact to sales process documentation to successful closing. You will work closely with other Do iT Lean Account Managers globally as well as with Pre-sales Engineers and partner with the OutSystems sales representatives to drive new opportunities. You will also take part in partner and customer events and contribute to the development and expansion of our business relationships.

Qualifications and Skills

  • Bachelor or Master’s degree
  • Experience in selling agile development services
  • Sound knowledge and experience in the technology & services markets 
  • Knowledge of the OutSystems ecosystem or other low-code suppliers is a plus
  • Working knowledge of CRM platforms to help manage sales cycles and tools such as LinkedIn to identify prospects

  • Excellent communication and organizational skills 
  • Critical thinking and a problem-solving attitude 
  • Team player with customer centric orientation
  • Fluent in both French and English
