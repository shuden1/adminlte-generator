Meaningful work

We create things that make people’s lives better. By joining us, you will start cooperation with various technological companies from different corners of the world. You will make an impact.

LANARS is our business. Everyone who is related to the company’s success — from CEO to security manager — works due to the most appropriate financial conditions. We spare no effort to offer market competitive salaries and above-market bonuses. Your work will be fairly evaluated.

We work with the most innovative technologies, industries and companies, and you will obtain experience of being a pioneer in the development of absolutely unique solutions. Nothing is carved in stone when it comes to progress, so the continuous exploration of new things is also guaranteed.

Comfortable conditions

Our team’s work is always comfortably organized. We offer a flexible work schedule, and guarantee a social package. Our workers can choose whether they want to work remotely or in the office. Our company hires according to a non-discrimination policy, thus, everyone can feel respected and free.

Growth

As an employer, LANARS is willing to be a better place for personal development. Every member of our team has their own development plan, and the company supports professional or language courses, sports activities and other ways for growth.
