Career Opportunities

We're Seeking Channel Managers (Nationwide)

Compensation
$45,000 to $70,000 Annually Plus Commission

Benefits Offered: Dental, Life, Medical, 401K, Paid-Time Off

Employment Type: Full-Time

Company Description: Headquartered in Cedar Knolls, NJ, TouchTone Communications is a facilities-based carrier and reseller. Privately-owned and debt-free company, TouchTone has been serving customers across the United States since 1993

  • Channel-oriented company with 25 years of industry experience
  • Nationwide product offering
  • Carrier-diverse, redundant network
  • What makes us different from other providers: individual, personalized support and customized solutions

    • Job Description

    The Agent Channel Manager is responsible for generating new revenue from the sale of TouchTone voice and data products and services through agent/channel partner relationships.

  • Recruits and qualifies new sales agents/channel partners
  • Communicates regularly with agent base to uncover new opportunities.
  • Assists agents to create, present and close opportunities with potential customers.
  • Ensures agents are educated and fully knowledgeable of TouchTone products, order process, support procedures, commissions and pricing.
  • Supports each agent partner to ensure they become, and continue to be, successful selling TouchTone’s full portfolio of products
  • Provides sales support, follow up and closing sales opportunities with TouchTone agents
  • As necessary, troubleshoot and communicate issues to various internal departments for resolution
  • Provides ongoing communication of products, processes or policy changes to assigned agents.
  • Provides feedback to sales management and marketing on a consistent basis.
  • Creates business plans as well as monthly/quarterly revenue targets
  • Remains current of technical information regarding TouchTone’s voice and data products and offerings, CPE, networking, etc.
  • Attends meetings, as requested, to assist in the development of our products and channel direction.
  • Meets with the Vice President of Sales and Marketing as needed to review partner outreach, sales metrics, and strategic opportunities

    • Performance Measures:

  • Achieves assigned sales quota.
  • Meets assigned expectations for agent retention.
  • Maintains high customer satisfaction rating with agents and agent’s customers.
  • Proactively seeks out new opportunities.

    • Minimum Requirements:

  • 5+ years in prior telecom sales and contracts within the agent community.
  • Valid driver’s license, good driving record, reliable transportation.
  • Strong negotiation, problem solving, and analytical skills
  • Strong people and relationship management skills
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team. Ability to work under pressure with tight deadlines. Ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Must be detail oriented in treatment of pricing and contracts.
  • Computer skills including Windows based applications (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook).
  • Ability to travel regionally and/or nationally as required.
