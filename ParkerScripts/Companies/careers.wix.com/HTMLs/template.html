    Wix & You

    Wix
    & You

    The work we do here enables millions of people worldwide to succeed online. Our users are our reason for being, and our commitment to building them the best products is our north star.

    ​

    ​

    Building the best is a challenge, and we’re proud of the extraordinary team of people who work together to make it happen. We are always pushing ourselves, the technology, and our way of thinking. We work hard, measure everything and continuously learn. We have a lot of fun while doing it.

    ​

    ​

    Which brings us to you.

    ​

    ​

    Our ambitious vision and the scale that we work at mean there's unlimited potential and support for you to grow and make an impact. If you’re a passionate doer, continuous learner, motivated by our mission, and this type of challenge makes your eyes light up, we’d love you to apply.
