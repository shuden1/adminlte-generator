Careers

Looking for a career in Financial Wellness? Join our FinFit team!

What’s it like to work at FinFit? We work hard, play hard and treat each other like family. FinFit’s work culture is vibrant, energetic and ever evolving. Innovation is not only encouraged – it’s expected.

We are seeking individuals with the unique ability to excel in their area of expertise, communicate effectively with team members to get things done and continually strive for excellence. FinFit supports a growing, thriving environment that values bright, motivated people working together to drive financial wellness services innovation.

A financial wellness career has never been this much fun. We guarantee it.
