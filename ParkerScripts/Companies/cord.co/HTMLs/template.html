Skip the waiting room, and get tested without leaving home.
Description
Size: 1 - 10 employees
Testmate Health’s simple urine test gives you results in minutes for the 4 common sexually transmitted diseases (STDs). Our test is just as accurate as a lab test and has been developed by sexual health doctors for you.
We take proven, validated technology out of the lab and compress it into tiny disposable kits that fit into the palm of your hand. Testmate combines DNA isothermal technology, with cutting edge Swiss microengineering techniques to bring you the world’s first home STD self-test.
Our mission is to put you in charge of your own sexual health by making accurate testing easy and accessible, no matter who you are, or where you are. It is our aim to work in partnership with healthcare providers, to help minimise appointments and save your precious time. We believe slow access to tests means slow access to health - and it’s time to change that.
