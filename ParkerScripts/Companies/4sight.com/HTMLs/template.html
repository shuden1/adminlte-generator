Careers

​Join the 4SiGHT Team!

As a fast-growing leader in the supply chain space, we’re always looking for exceptional talent to join our collaborative, hardworking team. We excel at delivering on client promises with every engagement and rely on our team’s expertise in consulting, engineering, and IT to deliver award-winning supply chain transformations. Join us for the opportunity to advance your career with like-minded professionals eager to make a difference for our clients—and have a rewarding experience in the process.

Benefits
4SiGHT provides a top-notch, comprehensive benefits package designed to enhance our employees’ work-life balance and future prosperity.

Health and Wellness
Stay healthy with medical, dental, and vision plans for yourself and your family. You can also plan ahead to save on anticipated expenses with medical and dependent care flex spending plans. Belong to a gym? 4SiGHT also reimburses a set amount for monthly fitness membership fees.

Retirement Savings
Join our 401k plan with matching to amp up your savings for the future.

Time Off
Enjoy paid vacation days and national holidays to make sure you get some R&R. We also offer maternity and paternity leave and time off for bereavement.

Compensation/Bonus Plans
4SiGHT is proud that its pay structure is above the industry average—and we offer multi-tiered bonus plans to round out your pay package.
