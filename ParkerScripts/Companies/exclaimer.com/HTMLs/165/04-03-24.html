Want to join us? Heres the scoop

Were on a mission to supercharge email signatures for businesses across the globe. Join our team of self-confessed tech geeks, to work on an award-winning product in one of the fastest growing SaaS companies. Read on to get the scoop on life at Exclaimer.

View open roles

About us

Were a tech scale-up that brings world-class email signature management to Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace users. Every day, we get to work on an award-winning product designed to make the lives of IT and marketing professionals easier.

Our headquarters are just outside of London, and we have regional offices worldwide, though we operate a remote/hybrid work model. Our solution is used by over 60,000 customers in 150+ countries, including the likes of Sony, Bank of America and the BBC.

Find out more about us

Exclaimer life

Weve got big growth plans. Were proud to have almost doubled our workforce over the last two years, but its our incredible team who made this happen.

We have regular company meet-ups with Quarterly Business Reviews and annual company kick-offs. We embrace learning with funded training opportunities, internal workshops, and a monthly book club. And we celebrate the big and little wins daily by giving each other virtual tacos (we even have a taco wizard who sometimes comes along to hand out prizes!).

And we care equally about your wellbeing, because you wont perform your best when youre not you. Thats why we host wellness workshops, give all employees a subscription to the Calm app, and even sneak our dogs into the office for some puppy therapy.

We live and breathe our company values: velocity, curiosity, learning, change, and success. If you come to work with us, youll see that we embrace these values through our work every day.

Boost your career

We believe in creating an environment where employees can thrive. We've got so many projects, challenges, and learning experiences waiting for you to dive into. As you kick off your Exclaimer adventure, you'll quickly realize that teamwork is the secret sauce to our success.

We foster interdependence, where everyone has a unique role to play. You won't just be a cog in the machine; we'll hand you the keys to projects, trusting you with ownership and empowering you to make a real impact in a fast-growing SaaS company. We know that autonomy is like a turbo boost for creativity and growth, so each employee gets to really own what they do. So, get ready for a job where your professional growth is nurtured, and your potential knows no bounds.
