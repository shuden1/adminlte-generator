Careers at iink payments

At iink Payments, our people are really good at what they do. And they’re happy doing it. Come see why.

As a first-of-its-kind insurance claim check processing fintech start-up, we value self-motivation, hard work, teamwork, and creativity in all of our team members. If these values align with your own, we encourage you to explore the career opportunities available at our company.

We believe that every member of our team has the potential to make a significant impact on our success. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting your career, we are committed to providing you with the support and resources you need to thrive.

As a member of our team, you'll have the opportunity to work on exciting and meaningful projects, collaborate with a talented and diverse group of colleagues, and contribute to the growth and development of our company.
