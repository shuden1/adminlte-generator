Market Logic’s award-winning Insights Engagement platform leverages AI to empower insights professionals and business professionals to act fast on insights and make smarter moves ahead of the competition.

 

We empower insights and analytics teams to turn data and information into actionable consumer and market knowledge, known as insights. Using our DeepSights AI assistant, business users to uncover the latest insights intuitively and incorporate them into go-to-market strategies faster.   

 

At Market Logic, you’ll be driving the evolution of how companies manage their knowledge, research, and business plans leveraging AI. Working closely with a dynamic group of engineers, salespeople, and product managers, you’ll play a part in bringing business innovation to life every day.
