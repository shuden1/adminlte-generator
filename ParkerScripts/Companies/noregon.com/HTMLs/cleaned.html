Future-Proofing Commercial Vehicle Maintenance

Learn how Noregon is helping the industry maximize vehicle uptime, improve decision making, and advance technician skillsets.

MISSION

Drive technological advancements in commercial equipment service and maintenance to ensure the success of our customers, employees, business partners, and the communities we serve.

VISION

Be the industry-recognized leader in service and maintenance technologies for commercial trucking and heavy-duty equipment by designing, building, and delivering products and solutions that positively change what is possible within our industry.

VALUES

RESPONSIBILITY

We will grow our business responsibly by considering the needs of our customers, the impact on our community, and our place in the industry. It is our responsibility to improve safety within the commercial vehicle and equipment industry and take accountability for results.

INTEGRITY

We will carry out our business and actions with the highest standard of integrity, which means we act with high ethical standards, treat others with honesty and respect, and keep our commitments to all stakeholders.

INNOVATION

We will drive technological advancements for the commercial vehicle, equipment service and maintenance industries by creating an environment that inspires our employees and fosters results-based creative innovation. These technological advancements will ensure the success of our people, customers, business partners, and the communities we serve.

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION

We are determined to exceed customer expectations and anticipate each customer’s needs in advance. Our commitment is to combine our core values of responsibility, innovation, and integrity, resulting in unparalleled customer satisfaction.
