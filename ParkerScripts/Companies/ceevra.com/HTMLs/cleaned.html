Pursue a career with Ceevra

Making a dent in the universe

We help surgeons determine the best type of operation to perform and the best way to perform it. Most of our users are cancer surgeons, peforming complex operations for life-threatening disease.

Developing leading-edge technology

Each day, we’re breaking new ground in applying advanced computing techniques such as machine learning to the field of medical imaging.

Open positions
