Account Manager

Spearhead the entire billing process and liase between the company and the long-term care facilities. Daily duties include communicating with residents, families, and facility staff to ensure all collections, admissions, and finance needs are up to date and taken care of. This position is best suited for someone who enjoys creating relationships with people and is an efficient multitasker. Learn More

Accounts Receivables

Medicaid Pending Specialist

Ensure all residents are approved for Medicaid in a timely fashion by coordinating between families, care facilities, and Medicaid. Speak with families to help them spend down their assets and gather the documents required to gain approval while remaining in contact with Medicaid throughout the approval process. This position is best suited for someone who is communicative, can effectively multitask, and is goal-oriented. Learn More
