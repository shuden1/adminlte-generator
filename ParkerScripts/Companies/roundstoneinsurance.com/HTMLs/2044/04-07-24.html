    Who is Roundstone?

    Founded in 2003, Roundstone is not your typical insurance company. We’re on a mission to help employers save on healthcare benefits so they can put those savings towards bettering their businesses and taking care of their employees.

    We believe in affordable, high-quality healthcare for all. Our solution is an innovative self-funded group medical captive that enables small and midsize organizations to band together and purchase health benefits like the Fortune 500. The average client with Roundstone saves 20% compared to traditional fully insured plans.

    A Better Benefits Package

    We’re leaders in our industry, so naturally we look out for our employees’ best interests with a robust benefits package.

    Day 1 PTO Eligibility

    Roundstone PTO balances are updated annually and pro-rated if you start mid-year. This means your time is yours to use from day 1!

    Dependent Care and Parental Leave

    Roundstone matches contributions to this plan to allow employees to provide day-care or in-home care for their children and dependent elders at a reduced cost. Additionally, we understand the importance of family. We provide 2 weeks paid leave to spend time bonding with a new child, whether through child birth, adoption or foster care placement.

    Tuition Reimbursement

    Roundstone reimburses you for pursuing the education to advance your career. Whether the reimbursement is for a single class or applied towards a degree program, we want to support your success!

    Medical, Dental and
    Vision Benefits

    Roundstone offers a full range of benefits, including a self-funded health plan, prescription drug benefit, dental plan, and vision plan. Roundstone even contributes toward medical expenses through an HSA and awards wellness program discounts on monthly premiums for participating employees.

    Annual Bonus

    Roundstone’s success is your success. Each year, employees receive up to 33% of their base compensation as a cash or retirement fund contribution, based on Roundstone’s growth and profitability. The bonus amount has been at the maximum level consistently for the past 8 years!

    Good Work/Life Balance

    Roundstone’s culture supports your total well-being, from providing an in-office fitness trainer and gym, to the EAP that offers financial, legal, career, and mental health support to employees free of charge. We also never miss a chance to celebrate and host family-friendly events throughout the year.

    Community Outreach and Sponsorships

    Roundstone never misses a chance to give back to the Lakewood and Greater Cleveland communities. Throughout the year, get involved in volunteering and sponsored events supporting local causes.

    Hybrid Working

    Roundstone has a rotating office schedule with employees alternating in-office weeks between their departments.

    401(k) Plan

    401(k) Plan: Roundstone matches 40% up to 8% on employee contributions to this plan.

    Introduce Yourself

    Each open job listing includes the option to upload a resume or apply through Indeed. You’ll also be asked to take an 8-10 minute survey as part of our culture index. This is not a test that will be evaluated pass or fail, just a chance to get to know you better.

    As a growing company, we are always looking for talented, enthusiastic and hard-working people to join our team. Don’t see an open job listing that fits your skills? Complete our 2-minute questionnaire to join our employment pipeline and we’ll keep you informed of future job openings that may be a good fit for you.
