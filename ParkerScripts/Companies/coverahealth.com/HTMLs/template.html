Making a difference

At Covera, you have the ability to directly impact millions of lives. Starting in radiology, we’re helping doctors enhance their care, patients get the right diagnosis, and the healthcare system support quality improvement at scale. To us, success is found in every patient, every doctor, and every outcome that we touch.

Solving tough challenges

Understanding quality is hard. Navigating healthcare is hard. Growing startups is hard. But we don’t shy away from challenges – we lean in! We’re incredibly passionate about and committed to solving big problems to improve the world, and today, that means helping doctors care for their patients; helping payers support their members, employees and providers; and helping each other grow personally and professionally.

To change everything, we need everyone

At Covera, we believe that amazing things happen when everyone feels respected, supported, and connected at work. We honor and celebrate differences in gender, age, race, faith, ethnicity, sexual orientation and gender identity, not simply because it is the right thing to do, but because it’s essential to everything we want to accomplish together. Covera's DEI committee partners directly with our senior leadership to set and advance initiatives rooted in our commitment to social justice in the workplace.
