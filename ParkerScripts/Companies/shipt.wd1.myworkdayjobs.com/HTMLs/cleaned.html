About Us

Shipt is an online marketplace providing same-day delivery from national retailers and local stores. Powered by a community of exceptional shoppers and convenient app and web platforms, we bring fresh groceries, home essentials, pet supplies, and more to more than 70% of households in over 5,000 U.S. cities. 

Shipt is an independently-operated, wholly-owned subsidiary of Target Corp. It was founded and is headquartered in Birmingham, Alabama, and also has an office in San Francisco. For more information, visit shipt.com.
