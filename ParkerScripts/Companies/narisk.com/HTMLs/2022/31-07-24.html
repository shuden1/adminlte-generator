Join our Team!

NARS is a privately owned, rapidly growing third party claims administrator. Although we have offices nationwide, NARS maintains a close-knit family feel. Benefits of joining our team include:

  • Competitive salaries
  • Generous benefits package
  • Up to a 6% match on 401(k)
  • In-house CEUs
  • Wellness program
  • National employee discount programs
  • Access to pet insurance through Pumpkin Pet Insurance
  • College tuition savings plan
  • Tuition reimbursement for insurance credentials/designations
  • Employee Activity and Community Involvement committees
  • Work-life balance
  • Upward mobility

We have a strong and dedicated team and our employee retention is well above the industry average. We are looking for individuals that are inspired by a fast-paced environment, enjoy variety in their tasks and have a desire to excel in the insurance industry.

If you have any questions, please email us at careers@narisk.com.
