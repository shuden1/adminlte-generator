Join our team now at Siemens Healthineers as IT Business Analyst for HR topics

This is a role well suited to an ambitious professional, looking for the next step in their career. As an IT Business Analyst for HR topics, you will be responsible for:

• You act as the functional HR/IT Business Analyst and Solution Designer for all HR/IT Absence- & Time Management related topics mainly US and Canada
• You align business demands and processes with our IT strategy and consult the business accordingly while acting as the subject matter expert within the respective portfolio element.
• You will translate the business demands into IT service requirements and ensure proper implementation of your solution design following our IT service management- and IT compliance framework.
• You think about the 'big picture', considering end-to-end processes and identify cross-topic dependencies.
• You collaborate closely with internal stakeholders and external service management teams and drive related activities.

This position may suit you best if you are familiar with what is below, and would like to do develop your career with Healthineers

Required skills to have for the success of this role

• You hold a bachelor’s or master’s degree in a subject related to information technology or business administration.

• You have a minimum of 3-5 years’ experience with SAP HCM Solutions - in depth time-management.

• You have a thorough understanding of Absence- & Time Management related processes with focus on US and Canada specific regulations and respective IT solutions / integrations

• You are experienced working in international and interdisciplinary HR/IT related projects.

• You have a good knowledge of managing IT service providers.

• You are familiar with and understand the compliance requirements as they pertain to IT projects in the healthcare industry.

Your Experience

• You have a personal affinity with the digital world and modern cloud-based technology.

• You strive to translate business challenges and operational challenges into value-adding technology solutions

• You are a well-structured and detail-oriented worker with a strong ability to think analytically, you are able to work independently, and you have a thirst for knowledge

• You work efficiently and you don't compromise on quality, even under intense time pressure and in highly complex situations.

• You have particularly strong teamworking skills, are proactive and like taking the lead on topics and you are highly motivated to drive topics to completion and deliver

• You have a knack for stakeholder management and a high level of service orientation.

• You already have extensive experience with international and intercultural cooperation.

• You communicate reliably across all organizational levels and working cultures.
