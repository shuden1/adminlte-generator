Company

Organization

Job Family

Experience Level

Experienced Professional

Siemens Mobility provides efficient and coordinated transportation of people and goods by rail – including rolling stock, rail automation, rail electrification, and service. We craft and manufacture across the entire spectrum of rolling stock, including commuter and regional passenger trains, light rail and streetcars, metros, locomotives, passenger coaches, and high-speed trainsets. Cities and Railroads also rely on us to provide traction-power substations and electricity transmission, as well as signaling and control technology for freight and passenger rail and transit systems.

As a Commodity Management Professional for Siemens Mobility Rolling Stock, you will serve as the primary procurement contact between local management, internal clients, and the supply chain management organization. You will collaborate with key departments including Material Management, Quality Management, Engineering, and Logistics. You will demonstrate a sufficient understanding of the global supplier market to select the most suitable vendors who can deliver high-quality products at competitive pricing. We will rely on your outstanding negotiation skills to build new value with regard to key metrics such as cost, lead time, and quality. In this role, you will drive performance with suppliers, collaborate with internal teams and make your mark within a team that is passionate about winning, succeeding, and making an impact.

Responsibilities

  • Main contact/interface within procurement and to your supply base for all inquiries in a proposal and execution phase for assigned commodities
  • Implement commercial evaluation of proposals. Conduct negotiations and contract documents that include price, scope, schedule, contractual terms and conditions, and others as applicable (for example, special requirements due to manufacturing location restrictions, government project requirements, etc.).
  • Lead supplier technical reviews with Engineering and Quality. Drive clarifications and provide innovative solutions to tackle highly sophisticated supply/supplier problems.
  • Work closely and collaboratively with Project Procurement Management (PPM), Engineering, Quality, Global Sourcing Team, and others as vital to strategize the best decision/outcome for Siemens.
  • Coordinate and lead local or regional focus teams to achieve optimum conditions and savings through collaborative cross-divisional sourcing targeted activities.

Required Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Supply Chain Management, Business Administration, Engineering, or other relevant area a plus
  • 3+ years of related experience in procurement, project management, or engineering with an international organization
  • Solid knowledge in Supply Chain Management, Strategic Sourcing, Commodity Management, Procurement Market Analysis, commercial terms and conditions, and high-mix low volume production.
  • Highly refined negotiating skills. Detailed track record of optimally negotiating complex, high-dollar contracts for products or services.
  • Proven track record of being ambitious, proactive, self-motivated, and willing to go above and beyond.

Preferred Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience

  • Ability to read and comprehend Engineering specifications and drawings.
  • Conduct should cost analysis
  • Experience with Build to Print fabrication requirements
  • Foreign languages (German, Spanish, Portuguese)
  • Solid experience in advanced negotiation concepts (e.g. eAuction)

Why you'll love working for Siemens:

  • Freedom and healthy work-life balance– Health, Dental, Vision Insurance, HSA/FSA, Commuter Benefits – starting Day 1
  • Solve the world's most significant problems – Be part of exciting and innovative projects.
  • Engaging, challenging, and fast-evolving, pioneering technological environment.
  • Competitive total rewards package.
  • Please contribute to our social responsibility initiatives passionate about access to education, access to technology, sustaining communities, and positively impacting the community.
