Codesmith

Our mission is to develop an exceptional network of software engineering leaders, who are technically brilliant, collaborative, supportive, and passionate about pushing the engineering community forward.

We're building a team of remarkable people who have a desire to drive our mission forward while also cultivating and contributing to a community of empathy, excellence, and innovation. 

We're looking for candidates who have pushed something to an extraordinary level - something that required determination and ingenuity. It might be your academic success, sports, a company, an organization, or a leadership role you held. We want to see that you're driven and ambitious but put the deeper company mission first.

If this sounds like a good fit for you, take a look at our open positions and apply to the role that best matches your skills, goals, and interests.

If there is more than one job you would like to be considered for, please only apply for your top choice. You may indicate any other role(s) you are interested in on your initial application.
