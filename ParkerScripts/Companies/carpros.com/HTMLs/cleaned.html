Car Pros Automotive Group is a well-respected automotive industry veteran, with change management leadership, a franchise retail advocate and EV evangelist. Founded in 1993, Car Pros began as a small used car lot. Our CEO, Matthew Phillips, and his family built it from two employees into an automotive group with over 700 employees and nine stores in Western Washington and Southern California, representing Kia, Hyundai, Honda, BMW and MINI. Car Pros sells over 2,500 cars per month with annual sales of over $1 billion, and is currently the top Kia retailer in the United States, having retailed over 175,000 new Kia models.

Mr. Phillips' personal journey of going from “lot to leadership” gives him a real-world perspective on how to build diverse, high-performance workplace cultures which he passes along to all at Car Pros.  He is a next-gen leader who brings in-the-trenches experience to his vision of changing lives and creating opportunity through excellence, as well as a powerful background in organizational psychology, financial management and business which leads us all to success.
