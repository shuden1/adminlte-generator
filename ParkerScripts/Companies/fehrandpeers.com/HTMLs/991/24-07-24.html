Expand Upon Your Abilities & Interests

We serve as an industry leader for the professional growth and development of our staff, which is at the very heart of the Fehr & Peers mission. We provide a uniquely robust Career Development Program, through which each employee may map out goals and take advantage of resources to accelerate individual growth.

Customized Coaching & Mentoring Programs

We offer a unique, multi-faceted program of both formal and informal mentoring to support every level of an employee’s career.

Early Career Mentoring Program

In year two, staff members have the opportunity to identify an informal mentor in another office to connect with for a more thorough understanding of company entrepreneurial leadership and culture, technical knowledge, and career advancement opportunities within the firm.

Senior Leadership Coaching Program

Our senior leaders experience a rigorous, high-level leadership program through group activities and peer-to-peer learning for developing effective leadership and management, trust-based teambuilding, time management, strategy and execution, management of conflict, and much more.

One-On-One Executive Coaching Program

Recognizing that even those at the highest ranks of the company will benefit from mentoring, our Executive Coaching program is dedicated to the development of senior leaders, and is strongly tailored to the individual’s development areas of interest

New Hire Buddy Program

To help integrate our new team members into the company culture right away, we pair each new employee with a “buddy” to assist in navigating the new position and answering questions.

One-On-One Formal Mentoring Program

As part of our investment in the long-term career development of our staff members, we offer an individualized, formal one-year partnership with a company-wide leader to work toward and accomplish next-level career development goals.

Career Insights

These free events offer an educational look into the transportation planning and engineering profession, as well as information on how you too could positively impact how people travel within a community.

At Fehr & Peers, we value industry participation and understand that a career in transportation planning offers opportunities to make meaningful impacts on how individuals experience travel within their communities. Career Insights began as a webinar-based forum for up-and-coming transportation consultants to learn more about the industry and have some of their burning questions answered. From program’s start in 2016, we committed to making sure those answers were unfiltered—objective, transparent, and unbiased. With that spirit in mind, our program now offers webinars and resources to students from a wide variety of backgrounds who share a curiosity about transportation. Students have the opportunity to learn from and talk to Fehr & Peers professionals to explore how their unique experiences and interests align with opportunities to improve communities through transportation.
