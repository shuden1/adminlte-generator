PROS Contract FLT Auditor

Remote
ARGUS PROS is a leading aviation services provider committed to ensuring the highest standards of safety, quality, and compliance. We are currently seeking an experienced and qualified individual to join our team as an IOSA Flight Auditor and PROS Support Advisor. The PROS Support Program (PSP) is a comprehensive service aimed at enhancing operational safety for aviation operators navigating the IOSA process. As a PROS Support Advisor, you'll play a vital role in guiding operators through documentation reviews, implementation assessments, and continuous conformance monitoring, ensuring adherence to IOSA standards. With a focus on collaboration, training, and innovative solutions, the PSP offers a dynamic platform to contribute to the safety and efficiency of global aviation.
