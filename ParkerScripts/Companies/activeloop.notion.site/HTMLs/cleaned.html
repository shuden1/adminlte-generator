About

Activeloop is building Deep Lake, the database for AI.  With Deep Lake, (https://github.com/activeloopai/deeplake; 7K+ stars on GitHub), teams can store complex data, such as images, audio, videos, annotations, & tabular data, in a AI-native format and stream them to Foundational Models real-time. The team is globally distributed but primarily looking to hire in the US right now. The team behind is from Princeton, Stanford, Google, and Tesla and is backed by Y Combinator. We have recently raised a $5M seed round.
