Careers Opportunities

World-class engineering starts with our employees

Every great product needs a great business behind it. innovative, challenging and rewarding, it takes a wide range of people and skills to deliver world-class German-made equipment.

Founded in 1871, we are a leading global producer of sheetfed offset printing presses and related technologies for commercial, packaging and publishing applications.
Manroland Sheetfed’s HQ is based in Offenbach, Germany with its own subsidiaries, sales and service partners across the globe.

Manroland is part of the of the multi-disciplined global UK engineering group, Langley Holdings plc.
