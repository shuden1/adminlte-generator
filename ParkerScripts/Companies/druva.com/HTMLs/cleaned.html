Who We Are

We’re innovators on a mission to defend business data and eliminate the stress typically associated with data protection and security. We believe this dedication to data will transform how every IT and security team operates, and our industry-first AI copilot for backup is already positively impacting companies worldwide. As a late-stage startup with efficient growth, we have grand ambitions for the future and the expertise to execute. 

If you like tackling real challenges, seeing your efforts make a difference, and supporting a diverse culture, Druva is the right place for you.

Social Responsibility

We take social responsibility seriously – and are committed to positively impacting our communities globally. We’re culturally diverse, with employees and offices distributed across geographies, and we have ample opportunity to give back to the populations we serve. Our technologies are built with environmental sustainability in mind, and we actively promote sustainability with cloud-native solutions that eliminate the need for physical hardware or travel for deployment.

Flexibility and Balance

Druva was founded in India and is now headquartered in the heart of Silicon Valley. With offices worldwide, our employees work in a hybrid environment, with some working in our offices and others working remotely from states and countries closest to our customers. We strongly believe that life comes first and prioritize our employees' well-being and mental health.

Perks and Benefits

We’re committed to ensuring every Druvan feels valued and cared for. Our comprehensive rewards package offers excellent earning potential and allows our employees to maintain their well-being with the flexibility to accommodate their changing needs. Benefits vary by country.

  • Well-being: We ensure Druvans and their families have comprehensive healthcare and mental health programs.

  • Family-Oriented: We provide paid maternity, paternity, adoption and family medical care leave – plus funding to support adoption, fertility treatment, and other related expenses.

  • Financial Gain: We offer competitive compensation, FSA/HSA accounts, equity, and more, to reward Druvans for their hard work.

  • Rest and Re-Energize: We encourage Druvans to rest with time away as often as needed because we believe it’s our employees’ passion and creativity that makes Druva special.

Embark on a New Journey

Each and every one of our employees are co-captains in navigating what’s next for Druva and our customers. Regardless of your specialty, years of experience, or industry expertise - your dream job awaits.
