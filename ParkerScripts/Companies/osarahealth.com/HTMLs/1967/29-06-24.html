    Our Culture

    Powered by individuals driven to take action

    • We’re a diverse and inclusive team passionate about transforming cancer care.
    • We are innovative and move fast – fresh ideas are welcomed and actioned.
    • We’re ready to simplify a complex, overwhelming problem and need people ready to make a change to join us on the mission.
    • Finally, transforming the outcomes for patients is our North Star. We are all here to do something meaningful that changes what it’s like to be impacted by cancer.

    Our values

    We are committed to data security.

    We speak from the heart.

    We are committed to a culture that welcomes, respects and values.

    We trust openly and honestly.

    We do the right thing when no one is watching.

    We harness innovation to achieve better health results.

    We operate an evidence first approach.

    Life at Osara

    • Remote first – we’re happy for you to work where you’re most comfortable.
    • A yearly professional development budget and learning opportunities from doctors, cancer specialists, operations experts and many, many more.
    • One additional wellbeing day each year for you to use as you wish.
    • In 2024, Osara Health was recognized as an Impact100 company, acknowledged as one of the most impactful companies to work for in America.
