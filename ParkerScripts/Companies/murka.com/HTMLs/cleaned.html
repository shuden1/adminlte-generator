About us

Murka Games is an international game development company that has been successfully producing social casino and casual games for over a decade. For several years in a row, Murka’s titles were called the most innovative in the category and ranked among top European companies: • Top Publishers Award of App Annie’s 2021 • EGR Global North America Awards 2021 • Top 15 Social Casino Game Publishers by EKG In 2019, Murka Games was acquired by the Blackstone Group, one of the world’s largest private equity firms. Murka Games brings fun and joy to 5 million monthly players in more than 200 countries worldwide. With ambitious creation plans in the mobile gaming industry, we are actively looking for new talents to join our team.
