PRESS RELEASE

Founder & Chairman of the Board Anton Krasnyy Steps Down

[26.10.23] – Murka Games (“Murka” or “the Company”), the international social and casual game development company, today announced that after 14 years Anton Krasnyy has decided to step down from his role as Chairman of the Board effective immediately.

Since Anton founded Murka in 2009, the Company has grown from a small Ukraine-based start-up to a top 10 force in Social Casino with titles including Scatter Slots, Slots Era, Infinity Slots and Scatter Poker, successfully expanding its Casual Gaming franchise and internationalizing the business with headquarters in Cyprus. In 2019, funds managed by Blackstone Tactical Opportunities acquired a majority stake in Murka to support this growth trajectory. Today, Murka has over 400 employees, and c.2 million monthly active users.

In January 2021, Anton transitioned from Chief Executive Officer to Chairman of the Board, with Chief Operating Officer Barak David appointed Chief Executive Officer.

Anton Krasnyy, Founder and Outgoing Chairman of Murka, said: “Since founding Murka 14 years ago, I have seen the Company grow beyond what I could have imagined. I am very proud of everything we have achieved and would like to thank the management team and all our incredible colleagues who have supported me and worked very hard to deliver exciting games to our player community. I leave my position knowing Murka is in very capable hands and look forward to following the Company’s progress in the years to come.”

Barak David, Chief Executive Officer of Murka, said: “On behalf of the Board and our employees, I would like to thank Anton for his guidance and wisdom over many years. I have had the honour to work closely alongside Anton, whose product vision and leadership have shaped the strength of the organisation and of Murka’s top titles today. Anton leaves Murka in a strong position with huge opportunities ahead, and we wish him every success going forward.”
