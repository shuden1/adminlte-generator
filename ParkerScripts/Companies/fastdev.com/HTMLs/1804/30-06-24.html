What is Fastdev?
Fastdev is a Swedish software development company providing our services to clients all over the world. Our vision is to improve quality of life through software solutions.
At Fastdev, you’ll be on a mission with us to create amazing products and experiences for our customers. We build our company’s success on a few values: we strive for excellence, we believe in our people, we keep our word and we put our customers’ interests first. Our approach has helped us grow to around 200 employees around the globe.
Our team members love solving problems and get excited about new technologies. We work in a vibrant atmosphere, with an emphasis on personal and professional development. We believe in work-life balance which is why our perks and benefits extend from the office to life at home. This is a community you will want to be a part of.
Come and join our team!
