From humble beginnings to the industry leader it is today, SpectrumVoIP has grown exponentially in both size and expertise since its inception in 2007.

What was once just a four employee company hosting an entire data center, is now over 300 employees all across the nation, with headquarters in a beautiful two-story office space in Plano, TX. SpectrumVoIP has changed the face of VoIP technology and continues to be the leader in innovation and talent.

This tremendous success is made possible by its team of exceptional people, the true architects of our success. SpectrumVoIP aims to nurture, encourage, and empower each employee’s professional growth and success by placing high value on continuous learning and personal development, which allow our teams to keep up with technological advances and industry standards, while attaining their personal goals along the way.

While we are serious about the work we do, we don’t take ourselves too seriously. We offer a relaxed, fun, and community-driven work environment at all of our locations. We hold weekly company-wide lunches, monthly events, and stock our office with ping pong and shuffleboard tables to further enable team building. We strongly believe that a team-oriented mindset helps us come together and achieve superior results.

As a family-owned company, SpectrumVoIP is dedicated to creating a healthy work/life balance. We understand that our employees have their own lives and juggling professional responsibilities with other important personal aspects of their lives can be a challenge at times. For this reason, we prioritize mental health and well-being, and encourage our teams to utilize the many resources we offer to help reduce stress and maintain health.

If you are a bright and talented professional with technical skills and initiative, we encourage you to join our rapidly growing company.
