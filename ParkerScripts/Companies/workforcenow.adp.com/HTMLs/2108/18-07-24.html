First Heritage Federal Credit Union is a leading employer and financial institution in the communities it serves and is dedicated to the success and financial well-being of both our members and our team. We currently operate 9 full-service locations throughout Chemung and Steuben Counties, NY and Tioga County, PA. Dedicated to being an employer of choice, we offer a competitive salary and a comprehensive benefit package. We also offer options for professional growth and development including educational assistance and internal and external leadership programs. Here at First Heritage, we strive to ensure our employees have both the knowledge and resources to pave their own career path.

Here at First Heritage, we strive to put the value of family in our family helping yours. We believe strongly in giving back to our community by offering scholarships to students, volunteering at local food banks, and participating in various events, such as parades and holiday events.
