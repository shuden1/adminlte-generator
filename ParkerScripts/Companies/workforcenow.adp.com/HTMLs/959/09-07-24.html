Fraudulent Job Posting: Phishing Alert Announcement:

It has been brought to our attention that a fraudulent Administrative Assistant job was posted this week on LinkedIn. The imitation job was posted as a part-time, remote Administrative Assistant and was promoted by a fake HR Assistant name. Additional reports today indicate that fraudulent job offers have been communicated related to this post. MEI has notified LinkedIn, and the LinkedIn Security Team is investigating this scam.

All MEI recruitment communications will be conducted via a formal MEI Company email address, never a personal Gmail email address. Our interview process will always include direct contact information for our company, and formal interviews will be required before employment offers will be extended. All employment offers will be issued and completed using our verified Applicant Tracking System process. If you, or someone that you know, has received a message about a remote, part-time Administrative Assistant position, please report this communication to law enforcement and LinkedIn. Please do not provide any sensitive, personal, and/or banking information. 


  Questions regarding our job opportunities can be directed to: Careers@meillc.com .  Thank you!
