UI/UX Designer (McLean, VA)

    Collaborate with developers, to complete the full scope of web development projects. Conduct user research, determine design requirements, and analyze user feedback to improve design quality. Develop new visual design concepts and modify concepts based on feedback. Help establish and promote design guidelines, best practices, and standards for the company and clients. Confer with product management and engineering teams to define product strategy, prioritize needs, resolve conflicts, develop content criteria, and choose solutions. Generate workflows, wireframes, and pixel-perfect, workable designs to deliver to developers. Identify problems uncovered by testing or customer feedback, and correct problems or refer problems to appropriate personnel for correction.
    Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Programming, or Web Design is required. Two years of experience in the job is required. 40hr/wk. Send resume to Faridun Nazarov, COO, Fourstay Inc, fn@4stay.com.
