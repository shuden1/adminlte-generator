    • Central Fabrication Customer Service

    Full Time — Troy, MI

    $13.00 - $17.00 per hour

    Becker Orthopedic is a leading biomedical device manufacturer for the orthotics industry. We are currently seeking Entry Level or Experienced Customer service representatives for our custom device manufacturing facility in Troy, Michigan.

    Customer Service Representatives will review and process incoming work for production. This will include communicating with customers, issuing quotes, and evaluating incoming work among other standard administrative duties.

    Required Skills
    • Typing
    • Microsoft Word and Outlook
    Preferred, but not required skills
    • Experience in the orthotics industry
    • Basic knowledge of human anatomy
    • Customer Service experience
    • Microsoft Excel
    • Sales

    The successful candidate will be reliable and detail oriented with strong organization and communication skills. The desire and ability to contribute to a growing, CQI oriented production team are required.

    This employer has been in business since 1938 and is an Equal Opportunity Employer. Competitive pay and benefits including medical, dental, Dependent Care/Health FSA, life insurance, paid holidays & time off, 401K plan.

    Orthotic Tech Trainee

    Full Time — Troy, MI

    $14.00 - $17.00 per hour

    Becker Orthopedic is a leading biomedical device manufacturer for the orthotics industry. We are currently seeking Entry Level trainees to assist our skilled technicians in the fabrication of orthotic devices at our custom device manufacturing facility in Troy, Michigan. The trainee will learn the required skills on the job and will be incrementally transitioned to independent working.

    This position requires a strong work ethic, mechanical aptitude, communication skills, and attention to detail. Individuals who enjoy working with their hands to fabricate unique devices are invited to apply.

    The successful candidate will be reliable and detail oriented with strong organization and communication skills. The desire and ability to contribute to a growing, CQI oriented production team are required.

    This employer has been in business since 1938 and is an Equal Opportunity Employer. Competitive pay and benefits including medical, dental, Dependent Care/Health FSA, life insurance, paid holidays & time off, 401K plan.

    Becker Orthopedic is one of the leaders in the O&P profession with a long history of manufacturing and providing superior patient care in southeast Michigan.
    We offer a competitive salary, 401K and full benefits including: Health, Dental, Short/Long-Term Disability, Life and AD&D insurances.
