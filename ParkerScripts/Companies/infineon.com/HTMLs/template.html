              At a glance

              To drive and lead logistic relating improvement project to achieve cost, efficiency and quality target. Meanwhile, provide logistic solution to support company sustainabililty and growth.

              Work experience5+ years
              Job IDHRC0846054
              Start dateSep 01, 2024
              TypeFull time
              ContractPermanent

              Job description

              In your new role you will:

              • Design and optimize logistic solution to support company expansion and operational requirements.
              • Optimize operational logistic flow to drive for continuous improvement on cost, efficiency and quality.
              • Establish and maintain good government relations, especially with Customs officials to ensure a good communication flow.
              • Ensure logistic compliance with PRC state and local Customs/ESH laws and regulations.
              • Be responsible for all logistic relating internal and external audit.
              • Initiate and lead logistic related improvement projects to meet cost & efficiency target.
              • Develop and train logistic team to maintain the team’s competency.

              Your Profile

              You are best equipped for this task if you have:

              • Minimum Bachelor’s Degree above, with experience in Logistic area for 8 years, with multi-international companies background and semiconductor industry background is preferred.
              • Sound experience in bonded material control, customs regulations, warehouse management and project management in logistic function.
              • Able to evaluate complex situations and solve problems by using analytical thought.
              • A good team player with very positive thinking and passionate of continuous improvement.
              • Have been in people management position at least 5 years.
              • Very effective communication with both Chinese and English.
