Senior Client Executive Employee Benefits Large Group
Venbrook
Northern California - Bay Area

JOB SUMMARY:

The Senior Client Executive in the Employee Benefits department acts as an agent for Producer assigned accounts, managing a book of business and partnering with the service team. This role is responsible for servicing and solicitation activities, maintaining client relationships, and driving growth in the employee benefits sector.

 

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Manage all client-required services and maintain client relationships with minimal input from Producers.
  • Oversee a large book of business through the direction of employees on the service team.
  • Develop new business leads from Producers and identify cross-sell opportunities.
  • Serve as the primary point of contact for clients from initial lead through ongoing service.
  • Participate in team selling processes, including finalist interviews and RFP responses.

 

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent combination of education and experience
  • Ten (10) or more years of related work experience in employee benefits
  • Valid insurance license with ongoing commitment to meeting Continuing Education requirements.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

About Us

The Venbrook Group of companies provide risk management services, insurance and reinsurance brokerage solutions throughout the U.S. 

VENBROOK noun | \ven-'brúk\

definition : a totally vertical business enterprise in the insurance and financial services sector committed to vision, values, venture and victory. 

origin : the genesis of our brokerage business and the evolutions of its leadership.
