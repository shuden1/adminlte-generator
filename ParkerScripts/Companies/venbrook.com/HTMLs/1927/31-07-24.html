COMPANY OVERVIEW:

Venbrook Insurance Services is a national leader in insurance, reinsurance, risk brokering and management solutions. We cater to a national client base across myriad industries with divergent needs. Our team of experts and industry specialists’ partner with our clients to manage their risks, create security, promote growth, and add value by delivering best-in-class insurance products and programs. Venbrook is committed to ongoing product innovation, market agility, and strength in our partnerships for risk management solutions that allow our clients to focus on driving their business forward.

 

Venbrook specializes in a wide range of insurance and risk management products, including Property, Construction, Primary and Excess Liability, Directors & Officers/Management Liability, Professional Liability, Cyber Liability, Privacy, Crime, Employment Practices Liability, Workers’ Compensation, Employee Benefits, Transportation, Ocean, and Inland Marine, Personal Lines, and Surety Bonds.

 

Venbrook is headquartered in Woodland Hills, CA, with various locations throughout the country.

JOB SUMMARY:

Come and join an Employee Benefits Team that is on the move! As a Senior Client Executive in the Employee Benefits department, you will manage a $4 to $5 million book of business, including the Trust and approximately 15-20 other clients ranging from 50 to 2500 lives. You will have a support team of four to assist you, ensuring you are not managing alone. This role is responsible for servicing and solicitation activities, maintaining client relationships, and driving growth in the employee benefits sector.

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Manage all client-required services and maintain client relationships with minimal input from Producers.
  • Oversee a large book of business through the direction of employees on the service team.
  • Develop new business leads from Producers and identify cross-sell opportunities.
  • Serve as the primary point of contact for clients from initial lead through ongoing service.
  • Participate in team selling processes, including finalist interviews and RFP responses.
  • Prepare and present renewal analyses to clients.
  • Sell renewal business to existing clients and, where applicable, sell additional lines of coverage.
  • Promote other Venbrook services to clients through Producer Service Proposals.
  • Provide analysis and recommendations concerning clients' existing coverages.
  • Select carriers for solicitation and negotiate benefit and premium considerations.
  • Respond to client inquiries, including benefit and claim questions and issues.
  • Review client coverage contracts and amendments for accuracy.
  • Manage and negotiate third-party vendor relationships and contracts on behalf of clients.
  • Manage a dedicated service team and provide mentoring and coaching to departmental staff.
  • Serve as a technical expert, assisting department members in resolving complex issues.
  • Comply with agency management system data standards and maintain data integrity.
  • Other duties as assigned.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Ten (10) or more years of related work experience in employee benefits.
  • Valid insurance license with an ongoing commitment to meeting Continuing Education requirements.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Strong problem-solving and time-management abilities.
  • Demonstrated ability to work within a team and foster teamwork.
  • Exceptional planning, organizational, and prioritization skills.
  • Trust experience preferred.
  • Experience managing a book of business of similar size.
  • Detail-oriented with a strategic and forward-thinking mindset.
  • Willingness to put in long hours.
  • Northern California location preferred to facilitate monthly visits to the Trust.

This is a fantastic opportunity to join a dynamic and growing team, manage a significant book of business, and make a substantial impact on our clients and company. If you are a strategic thinker with a passion for employee benefits and client service, we want to hear from you!

Salary up to $150k

Venbrook Insurance Services, a national leader in insurance, reinsurance, risk brokering, and management solutions, is seeking an experienced Facilities Manager to join our dynamic team.
