Careers

Altinity is the first and most successful enterprise ClickHouse provider. Since 2017 we have helped hundreds of customers deliver analytics that change the way their users see the world. Our customers run from two-person startups to Fortune 50 companies.

We work with ClickHouse because we believe it’s the best analytic database on the planet. First open sourced in 2016, ClickHouse now has more contributors than Spark or ElasticSearch. Our software and services enable ClickHouse to reach its full potential in user applications. 

We value employees, open source, and always doing the right thing by customers. We love databases, but working for Altinity is far more than technology. We are fully distributed, with a remote team that spans half the globe: from California to Asia. We share knowledge within our team, with customers and with the open source community that makes ClickHouse great. 

We offer great jobs and excellent benefits.  We are database geeks, cloud visionaries, technical puzzle solvers, open source evangelists, and much more.  We are growing fast, so check out our positions and detailed benefits below.

If you think Altinity is the company for you, send a resume and cover letter describing why you fit in to applynow@altinity.com. We’ll be in touch!

Benefits

Opportunity to Push the Envelope

We work with advanced technology in databases, analytic applications, clouds, and open source. We don’t just use technology–we learn from our users and raise the bar in innovative new products. We offer opportunities to own problems and innovate in every area of our business: engineering, sales, marketing, customer support, and administration.

Work from Anywhere, Anytime

Enjoy the flexibility of working from literally wherever and whenever. Create a schedule that works for you and your family or lifestyle. Recharge your batteries with our open vacation policies. At Altinity, we are not your typical nine-to-five shop!

Open and Culturally Diverse

We love that we get to work with passionate people from around the world. Our team includes professionals from 12 different countries! We work with open source communities whose members come from virtually every country on Earth.

Career Development

We provide opportunities to learn new technologies as well as try out new roles. We also offer access to training on leading edge technologies, plus flexible work schedules for external education.

Excellent Benefits

Company Travel
