Dear User,

Welcome to Lingaro Group’s website. By entering our site (without making any changes to the default settings of your browser) you agree for us to use “cookies” and to store them on the end device which you have used to visit our website.
A cookie is a small file that holds certain data, which our website sends to your browser. Cookies do not collect any personal data stored on your computer’s hard drive.
Cookies allow us to better understand the users of our site and the way that our site is being used. Thanks to the collected statistics, we can adapt the content to your preferences.
We use two types of cookies:
- session - temporary files that are stored on the user's end device until logging out, leaving the website, or turning off the web browser;
- persistent - files that are stored on the user's end device for a specified time or until they are deleted.
We use cookies from trusted partners such as: Benhauer (Sales Manago), Google Inc. (Google Analytics and Google Adwords).
To enhance our website, we may sometimes embed video or other content from social media websites. You may receive cookies from these websites, however we have no control over them and do not take responsibility for these files.
Each user may agree or refuse to give their consent to installing and using cookies by choosing the appropriate settings in their web browser. More information on this topic can be found in the user support section of your browser.
