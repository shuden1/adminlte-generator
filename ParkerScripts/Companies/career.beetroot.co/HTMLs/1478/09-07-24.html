Stand with Ukraine!

For Beetroot, Ukraine is our home and our future. And today, together with all Ukrainians, we oppose the brutal Russian aggression. Don’t stand aside! Do not be indifferent to the bloody crime taking place in the very heart of Europe. You can help Ukraine.

Together with our partners, non-profit foundation Ioca Arma Nostra, we have created a humanitarian aid fund for Ukraine. Help save lives.
