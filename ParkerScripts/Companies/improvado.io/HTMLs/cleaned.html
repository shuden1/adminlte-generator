Our seven key principles

At Improvado, our best work follows seven key principles

1
Be result-oriented

We focus on outcomes, not routines and clearly define expectations, even when confronted with obstacles.

2
Be disciplined

We clear our minds and clean our inbox. We only meet when there's agenda, work in sprints, and are on time.

3
Be open

We are people, not job titles. We are friendly and supportive of each other, and we encourage an atmosphere where everyone feels comfortable and safe.

Be aware
