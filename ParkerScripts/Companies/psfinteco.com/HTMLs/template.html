Looking for a friendly workplace?

For the sixth time we have obtained the HR Certificate of the Highest Quality 2024 awarded by the Polish Association of Human Resources Managment (PSZK). It confirm that our company is a good, credible and reliable employer, using the latest soluntions in creating a friendly workplace, as well as a modern approach to the professional development area and employee satisfaction.

Benefits we offer:

  • Work in a close-knit team focused on cooperation, exchange of experience and creating a great atmosphere,
  • Getting experience in the FinTech industry or further developing and sharing knowledge in this area,
  • Medical care + dental package,
  • MultiSport for you and your family,
  • Various trainings and internal Academy for leaders,
  • Spending time together,
  • Gift cards for larger expenses,
  • Reward system,
  • Flexible working hours,
  • Telecommuting,
  • Opportunity to pursue passions within the framework of interest sections, both sports and e.g. board games,
  • PRO-employee competitions and actions,

and many more…

Our recruitment process

We realize that looking for your dream job can be stressful so we do our best to make our meeting make you smile.

What does the recruitment process look like in our company?
