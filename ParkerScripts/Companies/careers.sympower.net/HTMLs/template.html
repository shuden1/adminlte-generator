Our Mission

The climate crisis requires swift action from the entire world. That's why we are driven by the mission of empowering energy users to use their existing flexibility to take immediate action towards a sustainable energy system.

What We Do

Our Values

Achieving Together

We embrace each other's differences knowing that we are stronger together.

We build meaningful relationships by being kind and authentic.

We trust each other by default because we act with good intentions.

We gift and welcome feedback to help each other to grow.

We appreciate healthy conflict because it supports us to think differently.

We have fun and don’t take ourselves too seriously because we want to enjoy what we do.

Acting Responsibly

We act in the best interests of Sympower, with our customers and the environment in mind.

Everything we do, we do with honesty and integrity.

We take informed risks and see failure as an opportunity to be vulnerable and to learn.

We reflect on our behavior and how our actions contribute to our mission.

We speak up to provide relevant information towards decisions.

Focusing on Impact

We make informed decisions by combining data, our experience, and our intuition.

We prioritise based on impact knowing that we can’t do everything at once.

We choose progress over perfection because acting now is better than waiting for tomorrow.

We change course if we learn something new that challenges our expected outcomes.

We keep things simple because we know that reducing complexity will support us to scale.

Crossing Borders

With offices all over Europe, our team brings together their diverse experiences to share in the vision of a truly sustainable energy system. It's only by achieving together that we can create a carbon-free future.

Our Recruitment Process

Review

We review your application to screen for the position requirements.

Screening call

We invite you for a screening call to get to know each other.

Assignment

For certain roles we will send you an assignment or case study to assess your skills.

Interview

We invite you for one or two interviews with your future team lead and colleagues to assess competencies and values.

Reference Check

For leadership and commercial roles we ask you for a contact from your previous employer(s) and give them a call.

Offer

We send you an offer to join our team!
