Philanthropy

Utility Connect doesn't just help people move into their new homes & businesses but also loves to give back to the community when they can.

Tango Tab

With over 50 million people in America living under the shadow of food insecurity, Tango Tab was developed by Andre Angel to help impact the number of those in local communities who go without meals. Tango Tab 's "When You Eat, They Eat" promise helps solve the hunger epidemic by partnering with numerous restaurants nationwide to donate a meal to a person in need through a local food charity when "checking in" to the restaurant through the Tango Tab app. Also, the first Saturday of every month, Tango Tab sponsors a sandwich-making event to help "Feed the City" where volunteers meet at a sponsoring restaurant bringing meat, bread, & mustard. Event after event, thousands of sandwiches are made to help feed those in need in the community. We at Utility Connect are touched and inspired by Tango Tab's organization and volunteers on a monthly basis giving back to the community.

BvB Dallas

BvB Dallas is a yearly powder-puff football game geared to raise money for Alzheimer's research and awareness. In 8 years BvB Dallas has raised over $2,000,000 helping those afflicted by Alzheimer's disease. In 2016, Utility Connects Director of Business Development, Justin Sarmiento was selected as a coach of the "Blonde" Team and a fundraising advocate lead for BvB Dallas.

Positions

Concierge Agent

A Concierge Agent assists customer in setting up all home services & must be good at responding to customer inquiries, processing orders & routing calls to appropriate departments. Handles a high volume of calls, usually leveraging an auto dialer system. Performs data entry, maintains customer databases & logs calls. Excellent communication, customer service & alphanumeric data entry skills are required.

Transaction Coordinator

A transaction coordinator will help aid our business development teams get lunches & other events scheduled, as well as maintaining outside strategic partnerships by helping reps manage their accounts & assess any issue that might arise between our strategic partnerships.

Outside Business Development

An outside business development rep must find & create strategic partnerships with real estate professionals, homebuilders, locators, relocation companies, title companies, property management companies, leasing agents, & so on. You are in charge of researching companies & setting appointments! Your goal is to develop a strategic partnership by setting up luncheons, breakfasts, or even happy hour events where we can win them over. Your transaction coordinator will help you manage these relationships as you get them.

Internet Marketing Manager

An internet marketing manager will focus on assisting with the execution of the Utility Connect online marketing strategy including SEO/SEM, website and landing page maintenance, online analytics, & email marketing. The online marketing specialist will help implement marketing activities across a variety of online channels as well as assist with marketing support. The ideal candidate will be someone who works well both individually & as part of a team, and is highly motivated & results-driven.
