Philanthropy

Utility Connect doesn't just help people move into their new homes & businesses but also loves to give back to the community when they can.

Tango Tab

With over 50 million people in America living under the shadow of food insecurity, Tango Tab was developed by Andre Angel to help impact the number of those in local communities who go without meals. Tango Tab 's "When You Eat, They Eat" promise helps solve the hunger epidemic by partnering with numerous restaurants nationwide to donate a meal to a person in need through a local food charity when "checking in" to the restaurant through the Tango Tab app. Also, the first Saturday of every month, Tango Tab sponsors a sandwich-making event to help "Feed the City" where volunteers meet at a sponsoring restaurant bringing meat, bread, & mustard. Event after event, thousands of sandwiches are made to help feed those in need in the community. We at Utility Connect are touched and inspired by Tango Tab's organization and volunteers on a monthly basis giving back to the community.

BvB Dallas

BvB Dallas is a yearly powder-puff football game geared to raise money for Alzheimer's research and awareness. In 8 years BvB Dallas has raised over $2,000,000 helping those afflicted by Alzheimer's disease. In 2016, Utility Connects Director of Business Development, Justin Sarmiento was selected as a coach of the "Blonde" Team and a fundraising advocate lead for BvB Dallas.

Life at Utility Connect

Flexible PTO

We have recognized culture being the most important influence of our business model. We believe a rested mind is a sharp mind & a happy employee creates a happy customer. Our employees start earning paid time off on there very first day. In your first year alone, you can accumulate up to two weeks of PTO.

We love a competitive atmosphere in our office, so we hold daily contest to incentivize our staff to work hard. Food seems to be the key to their hearts, so we set certain daily goals and if they are met, we feed the staff. Monthly Bonus

We have put together a cool programs where we do a profit share based on company revenue goals. At the end of each month, the profit share earned is divided between the top-grossing team & top-grossing agent.
