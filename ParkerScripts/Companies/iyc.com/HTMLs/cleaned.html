Working at IYC

IYC is an international yachting company with over 130 passionate professionals based in 15 strategically located offices around the world. At IYC we believe in the power of a global team; we employee people from 23 countries and speak over 18 languages throughout our offices.

We understand that our people are our greatest strength and we strive to hire the best talent, whether it’s an experienced professional or a young graduate beginning his or her career. We believe that each individual can bring their unique set of experiences and we look for professionals that are driven and aspire to own impact by working on what they do best everyday.

IYC employs experienced professionals in all yachting disciplines including sales & purchase, chartering, marketing, yacht management, new building, charter management, crew placement, yacht agency services, and insurance & claims management.

Company Culture

Building your career at IYC is about being part of a dedicated, talented team of intelligent individuals who are empowered to make a difference in the marketplace.  We feel our team is our best asset. IYC is a place where honesty dominates and integrity is integrated into everything we do.  We take pride in our company and team, as well as the products and services we provide.

Career Development

At IYC we like to see people grow with the company and we strive to provide the tools that will help them do their personal best.
