OUR MISSION

PLACING POWERFUL MINDS IN ACTION

Atheneum is a leading global insights platform servicing various clients including distinguished strategy consulting firms, renowned investment houses and well known global corporations.

Our mission is to provide faster insights to empower our clients’ decision making. Through our AI capabilities we accelerate the decision-making of our clients by connecting them to the world's top professionals and industry leaders.

Atheneum is growing rapidly, with 500+ professionals in 11 offices across the US, South America, Europe, Middle East, and Asia.

We are proud to say that our people are from over 50 nations and we speak more than 50 languages. 

OUR VALUES

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Sustainability: Together we will strive towards cultivating  a meaningful future. We care about the mark we make on our world.

Entrepreneurship: Our vision combines powerful human thinking with innovative technology to create a winning formula. 

Excellence: Integrity and quality are prominent in all we do. We strive to realize and achieve our full potential.

Diversity: Equality in our difference is ingrained in our DNA. Diversity is not just part of our culture, it is what defines us.

Supportive Spirit: We are one global team, united in our shared mission to grow and carve the way for others to follow. We embark on this journey together, everyone has a voice and everyone will be heard.
