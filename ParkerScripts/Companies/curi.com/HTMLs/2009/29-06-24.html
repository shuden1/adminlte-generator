Where bettering lives is your life’s work

Our members rely on our committed, smart team to keep them successful in medicine, business, and life. If you want pride beyond a paycheck, find purpose in a career with Curi.

Come to work prepared
to make a difference

The most rewarding careers always have a greater purpose. Ours is to protect and invest in our physician members so they can focus on helping others. To do that, we only hire the best. From our receptionists to technologists, underwriters to claims attorneys, national-leading financial advisors to business analysts and marketing experts, everyone at Curi has a value and a say in how we operate every day. Ready for a rewarding career? See our current job openings.

Current Job Openings

  • Accountant-Practice Consulting

    Raleigh
    Curi Advisory
    We are looking for an analytical team player with a unique background in accounting and financial analysis roles within the healthcare industry. You will work on our Practice Consulting team within our Advisory division as an Accountant with our Practice Consulting team.  In this role, you will report to the Project Director and be responsible for preparing journal entries, reconciliations, financial statements, financial reporting packages, and related financial analyses for client practices. Financial analysis engagements support and help manage practice operations. Examples of analysis engagements are revenue cycle reviews, proformas for new service lines, and provider compensation modeling. Engagements can be requested by our clients and will likely include benchmarking based upon similar clients' results and/or national benchmarks. You will be responsible for presenting the analysis to members of the Practice Consulting team and to Clients. In addition, you will be responsible for responding to ad hoc report requests from senior management, clients, and other vendors.

  • Claims Consultant

    Raleigh
    Curi Insurance
    We’re looking for a dynamic team player to join our Claims Department as a Claims Examiner. In this role, you will help us coordinate the defense of our insured physicians. You will be responsible for investigating and managing claims, including, but not limited to, speaking with insureds and claimants, providing coverage analysis, assigning and working with defense counsel to ensure proper case management, establishing appropriate reserves, and negotiating resolution or issuing denials as appropriate for the Florida market. You are not required to live in Florida to perform the role's duties; however, you will need to be available to travel to Florida for meetings.

  • Claims Consultant

    Wisconsin | Indiana | Illinois
    Curi Insurance
    We’re looking for a focused and experienced individual to work in our Claims department as a Claims Consultant. Reporting to our Claims Team Lead, you will be responsible for managing cases from beginning to end using only best practices. You will work with our clients while also partnering with outside defense counsel. You will provide expertise in claims investigation & evaluation, claims negotiation & resolution, and manage the litigation process including the partnership with defense counsel.

  • Cyber Security Engineer

    Remote
    Curi
    We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Cyber Security Engineer with a primary focus on AWS security to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and managing security solutions to safeguard our cloud infrastructure on AWS. You will work closely with our security and engineering teams to ensure the security and compliance of our cloud environments (AWS, Azure and GCP).

  • Director, Engineering and Operations

    Remote
    Curi Management Company
    We are looking for a strategic leader with technical expertise in AWS to work in our IT Corporate department as a Director, IT Engineering and Operations. In this role, you will report to the VP, IT Corporate Technology and will be responsible for overseeing Curi’s infrastructure engineering and operations. This includes building new cloud solutions, modernizing legacy workloads, and automating maintenance. You will also focus on ensuring high availability, optimal performance, and scalability of IT systems to meet the growing needs of the organization.

  • People Business Partner

    Hybrid
    Curi Management Company
    We are looking for an experienced HR professional to work on our People Team as a People Business Partner. In this role, you will report to the Vice President, People and be responsible for providing outstanding service, consultation and support to the leaders and team members within their assigned business unit. In this role, you will work to understand business unit objectives and develop strategies to support people practices that enable leaders and team members to meet business objectives effectively.  In addition, you will work to proactively identify potential issues, resolve team member matters, coach and advise leaders, interpret policies and laws to mitigate risks and develop approaches to effectively execute the organization’s core HR processes and deliverables.

  • Senior Account Manager

    Remote
    Curi Management Company
    We’re looking for a customer focused individual to work in our Member Services Department as a Senior Account Manager. Reporting to the Director, Member Services. In this role, you will be the professional liability account manager for assigned large accounts and supporting Account Executives with their accounts in terms of customer retention, providing excellent customer service, account troubleshooting, relationship-management, and other activities to provide an excellent client experience as well as maintain a high-level client retention.

  • Underwriting Assistant

    Remote
    Curi Insurance
    We’re looking for a detail-oriented individual with excellent verbal and written communications skills to work in our Underwriting Department as an Underwriting Assistant. Reporting to the Director of Underwriting, you will be responsible for supporting the underwriters in all phases of the management of new and renewed professional liability policies, including, but not limited to, data entry and policy creation, processing policy changes, and providing direct customer service to both internal and external customers.
