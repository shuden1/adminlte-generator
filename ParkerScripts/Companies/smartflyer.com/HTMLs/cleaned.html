we are entrepreneurial self-starters

For more than three decades, we have built a team of curious travel professionals who share a passion for crafting personalized experiences on behalf of our clients. Smarties – as we endearingly call our SmartFlyer team – are propelled forward by an entrepreneurial spirit that drives our collective growth.

we are purpose-driven

Our purpose is to create lifelong memories for travelers and we want that to show up in everything we do – be it for our internal team, independent advisor community, or clients – to serve that greater mission. We believe that maximum impact comes from brands that tactfully enact the human with a seamless client journey. As a collaborative in-house team, we focus on fostering creativity and strategy to leverage each individual contributor’s strengths and support their development.

we are global

Our hybrid model with a New York City-based headquarters team and independent contractors around the world enables our team to do their best work. Our leadership team champions the importance of a truly flexible workplace that better suits both our people and our business.

we value diversity, equity + inclusion

We believe in making room for important conversations around diversity, equity, and inclusion. We want to make sure SmartFlyer is a place where everyone can succeed, wherever they are working from, regardless of background or identity.
