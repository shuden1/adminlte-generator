Career Opportunities

 

Your Career. Your Credit Union.

 

GreenState is seeking career-minded candidates with a desire to learn and build long-lasting relationships with our teammates and our membership. GreenState places great value in finding the right job "fit" not only for us, but for you! If you believe that a long-term employment relationship within a growing, fast-paced, and ever-changing environment is the right "fit" for you, consider GreenState as your future employer of choice.

 

Listed here are the current job openings within GreenState. Please click on the job title below for more information and to apply!

 

Please note: You will need to submit a separate job application for each position that you wish to apply for. We look forward to receiving your application!

If you are a current GreenState employee, please complete your application using the internal employee portal. Contact Human Resources for the web address if you do not have it handy. 

 

GreenState is an Equal Opportunity Employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, or protected veteran status and will not be discriminated against on the basis of disability.

 

If you are unable to complete our online employment application please call 1-800-397-3790 or email humanresources@greenstate.org for assistance.
