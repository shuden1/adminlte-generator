Serving our fleet customers since 1953

Tidewater Fleet Supply/TNT Parts is a full line fleet, truck, trailer and heavy equipment parts distributor. We work in both the private and the public sector and have helped Federal, State, and Local governments. This experience also extends to fleets and installers alike. We specialize in streamlining the supply chain and formatting an integrated supply demand for municipalities and large corporations. In 2016, Tidewater Fleet Supply acquired TNT Parts, based in Atlanta, GA. This allowed us to extend our distribution chain to serve customers from Richmond, VA to Orlando, FL. We are one of the largest independent aftermarket distributors across the Southeast. This geographic reach allows our 65 delivery vehicles to deliver to most of our customers on an hourly basis.

Employee story

Marvin started his career with TFS as a Delivery Driver in October 2005, worked his way up to Counter Sales, was further promoted to Manager of our City of Hampton Garage Parts Department, and later managed our Virginia Beach location.

 

As one of our ASE Parts Specialists, Marvin is well known for going the extra mile and locating those hard-to-find parts our customers need. He is now at our Norfolk Distribution Sales Counter. Marvin and his wife welcomed their 3rd child this year and he enjoys both fishing and hunting in his spare time.

Tidewater Fleet Supply/TNT Parts offers great growth potential. There aren’t many places these days where you can start at an entry level position and work up to a management position in a short period of time. I knew very little about the truck parts business when I started but, through training, working hard and learning from both employees and customers, I was able to work my way up to manager.

Marvin Davenport

Assistant Manager, Inside Sales Counter

TFS Norfolk, VA

Lee Anderson is currently the Area Branch Manager and oversees both the TNT Jacksonville and the TNT Savannah locations. He started with TNT Parts in 2004 at the age of 16 as a Warehouse Worker. He was then promoted to Warehouse Manager in 2006 and then to Counter Sales in 2008. As the sole contact for the Jacksonville route, and once we opened Jacksonville, Lee was promoted to Branch Manager when that store opened. In 2020 he accepted promotion to Area Branch Manager and was given the opportunity to manage both locations. We have recently opened an Orlando location which will also fall under his leadership.

Working for TNT Parts is the only job I’ve ever had. I have had nothing but opportunity for advancement for my entire tenure. The first couple of years after the merger were stressful but I am excited for where the company is headed. I believe there will be a lot of opportunities for everyone in the company who shows a strong work ethic and an eagerness to learn and grow.

Lee Anderson

Area Branch Manager

TNT Parts Jacksonville and Orlando

Larry Cuffee has been an employee of Tidewater Fleet Supply for more than 48 years. He was originally hired as a Delivery Driver and worked his way up to Warehouse Manager in Chesapeake. He handled all aspects of shipping, receiving, dispatching, stocking, and also the picking of parts.

 

Larry is currently working in our Norfolk Distribution Center and is one of our team’s most valuable assets, not just in tenure, but for his dedication, hard work, and positive attitude. Larry has been a great leader and has had many duties and responsibilities within warehouse and distribution operations. He always comes to work with a can-do approach and tries to make work fun. You can often hear his laughter throughout the day while working in the Distribution Center.

I was hired 48 years ago and Tidewater Fleet Supply has been a great employer to work for. I have always been treated fairly and with respect. I worked my way up from Delivery Driver to Warehouse Foreman and eventually, Warehouse Manager for one of our highest volume locations. I appreciate what the company has done for me and my family over the years.

Larry Cuffee

Management Assistant, Distribution Center

TFS Norfolk, VA

David Forrester was first employed part time with TNT Parts in 2010-2011 while still in high school. His job was boxing brake shoes kits before they came pre-boxed from the manufacturer. When he became a full-time employee in 2015, he started out order pulling. After doing that for a couple years, David took over inter-branch orders and transfers and started processing cores. In 2018, cores became a full-time job and was his focus until being promoted to Counter Sales in 2019 and then to Assistant Branch Manager of our Atlanta location in 2021.

I think TFS/TNT Parts is a good company because they have always taken care of me and treated me like family. They have shown me that if you are willing to put in the work and the time, there is room for growth. I tell everyone that I interview about my success story to motivate them and to show them that hard work truly pays off at Tidewater Fleet Supply/TNT Parts.

David Forrester

Assistant Branch Manager

TNT Parts Atlanta
