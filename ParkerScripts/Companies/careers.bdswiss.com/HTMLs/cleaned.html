About BDSwiss

BDSwiss is a leading financial services brand operating under a group of regulated entities whose mission is to deliver superior trading and investing solutions that are accessible to everyone. 

Since its inception back in 2012, BDSwiss has been providing bespoke online trading products, competitive pricing, and exceptional execution on more than 1000 underlying instruments including stocks and ETFs at DMA as well as a wide range of CFDs on forex, shares, commodities, and indices. 

BDSwiss complies with a strict regulatory framework and operates its services on a global scale under a number of different licenses and registrations.
