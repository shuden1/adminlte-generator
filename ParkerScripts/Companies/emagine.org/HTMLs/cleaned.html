We empower companies with high-end IT services

emagine is a high-end business, engineering & IT consulting company. Enabled by an extensive network of experts, emagine powers progress, solves challenges and delivers real results through tailored high-end consulting services. emagine is headquartered in Copenhagen with departments in Aarhus, Aalborg, France, Germany, Ireland, the Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Sweden, and the UK as well as India. Over the last couple of years, the company has grown extensively, increasing its international footprint to become one of the biggest consultancies in its segment in Europe. emagine A/S is owned by private equity company Axcel and a group of senior staff and board members.

Are you our new colleague?

At emagine, we value diversity immensely. Both when it comes to backgrounds and competences, but also in relation to personality and individual ambition. We are a diverse and well-rounded group made up of experienced specialists, generalists, recent graduates and self-taught people. We are generally curious, helpful and happy to share knowledge with our colleagues.

We are proud of our values that are based on honesty, kindness, helpfulness and solidarity. We nurture a strong community culture, where cooperation is paramount – not just locally but also across borders with our international departments. Only in unison can we provide the best service to our clients and affiliated consultants – and that is our goal. As an employee of emagine, you will experience a close-knit culture in which people stand side by side and where there is an emphasis on building close relationships. Going to work must be a fun and enriching experience yet still provide a challenge. That is why we take several steps to ensure that work is motivating and engaging. Finally, we also strive to create the right conditions for physical and mental wellbeing.

Our values

Acting on the same set of values

The essential tool in our daily interaction with clients, consultants, and colleagues is our ability to listen to our partners.

emagine's corporate values are the standards that guide how we act and behave as a company and as individual emagineers.

In every single moment.

Dedicated Expand

We are uncompromising in our dedication to delivering quality.

By fully utilizing our knowledge and expertise, we go the extra mile to deliver unparalleled services for our clients and consultants.

Responsible Expand

We are committed to not only supporting the goals of our clients but to foster a positive and equitable working relationship to our consultants and colleagues.

  We seek to reduce any possible negative impact of our business operations and to deliver a positive contribution to the wellbeing of the people around us.

Confident Expand

We trust our expertise makes a true impact with our clients and consultants.

  Being competent and professional is in our nature, and we insist on listening and asking questions, because it is key to solving real challenges.

Genuine Expand

We are honest, clear and direct with the sole intention to create the best possible dialogue.

  By creating a culture of openness and integrity, we build genuine and strong relations and partnerships, enabling us to deliver the optimal service and experience for our clients, consultants and colleagues.
