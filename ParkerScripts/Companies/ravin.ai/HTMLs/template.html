What it is like to work at Ravin

• Company events: tours, trips, gifts, celebrations, and a rich welfare & wellbeing program •
• Training programs - employees' personal and professional development •

Innovation

Surround yourself with brilliant and innovative colleagues in a cutting-edge technological environment. Be ready to truly be a part of something new and exciting.

Personal Growth

We understand the importance of personal growth, that’s why we offer flexible working hours/locations, training programs, company
tours, trips, celebrations and a rich welfare &
wellbeing program.

Education

Ravin AI invests hours of training, making sure our employees are proficient and up to date, whether in Technology or in Managerial skills. We make sure our employees get all training and professional courses for their personal and Professional development in their careers.

Facilitating Change

We're looking for you: a self-starter, open-minded individual that has promoted complex initiatives at larger organizations, has a strong bias towards action in ambiguous environments, and most importantly, wants to introduce Ravin’s technology to facilitate change.

A Global Culture

Working in Ravin AI will expose you to a multicultural environment, meeting and working with brilliant people from all over the globe, keeping a healthy balance between work and private life, and keeping a flexible work schedule.

New Offices

Ravin AI has new, modernly designed offices in Israel, Europe and USA, Working in one of our offices means working in a young, vivid environment, in prime locations in the cities, with the best restaurants, street food and cafe's all around. Such a wonderful way to combine work from a convenient new office with your daily life.

Why we do, what we do

Our mission is to create trust and transparency whenever a vehicle changes hands.
To do this, we start with our own community by creating an open, inclusive, and diverse organization across various offices around the world.

About Ravin AI: 

● Ravin.ai’s mission is to bring trust and efficiency wherever vehicles operate or change hands, by using computer vision AI technology to make vehicle inspections faster and more reliable. 

● Our technology allows vehicles to be scanned using smartphone or CCTV-style cameras and then automatically produces a vehicle condition report. ● We are a VC-backed Israeli tech startup with a globally distributed team of around 70 people, serving industry-leading players in the mobility space. ● We were featured in Time Magazine as one of the top 200 inventions of 2022. We’ve previously been featured in Forbes as one of the top machine-learning startups to watch. 

● We offer flexible hybrid working and would expect you to be in our Haifa office at least 2 days per week. 

‍

Main responsibilities: 

● We’re looking for someone who has a very strong UX background but also has a good eye for interface designs and can carry out user research. ● You will be expected to take ownership of the design output for Ravin’s mobile web application and desktop software. 

● You will be working closely with customers, product management, and business development to validate your thinking and optimise Ravin’s tools to different use cases. 

● You will also be working closely with the development teams (front end, backend and data science) and QA engineers to turn your ideas into reality. ● Your goal is to create a product experience that users love and that supports our mission to make vehicle inspections faster and more trustworthy. ● You must be ready to get stuck into detailed design work, to make sure your ideas are turned into products that our users love.


Skills and experience: 

● You must have proven experience designing complex software products (5 years minimum of design experience), working with dev teams to build them and then shipping them to real users. You should have seen multiple products / features through the full build lifecycle, from discovery to production. 

● You must have excellent communication skills and can articulate ideas to technical and non-technical audiences. You should be highly collaborative and should enjoy working in a close-knit team. 

● You should be comfortable working in a startup environment where change is constant and progress is rapid. You will be expected to work autonomously and take responsibility for the success of our products. You must demonstrate you are a proactive person and don’t need someone to always tell you what to do. 

● You should be comfortable working with distributed teams. You will be based in Israel but we also have team members across Europe and the US, who you will be working with day-to-day. 

● You should be willing to travel to carry out in-field customer research. ● It is a bonus if you have worked on B2B AI/ML/computer vision products and/or in the automotive sector before. 

● You should be proficient in modern design and prototyping tools (e.g. Figma, Adobe Suite, UserTesting.com). 

● Your day-to-day working languages will be English and Hebrew. If you can speak Russian that would be a bonus. 


Benefits: 

● We are offering you the opportunity to help shape our suite of products and own end-to-end design for specific flows and features.. 

● You’ll be working with a highly experienced and skilled team.. We move fast, iterate quickly and define success by the business outcomes, not the volume of output you produce. 

● This is an opportunity to work with cutting edge AI technologies in a fast-changing mobility sector. 

● We also offer 25 days paid holiday, alongside public holidays, a company pension scheme and an employee options scheme.
