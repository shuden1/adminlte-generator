Set Opportunity in Motion


If you thrive on challenge, are passionate about ideas and enjoy solving problems, we invite you to learn more about Lenox Advisors. We are always searching for talented individuals who enjoy working in a dynamic and energetic environment. Our talented employees come from diverse academic and professional backgrounds but share a common goal — a determination for helping our clients achieve their financial goals.


If you share these same attributes, enjoy a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment, then Lenox Advisors is the place for you.


Lenox Advisors is a certified great workplace by the independent analysts at Great Place to Work®. We earned this credential based on extensive ratings provided by its employees in anonymous surveys. “The ‘Great Place to Work’ survey is an example of our culture where candid, constructive feedback is welcome and addressed. This drives the continuous growth of our entire organization,” added Greg Large, Managing Partner.
