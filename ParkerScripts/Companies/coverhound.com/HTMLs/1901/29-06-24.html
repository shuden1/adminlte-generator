Who We Are

CyberPolicy is the only place where you can compare business insurance quotes AND buy multiple insurance policies online. You get fast, accurate, and actionable quotes based on your specific needs with competitive rates and coverages all in one place.  CoverHound and CyberPolicy (subsidiaries of Brown & Brown, Inc.) are sister companies operating at the forefront of the InsurTech space. Our unique technology offers a platform for businesses and consumers to easily compare and purchase insurance online. We deliver fast, accurate, and actionable rates while adding transparency to the insurance industry.
