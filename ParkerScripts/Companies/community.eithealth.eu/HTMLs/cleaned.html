Working Student - Corporate Communications (m/f/d)
EIT Health
Germany
Post an internship
Description
YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES

About the Position

EIT Health is seeking a highly motivated working student to join our Communications and Events team. This role presents an exciting opportunity to gain invaluable hands-one experience in both internal and external communications within a fast-paced healthcare innovation environment. The successful candidate will collaborate closely with all members of our Communications Team, reporting to our Senior Corporate Communications Manager. They will support various communication initiatives aimed at internal and external audiences.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in developing and implementing comprehensive internal communication strategies to engage EIT Health staff and stakeholders effectively.
  • Support in drafting compelling internal and external announcements, newsletters, and other communication materials.
  • Support in the management of internal communication channels, including the company intranet and email newsletters, ensuring timely and relevant content delivery.
  • Collaborate with others across the organsiaton (acrosss departments and regions) to gather and inform content and for internal and external communications.
  • Contribute to the planning and execution of external communication activities to enhance EIT Health's visibility and reputation including supporting social media management, community management, Wordpress website management, and media relations.
YOUR SKILLS & KNOWLEDGE
  • Currently enrolled as a full time student at a Germany university pursuing a degree in Communications, Marketing, Public Relations, or a related field.
  • Native level English essential – both spoken and written.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, with exceptional attention to detail.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office is required. Additional knowledge of Canva, Wordpresss, and social media platforms is a plus.
  • Excellent organisational and time management skills, with the ability to multitask and prioritise tasks effectively.
WHAT WE OFFER
  • We are a “Remote First” company, hiring in Germany
  • The possibility to join offices in various locations around Europe.
  • Your personal equipment
  • 30 days of vacation & flexible working hours
  • Straight forward internal communication, non-hierarchical structure, and freedom to operate with autonomy.
  • 20 hours per week during term time (to be worked Monday - Friday), with opportunity to work 40 hours per week during holidays 
  • Compensation: 13 euros per hour.
  • Contract duration: Initial 6-month contract, with possibility to extend
APPLICATION PROCESS
  • EIT Health applies a policy of equal opportunities and accepts applications without discrimination on any grounds.
  • Applications should consist of a cover letter and a full curriculum vitae. 
  • Applications via our careers page only
