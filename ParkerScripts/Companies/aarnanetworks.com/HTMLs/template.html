We’re looking for a DevRel manager to help us cultivate growth opportunities in the open source community. This person is responsible to help scale, execute, and refine our open source developer strategy. The DevRel Manager will be the company ambassador to all developer communities related to our core competencies and will focus on growing engagement with open source assets as a gateway to using our products. The key will be to provide value to the community by creating technical content (artifacts, code, blueprints, documentation, RFCs, proposals etc.) and how-to guides to facilitate onboarding to jointly identified open source projects. This person will engage with users at virtual events, on open source project community calls, and online forums. The DevRel manager will develop, track, and analyze community engagement metrics to make data-driven decisions, measure progress, and achieve substantial growth. 

The DevRel manager will provide guidance to the engineering team in terms of how to engage with the open source community by providing concrete feedback to developers on how to contribute code, proposals, documentation, videos, blogs in a way that enhances Aarna’s brand in the community. This person should be comfortable proposing new ideas/proposals at the governing board or technical steering committee level and not hesitate in taking controversial positions if needed.

Responsibilities 

  • Become the bridge between Marketing, Product, and Engineering 
  • Become an open source champion internally and externally
  • Join and participate in open source project communities important to Aarna
  • Create technical content (artifacts, code, blueprints, proposals etc.) to be open sourced
  • Create how-to content (documentation, videos, briefs, API-Guides, blogs. etc.)
  • Actively monitor and respond to requests for help from the community 
  • Actively monitor and engage with developers active in target open source projects
  • Develop, measure, analyze, and report on all relevant community metrics

‍Requirements

  • Hands-on open source developer with 4+ years experience with open source communities (e.g. Linux, K8s, Terraform) and working with APIs
  • Familiarity with networking technologies (SDN, NFV, VMs, VNFs, CNFs, CNAs, etc.)
  • Familiar with cloud computing, edge computing, and AI/ML
  • Excellent communicator (speaking and writing) and fluency in English
  • Enjoy connecting technical people with each other on common interests
  • Experience creating high-quality technical content creation (videos, slides, docs, etc.)
  • Deep familiarity with open source tooling (Git, GitHub, GitLab, ReadTheDocs, etc.) 
  • Ability to write code in common languages (Python, C++, Java, Ruby, etc.) 
  • Experience with developer channels and sites (Slack, StackOverflow, Discord, etc.)
  • Confidence to be an open source leader
  • Make connections between communities and understand what drives each community

Preferred:

  • 2+ years experience in a related role
  • Familiarity with HubSpot 
  • Experience coordinating developer events
  • Previous startup experience
  • Public speaking experience
