Facts, benefits, aspirations

Social & environmental initiatives

PixelPlex supports charity and philanthropy, practices donations, and aims to create opportunities. Our signature projects span sharing, green and decentralized economy.

Non-stop education

Continuously growing the team’s tech muscle, we organize hackathons, scholarships, internal meetups. On YouTube we dwell on the latest IT trends and explain the essentials.

Professional competitiveness

Being a part of the squad brings a great edge at the job market. We help improve skills by recompensing enhancement training, providing tech literature, workshops, and pay rises.

We take care to provide virus-free workspaces and allow remote jobs. Additionally, we pay health insurance, vaccination, gym, and sick leaves, while offering partnership discounts.

PixelPlex team composition is 55% guys, 45% ladies. We respect diversity and equally value each member of the squad. Speaking of which — the team broke 100+ pixelplexians!
