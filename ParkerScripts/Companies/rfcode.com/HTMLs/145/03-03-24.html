      Make an Impact in Real-time with RF CODE!

      There are plenty of places to ”work.” At RF Code, the opportunities to make an impact are endless. Whether you’re a developer, sales representative, support representative, or in our manufacturing department, you have the direct ability to contribute to something great every day. We’re always looking to meet new talent, so if you don’t see a role of interest at the current time, please reach out to us here to connect with our recruiting team.

      Opportunities at RF Code

      Sales Operations

      We are looking for a collaborative, energetic, and results-driven Sales Operations professional, with experience in supporting a wide variety of sales-related responsibilities. This impactful role is perfect for someone who is inquisitive, detail-oriented, organized, able to learn on the fly, quick with decision-making, and comfortable working through both strategic and tactical projects simultaneously.

      Responsibilities

      Credit Check for New Customers

      • Owns Dun and Bradstreet Reporting and summarizing of data.
      • Ensure cross-functional engagement through interactions with other departments.

      New Account Creation

      • Owns the process of validating new client data for accuracy.
      • Generate Records in the order management system.

      Subscription Notification Management

      • Engage with customers to provide upcoming payment details.
      • Update the original order in the order management system with the acquired information.
      • Performs reporting analysis.

      License Key Request

      • Conduct account analytics.
      • Ensure cross-functional engagement. through interactions with other departments.

      Hardware Order Processing

      • Perform data validation and order entry with accuracy.
      • Responsible for obtaining credit card details from customers, as necessary.
      • Ensure cross-functional engagement through interactions with other departments.

      Additional Responsibilities

      • Become an SME and trusted advisor within the Company's CRM, supporting the team with day-to-day inquiries.
      • Assist our Sales team to administer and evaluate new sales efficiency tools.
      • Continually explore new ways that we can expand our business, and work to implement recommendations.
      • Ensures positive client and customer engagement experience throughout every interaction.

      Qualifications

      • 3-5 years of sales operations experience in a fast-paced environment.
      • Ability to understand and adhere to contractual obligations with customers.
      • Exceptional communication, analytical, and problem-solving skills.
      • Attention to detail, with an eye for streamlining tasks.
      • Demonstrated ability to communicate clearly and effectively to a variety of business partners, motivating and influencing decision-makers.
      • Degree required.
      • Self-driven work style, able to manage multiple projects with little oversight, and continually excited to bring new ideas to the table.
      • Proficient with Salesforce, MS Office Suite, and Order Management. NetSuite experience is a plus.

      Purchasing Manager

      We are seeking an experienced and motivated Purchasing Manager to join our team at RF Code in Austin, TX. The Purchasing Manager will play a crucial role in managing our procurement processes, ensuring timely and cost-effective acquisition of materials and supplies necessary for the smooth operation of our business. The ideal candidate will also be responsible for identifying and fostering a relationship with our suppliers and vendors.

      Responsibilities


      Procurement

      • Monitor product availability to make informed decisions about purchasing strategies.
      • Develop effective procurement strategies that align with company goals and budget constraints.
      • Coordinate with various departments to understand their purchasing needs and requirements.
      • Work closely with cross-functional teams, including engineering, operations, finance, and warehouse, to streamline procurement processes and resolve any issues that arise.

      Vendor Management

      • Establish relationships with suppliers, negotiating favorable terms and pricing agreements.
      • Develop a supply and production plan to ensure timely delivery to our customers.
      • Ensure suppliers deliver quality products, and manage failure root cause analysis by working with manufacturing and engineering.

      Inventory Control

      • Maintain optimal inventory levels by tracking usage patterns, demand forecasts, and lead times.
      • Implement inventory control measures to prevent overstocking or stockouts while minimizing carrying costs.
      • Develop cost-effective purchasing strategies that balance quality and value.
      • Identify opportunities for cost savings through negotiation, bulk purchases, and supplier relationships.
      • Monitor and report inventory levels.

      Purchase Order Management

      • Create and manage purchase orders ensuring compliance with internal policies.
      • Monitor order status and resolve any discrepancies or delays in collaboration with suppliers and internal stakeholders.

      Qualifications

      • 5-10 years of purchasing experience - preferably in the electronics industry.
      • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
      • ERP experience - preferably NetSuite.
      • Familiarity with PLM software - preferably Autodesk.
      • Ability to meet with Southeast Asia on occasion, potentially after hours during Asia's workday.
