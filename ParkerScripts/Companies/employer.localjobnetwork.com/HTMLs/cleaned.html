Company Overview

Since 2006, John Marshall Bank has been providing businesses and individuals with the right balance of personalized, accessible, and exceptional service with the latest banking technology. As a commercially focused community bank headquartered in Reston, VA, and with eight branches throughout the DC Metro area, we serve our customers with local knowledge and expertise. Our success is driven by what sets us apart: our team. Our culture of ownership and innovation promotes the growth of our employees. At JMB, you’ll have the flexibility to build a fulfilling career and choose where you want to make an impact. Sales, service, support, or management – we’re always looking for business-minded individuals with a drive to advance in their profession. John Marshall Bank Highlights  

  • Earned 5-Star highest rating for financial strength by Bauer Financial
  • Ranked in the 2020 Deposit Accounts Top 200 list of Healthiest Banks in the United States by Lending Tree
  • Top bank with asset size between $1 billion to $5 billion and the second best bank across all asset size categories in Bank Director’s 2023 RankingBanking study
  • Tenured executive management team with extensive experience serving the DC Metro market

Find Your Place in JMB We pride ourselves on our commitment to the community, both professionally as bankers but also as active members on the Boards of a variety of nonprofit and professional organizations we serve. With branch locations in Alexandria, Arlington, Washington DC, Loudoun, Prince William County, Reston, Rockville, and Tysons, there is a place for you on the JMB team. Attractive Benefits We offer competitive benefits that reflects our appreciation for our employees’ commitment. As a member of the JMB team, you’ll have access to an appealing health benefits package, a company match on your 401(k), Employee Assistance Programs, generous vacation time, maternity leave, and much more. Join the JMB team today and begin building a career of success.
