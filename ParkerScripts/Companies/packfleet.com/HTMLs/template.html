LETTER FROM THE CO-FOUNDER

Why deliveries?

Everyone has a horror story of receiving a delivery and it not going to plan. Its a universal experience and delivery companies have attracted an awful reputation as a result.

The industry has become a race to the bottom, and if deliveries cost less that saving has to come from somewhere. Demotivated drivers chuck parcels over the fence or leave little bits of paper saying sorry we missed you, customer queries skyrocket and those cheap deliveries quickly become expensive. All while customers keep asking

Wheres my package?!

Thats why we started Packfleet. Not to be the cheapest, but to be the best. Were here to make deliveries delightful, to raise the bar in an industry that so desperately needs it.

What are we focused on?

We constantly look at how things are done now and ask: why?. Without the weight of legacy holding us back we've been able to start from scratch and build something with an experience that truly delights.

This has led us to designing and building so much in house. From what recipients see when they get a delivery, to the app our drivers use, the custom algorithms powering our routing software, and everything in between.

In doing so weve built a team that deeply cares about being:

Technology first

Customer obsessed

Planet conscious

And that team is always growing.

Thats why we are looking for talented people who share our passion for crafting something new, something delightful, something that breaks everyones notion of a delivery. Could that person be you?
