About Us

Our 46k sq ft distribution center and offices are headquartered in Jacksonville, Florida where we have many fantastic job opportunities for the right candidates. If you are a motivated, self-starter who is highly organized and thrives in a fast-paced environment, we need you on our team.

Send us your resume, we would love to hear from you!

PCLiquidations is a reseller of telecom equipment, computers, laptops, point of sale hardware, and networking gear. Founded in 1991 we now play a significant role in recycling computers and returning IT hardware back into the marketplace. With a vision of innovation and excellence for our customers and employees we have kept millions of pounds of electronic equipment out of landfills.
