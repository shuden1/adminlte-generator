Employment at John Foy & Associates

We’re looking for top-notch attorneys and paralegals that make client relationships the most important part of each case. We’re a tough Georgia personal injury firm that works hard to serve our clients. Are you ready to fight with us?

We have an enthusiastic, energetic support staff with good camaraderie. Our office is in the heart of Atlanta’s trendy Buckhead district with lots of restaurants, shopping and entertainment. Our goal is to work hard and serve our clients. At the same time, our office likes to have fun, taking in an occasional Braves game or competing in Fantasy Football.

Benefits include 401K plan, medical insurance and free parking.

If you’re interested in applying for any of these positions, send resume and cover letter to jobs@johnfoy.com.

Current Openings

Pre-Litigation Associate Attorney

John Foy & Associates is a high-paced Personal Injury Law Firm that strives to deliver the best customer service to our clients in their time of need. John Foy & Associates seeks a self-motivated, results-driven, detailed plaintiff’s attorney. Qualified candidates will have strong academic credentials and 3+ years experience in pre-litigation personal injury and car accident cases.

The right applicant should have a demonstrated interest in personal injury/insurance law, strong analytical skills and exceptional communication talent. This attorney will be aggressive on behalf of their clients with solid negotiating skills and the ability to handle a case load of their own. John Foy & Associates offers a professional and friendly work environment, a robust assistant team and a competitive compensation and benefits package.

Juris Doctorate and in good standing with the Georgia Bar Association required. Bilingual in Spanish a plus. Send resume and cover letter to jobs@johnfoy.com.

Personal Injury Call Center Rep

Personal Injury Law Firm John Foy & Associates is seeking an Intake Specialist. This role will be a part of the initial contact team for our potential clients. Great phone skills, sales ability, professionalism and compassion for the client a must.

Job Duties include, but are not limited to: Answering telephone calls, verifying information, and showing empathy to potential clients. Skills and Qualifications: Verbal Communication, Phone Skills, Listening Techniques, Data Entry Skills, People Skills, Informing, Customer Focus, Customer Service, Attention to Detail, Professionalism, Multi-tasking, Time Management, Relationship Building, Overcoming Objections.

Must have a minimum of 3 to 5 years customer care and call center experience. College degree preferred but not required. Bilingual and Personal Injury Law knowledge is a plus. Send resume and cover letter to jobs@johnfoy.com.

Legal Assistant

Personal injury law firm John Foy & Associates is seeking quality, dynamic, can-do candidates to join our team. Legal experience necessary and knowledge of Personal Injury Law strongly desired. Qualities and skills that are required for this position include the ability to learn quickly, great work ethic, computer literate, skilled at multi-tasking, detail driven and an excellent attitude.

You are motivated by the desire to educate and communicate with our clients with compassion and professionalism. Certain positions require a Georgia Paralegal certificate. Knowledge of Needles and/or Total Office CRM systems a plus. Bilingual in Spanish a plus. College degree preferred. Send resume, salary expectations and cover jobs@johnfoy.com.

For all job applicants:

  • Reference the position for which you are applying.
  • Principals only. Recruiters please do not contact this job poster.
  • Please do NOT contact us with unsolicited services or offers.
