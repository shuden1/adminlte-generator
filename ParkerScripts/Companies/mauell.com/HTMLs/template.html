Careers

Come work with us.

Mauell Corporation is an equal opportunity employer that specializes in designing and building critical command and control room environments. We lead the North American market as the most trusted single source for purpose-built control rooms, and customers seek us out for our unparalleled expertise.

High-quality technological competence and extensive experience are at the foundation of our success. We treat colleagues, customers, the community, and the environment with care and consideration. Our employees love working here due to our focus on excellence, collaboration, and continuous improvement. This working environment encourages and supports employee growth.

Our employees enjoy a wide range of benefits, including:

  • Pleasant working environment
  • Excellent pay
  • Flexible working environment
  • Medical, dental, and vision insurance
  • Paid time off
  • Employee assistance program
  • Voluntary term life and AD&D insurance
  • Accident Insurance and specified critical illness insurance
  • 401(k) individual retirement plan 
  • Continuous training opportunities 
  • Tuition reimbursement program
