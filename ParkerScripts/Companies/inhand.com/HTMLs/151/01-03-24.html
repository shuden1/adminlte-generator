Our Values

Rationality

Celebrating reason and facts, our culture thrives on data-driven insights and logical, evidence-based decisions.

Inclusion

Diverse voices unite here, fostering an inclusive culture that welcomes all perspectives and backgrounds, driving excellence.

Innovation

Fueling progress through relentless innovation, we transform ideas into solutions, shaping a dynamic and forward-thinking culture.

Building a World with Everything Conected

Unleashing the power of connectivity, together we run the marathon of innovation. With a steadfast dedication to uplifting humanity, we embark on this enduring journey to triumph and shape a connected world.

Build a global network

At InHand Networks, we are dedicated to crafting industry-leading connectivity solutions and communication devices. Each product is a testament to the talent and hard work of our team members. With a united spirit and relentless pursuit of excellence, we believe in building a world where everything is interconnected.

Sales

Sales Manager       Virginia, US
Responsibilities
1. Provide technical and business knowledge of InHand’s industrial IoT solutions to the sales team;

2. Understand customer needs and requirements, and collaborate with the sales team to develop and propose tailored solutions;

3. Conduct product demonstrations, presentations, and webinars to illustrate the benefits and unique selling points of our IoT solutions;

4. Develop strong relationships with machine makers, system integrators, and channels, understanding their business goals and demonstrating how our solutions can help achieve them;

5. Work closely with the product team to provide feedback and insights from customers, aiding in future product development;

6. Prepare and deliver technical proposals, product updates, and special technical reports;

7. Participate in industry events, conferences, and exhibitions to maintain a comprehensive understanding of market trends and competitor activities.

Qualifications
1. Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field;

2. 5+ years of experience in sales, pre-sales, or a similar customer-facing role;

3. Deep understanding of industrial IoT, including AI, machine learning, and related technologies;

4. Demonstrated ability to explain complex technical concepts in a clear, understandable way;

5. Proven ability to build and maintain strong relationships with a diverse set of internal and external stakeholders including senior-level executives, legal, technical, finance, sales, and marketing experts;

6. Excellent presentation, communication, and negotiation skills;

7. Willingness to travel as required;

8. Bilingual English and Mandarin Chinese will be a strong plus.

Marketing

Marketing Specialist       Virginia, US
Responsibilities
1. Develop and implement marketing strategies to drive sales and increase brand awareness;
2. Conduct market research and analyze data to identify trends and opportunities;
3. Manage advertising campaigns across various channels, including Google Ads and print advertising;
4. Create compelling copy for marketing materials, including website content, social media posts, and email campaigns;
 
5. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to coordinate product launches and promotions;
 
6. Monitor and optimize marketing campaigns using analytics tools to maximize performance;
 
7. Assist with budgeting and forecasting for marketing initiatives;
 
8. Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in performance marketing and e-commerce.
Qualifications
1. Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or a related field;
2. Proven experience in marketing, preferably in a similar role;
3. Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret data and make data-driven decisions
4. Proficiency in Google Analytics and other marketing analytics tools;
5. Excellent written and verbal communication skills
6. Ability to multitask and prioritize projects effectively
7. Knowledge of budgeting principles and experience managing marketing budgets
8. Familiarity with product management concepts is a plus.
