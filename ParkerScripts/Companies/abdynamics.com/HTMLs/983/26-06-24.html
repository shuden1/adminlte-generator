Who we are

Driving innovation, together

AB Dynamics is one of the world’s leading providers of automotive testing and development solutions. We pride ourselves in developing the most innovative and reliable testing products on the market today for our customers, which include all the major automotive brands, high tech autonomous vehicle developers, motorsport racing teams, and global test facilities.

It’s our people that are the key to our success; their passion and energy is what drives our business forward.

Our values

Customers

We create valuable partnerships with our customers through collaboration to understand and deliver their requirements.

People

We empower people by supporting and challenging each other to thrive. Integrity and respect are at the forefront of everything we do.

Diversity

We recognise the importance of strengthening, improving, and enriching our culture and practices through diverse opinions, skills and people.

Innovation

We inspire creativity by giving people the space to challenge the ‘now’ and engineer for the future.

Excellence

We are never satisfied with the status quo. We invest in our people, products and processes by encouraging learning and self-enrichment to deliver world class services and products to our customers.

Responsibility

We enable a culture of personal ownership and commitment to ourselves, our customers, our shareholders, and the environment. We are always looking for opportunities to improve the sustainability of our operations.

Diversity and inclusion

We’re committed to fostering an inclusive culture that accelerates the development of safer, more efficient vehicles with less environmental impact. Our partnerships with the Royal Academy of Engineering and their GEEP programme, as well as our participation in an inclusive leadership pilot initiative, aim to support UK engineering students from under-represented backgrounds and embed inclusive practices across the sector.

Passionate about inspiring future engineers, especially those from underrepresented groups, we collaborate with national and local organisations like the Women’s Engineering Society, Mighty Girls, Formula Student, and Arkwright of the Smallpeice Trust to mentor young engineering enthusiasts.

Part of a wider Group

We’re proud to be a truly international company and part of AB Dynamics plc.

FAQs

How do I apply for a role at AB Dynamics?

Visit our live vacancies here. If you identify a suitable vacancy that meets your skills and experience, apply with your CV highlighting why you’re a great match.

Are you accepting speculative CVs?

We welcome speculative applications from candidates with a proven interest in our areas of operation. To submit a speculative application, visit our latest vacancies page.

Do you sponsor visas?

We can sponsor visas. If you do not have the right to work in the UK, please still apply for any roles that you may be interested in.

What is the AB Dynamics recruitment process?

If you see a role on our vacancies page that you like the look of you can apply for the role directly through the website, attaching your CV. All applicants will receive an automatic email confirming receipt of their application. If successful you will be invited to an initial phone call to discuss your application further. All candidates are advised of their progress regardless of whether they’re successful or not in their application and are able to view a live status through our recruitment platform.

How do I apply for work experience?

We currently work with two schools local to our Bradford on Avon UK office to host work experience opportunities for year 10 and 12 students – if you’re a student at St Laurence or Melksham Oak, please discuss opportunities with your career’s teams. In addition, we periodically host work experience weeks, and applications will be open on our vacancies page.

 Do you have a graduate training scheme?

We have created a structured graduate scheme where successful applicants spend their two years rotating through various teams, embedding in projects to obtain a solid platform for their career. We advertise our placement and graduate opportunities in the winter, so keep an eye on our vacancies page from December through to February.
