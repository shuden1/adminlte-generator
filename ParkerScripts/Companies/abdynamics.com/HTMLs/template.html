AB Dynamics is one of the world's leading specialists in automotive test systems

We develop cutting-edge products and solutions for our customers which include all the major automotive brands, high-tech autonomous vehicle developers, motorsport racing teams, and global test facilities.

About the AB Dynamics group

AB Dynamics is an international group of companies with locations in the UK (Wiltshire and Hampshire), Germany (Gießen and Munich), USA (California and Michigan) and Japan (Yokohama). The technologies we develop are making a difference in the automotive industry to create safer vehicles.

Click here to find out more about us and our history.

It's the people at AB Dynamics that are key to our success; their passion and energy is what drives the company forward.

We're looking for exceptional people who are passionate about what they do.

Why work for us?

We're an innovative, dynamic company in one of the most interesting areas of automotive engineering. We create industry-leading solutions to solve the most complex customer project needs.

At our UK company we offer the following benefits:

  • Competitive salary
  • Discretionary bonus
  • 25 days holiday (increases with long service) + bank holidays
  • Private healthcare
  • Competitive company pension scheme - with an employee match of up to 10% of annual basic salary
  • Life assurance
  • Up to 40% off an EV through our Electric Dreams Scheme
  • Income protection
  • Social events calendar
  • Employee Assistance Programme


We are committed to supporting the future of engineering, we do this by working closely with Women in Engineering (WES), STEMWomen, Team Bath Racing Electric and The Smallpeice Trust.

Graduates, apprenticeships, placement schemes and work experience

At AB Dynamics there are plenty of opportunities for those starting out in their career or looking to strengthen their academic studies with hands-on experience.

We partner with local schools, colleges and universities, offering interesting and rewarding apprenticeships, placement schemes and work experience. Get in touch with us today to find out more.

How to apply

Please send your CV, covering letter and salary requirements, quoting the job you are applying for to: careers@abdynamics.com.

Due to the high volume of applications, we are unable to acknowledge all of these. We will be in touch within four weeks of your application if we wish to pursue it further.

We welcome speculative applications from candidates with a proven interest in our areas of operation.
