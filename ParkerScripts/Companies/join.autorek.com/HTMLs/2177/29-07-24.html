Benefits

We work hard, but we also know you have a family, friends, hobbies, and a life outside the office. That’s why we offer a great benefits package, not just a competitive salary.

Generous holidays

Everyone needs a healthy, happy, and productive life. This means you'll need time to rest and recover with a generous annual holiday allowance. 

Amazing office spaces

Our offices are all about being a great place for our teams to work at their best.  The spaces are created with collaboration in mind, as we know work isn't about being at a desk all day.  The great coffee helps too. 

Health and wellbeing

Generous private health care, discounted gym memberships, yoga, massages  and mindfulness sessions are just some of the ways we help you to prioritise your wellbeing.

Learn and grow

We give access to the best tools and training to help our team learn new skills and thrive in their career. 

A team that cares

You’ll be joining a team of smart, capable, helpful people that want you to win, our clients to be wowed, and our business to grow.

Birthday off

Who wants to work on their birthday? It is your day to be with family, friends and celebrate. Everyone at AutoRek gets to take their birthday off. 

We invest time and resource into ensuring we develop in ways that benefits our team, our clients and builds on our success.

Digital data and financial transaction volumes are growing exponentially, this combined with increasing regulatory obligations is driving the demand for improved efficiencies, better control, and management of risk across the financial services industry.  

Our focus is to ensure the AutoRek platform continues to meet these ever changing demands and remain a leading player in the market.  We do this by having a dedicated and passionate team in place.
