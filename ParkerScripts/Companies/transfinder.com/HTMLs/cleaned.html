Human Resources Manager

 

The HR Manager will play a critical role in shaping and executing HR strategies that align with our business objectives and support our employees' growth and development.

  • HR Strategy Development: Develop and implement HR strategies, policies, and initiatives aligned with the company's overall objectives and values.
  • HR Compliance: Ensure compliance with labor regulations and company policies, and keep abreast of changes in employment laws.
  • Employee Relations: Act as a trusted advisor to management and employees, providing guidance on employee relations issues, conflict resolution, and performance management.
  • Performance Management: Develop and implement performance management processes, including goal setting, performance reviews, and career development plans.
  • Compensation and Benefits: Provide leadership guidance on compensation and benefits programs to ensure competitiveness and alignment with industry standards and employee needs.
  • Proven experience (5+ years) in a HR leadership role, within the software/technology industry.
  • Strong knowledge of HR best practices, employment laws, and regulations.
  • Experience in talent acquisition, performance management, employee relations, and organizational development.
  • Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively across all levels of the organization.
  • Strategic thinker with the ability to translate business needs into HR initiatives and programs.
