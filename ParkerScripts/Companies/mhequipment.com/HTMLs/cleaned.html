Company Culture

The MH Way

Company culture is the "personality” and atmosphere that defines the environment in which all employees work. Every business has one no matter the size. Company culture includes a variety of things from employee demographics to the company’s mission, vision, and values.

Each location is part of one company but a part of many communities. Our dedication to helping our communities has always been a major focus for MH Equipment. We encourage our employees to take this focus to heart and help the communities we call home through excellent service in the work place and out in the community.

At MH Equipment, we understand in the world of material handling there is a continued state of change and raising of the bar for performance. We are continually growing and adding new locations. Every year we add more and more employees to the MH family cross the Midwest.

Why choose MH

We honor and respect all people. We strengthen our Company by disciplined effort to pursue effective relationships and implement sound business practices that ensure the long-term well-being of the Company and employees through programs such as:

Medical, dental, vision, life, STD, LTD, EAP, FSA, HSA. We offer one of the industry’s best and most comprehensive benefits packages to ensure they and their families are protected.

Adoption Assistance, Wellness, Pet Insurance, Money Management and Financial Planning, Weight Management, Marriage Conferences, Vendor Discount programs, Travel Assistance, and more. We offer many unique benefits to support and enrich the lives of our employees.

We invest in our Communities for a positive impact by coming alongside our employees’ passions with support and financial assistance through our His First Foundation charitable program.

Paid Charity Time Off and His First Foundation are two of the ways we support our employees’ passions.
