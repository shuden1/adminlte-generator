What are cookies? Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behaviour information. When you visit our Website, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

How do we use cookies? Shift uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our Website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in;
  • Understanding how you use our Website;
  • Personalizing content.

What types of cookies do we use? There are a number of different types of cookies.  This Website uses cookies and stores cookies on your computer or device. These cookies are used to help us collect information about how you use our Website and enable us to remember you. Our Website uses the following cookies:  

  • Functionality – Shift uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our Website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in.  A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.
  • Advertising – Shift uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to our Website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address.
  • Statistical cookies: Shift uses these cookies to analyse the behaviour of visitors on the Website. The cookies allow us to keep track of the number of visitors and to see which parts of our Website are popular.
  • Targeting cookies: Shift uses these cookies to build a profile of our Website visitors. It is based on your surfing behavior on our Website, after which we may contact you by telephone, e-mail or an advert based on the interests you have shown in order to offer you Shift services or Content (like blogs, Ebooks, whitepapers etc.).

How to manage cookies. You may set your browser not to accept cookies, and the Website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our Website features may not function as a result.  The law states that we may store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies we need your permission.  At any time you may change or withdraw your consent from the Cookie Declaration on our website.  Should you contact us to change or withdraw your consent, please state your consent ID if available.

WORK@SHIFT

We’re on a mission to use AI to unlock the future of insurance decision making. Will you join us?

See Open Roles  

 

Shift is a technology-first organization made up of smart, creative team members from diverse backgrounds and locations. Our fast-growing team is changing the way insurance works with AI.

From Data Science and Project Management to Sales, Marketing, IT and Operations, we’re looking for passionate teammates who are willing to do whatever it takes to make our clients successful using the power of Artificial Intelligence.

See Open Roles  

Purpose

To free insurers to be there for more customers in more ways than ever before.
We think big. And we think insurance is an industry that’s ripe for transformation. The right technology can unlock new ways of doing business for insurers–which in turn creates new ways for customers to benefit from insurance.

Vision

An insurance industry in which better decisions lead to amazing opportunities and outcomes.
When it comes to making insurance decisions, Insurers must meet ever-increasing expectations for speed, accuracy, fairness and transparency. Shift helps insurers succeed in all areas, creating new opportunities for insurers and their customers.

Mission

To imagine and invent solutions that transform insurance decisions for the benefit of all.
Insurance is a force for good in the world, especially when it’s designed to meet the needs of today’s customers. We unlock the good in insurance by using AI to automate and optimize decision making, so insurance decisions are fast, accurate, fair, and transparent.

Our Values

We trust talent.

We value experience and potential. Whether you’re a recent graduate or a mid-career professional, we’re interested in what you’ve done, but even more interested in what you will do to contribute to the future of insurance with Shift.

We are stronger together.

We’re a team with diverse backgrounds, experiences, and stories. We count on each other to help solve difficult problems and deliver the best for our clients–together.

We focus on what matters to our customers.

Big ideas are great. Big ideas that contribute to achieving big goals are even better. We focus on doing what it takes to enable our customers to serve their customers better. Because that’s when everybody wins.

We make it happen.

The (not so) secret ingredient for Shift’s incredible growth and success? It’s our commitment to our teammates, our customers, and ourselves to go above and beyond–in large and small ways–to move the insurance industry forward.
