Our mission is

To be the world’s largest ground travel marketplace by powering great rides and guiding travelers throughout their journeys.

The values that drive us

  • Going places

    Our customers are going places. We are, too. We’re excited to help redefine the future of the travel industry.

  • Sustainable

    We promote efficient travel that helps travelers reduce their environmental impact. We also embrace sustainability in our workplace and community.

  • Independent spirit

    We cultivate our curiosity, share new knowledge, and move along the path of continuous learning.

  • Super-helpful

    Helpfulness is a core part of our mission. Our team members help each other succeed through collaboration.

Culture makes the difference

We’re passionate about travel and connection, and we thrive on a culture of openness, continuous improvement, and mutual respect.

How we approach our work is important: we try to stay humble, knowing the right answer isn’t always the obvious one.

Cultural diversity matters. Part of the Diversity Type initiative, our design team created posters promoting cultural diversity.

Diversity

As a company with a globally distributed team, we value diversity, inclusion, and the many ways differences can become our strengths. Here’s how we do this:

  • by promoting a culture of equity and belonging
  • by providing a fair work environment
  • by giving each team member a seat at the table
  • by putting our values into practice, daily
Our people

The soul of our company is our people, and the connections we share. We proactively hire talented people, regardless of location, and we empower them to make an impact.

Our peers

We work in agile, cross-functional teams, collaborating across time zones to improve our users’ lives. What really counts: initiative, impact, and the work you’re prepared to do.

Our customers

Busbud customers are the reason we exist. They live in far-flung places, and they rely on us for important travel choices. We strive to understand their needs and the challenges they face.

  • Perks & Benefits

  • Flexibility

    The Busbud work policy offers maximum flexibility and the option to work from anywhere. We’re flexible on hours and work setup. If you’re near Montreal, you can also work onsite at our newly renovated headquarters.

  • Stock options

    We believe you should share in our company’s success. We offer generous stock options for every Busbud employee.

  • Health and wellness

    Your health comes first. We offer comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as unlimited sick days, as well as unlimited access to telemedicine, including mental health services.

  • IT gear

    Unlock your productivity with the best tools available. We provide the latest computers and tech equipment so you can do your best work every day.

  • Fun and travel

    Busbud team members get free bus tickets across the globe. We offer this perk so you can see the world through the eyes of our customers. We also have regular company events and annual hackathons.
