We Are PerfectScale.

Providing DevOps teams the intelligence they need
to bring order to the Kubernetes chaos.
We get it.
Kubernetes is a critical part of your company's competitive advantage, allowing you to release new innovations at a rapid pace. This means you can continually “wow” your customers.
However, Kubernetes is also a colossal beast, and continually optimizing your environment is a complex task beyond human-scale to tackle. 
This is why we built PerfectScale. With the mission to provide easy-to-use solutions that empower teams to make quick, data-driven decisions, PerfectScale ultimately works to continuously improve your Kubernetes performance and availability, while keeping costs in check.

Our Values

Passion drives innovation

Our passion is to streamline how DevOps\SRE teams operate by turning data into actionable intelligence that fights ineffectiveness, endless firefighting, and, above all, chaos

Strive to delight

By listening to our customer’s needs, we are able to go above and beyond to design solutions that are both delightful and impactful

Bias for action and progress

One constant is–we are always learning. We experiment, we win, we lose, and we are not afraid of failure

People are our strength

We believe the future of work transcends borders. As a global, remote  team, every voice matters and the attributes brought by each individual is our competitive advantage

Trust and Transparency

We give our team members the space and trust to build amazing solutions, by empowering them with ethics, honesty, and transparency
