Who Are We?

See Open Positions

We are customer-focused

We listen actively to our clients and put ourselves in their place to understand their frustrations. We thrive on the challenges associated with innovation, and are passionate about R&D, but we never lose sight of our objective: to develop and provide creative solutions to our customers' concrete needs.

We prioritize efficiency

We strive to find the most efficient way to overcome any challenge. We like to be hands-on, building all our processes and products internally to keep costs down while ensuring the highest quality degree in everything we create.

Our talent is our biggest asset

We host some of the biggest experts in robotics worldwide, developers of the first commercial humanoid robots, and awarded with the 1st prizes of the DARPA Robotics Challenge and the NASA Robotics Challenge. We bring in the best in their fields and offer them total flexibility and support to keep working on projects they are passionate about. We value each individual contribution while fostering an atmosphere of teamwork and collaboration.
