Explore a Career at CalTex

Why CalTex?

If you’re looking for a company that offers a friendly and dedicated culture, look no further than CalTex. At CalTex, you will work with a talented group of individuals who are shaping the future of automotive protection. Our employees possess an incredible breadth and depth of experience that is reflected in our innovative and boundary-pushing work.

Our Company Culture

Our company fosters a culture that promotes continual opportunities for growth and development. Our Core Purpose is to serve our customers and each other with excellence. We operate on a set of five Core Values that serve as the foundation of how we think, act, and interact with our employees, customers and vendors. These values are our deep-rooted beliefs and the keys to our success: Compassion, Humility, Integrity, Service, and Teamwork.

Our Commitment to Excellence

What do we expect of our employees at CalTex? We expect everyone who joins our company to work hard and maintain our commitment to safety, quality, productivity, customer service, the reduction of waste, and to contribute to our efforts of being environmentally friendly. We work hard not only to produce the highest quality products, but also to maintain an image of professionalism.
