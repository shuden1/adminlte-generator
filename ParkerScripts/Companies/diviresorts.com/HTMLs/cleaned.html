If you live in St. Croix, the USVI, or outside of the islands, and you're a US citizen or permanent resident, come and join our team! To apply for any of the full-time positions listed below, please send your completed employment application, resume, and contact information to our Human Resources Department at letisia.williams@divicarina.com.

Bartender: A bartender is responsible for consistent, efficient, and friendly beverage service to all guests. Responsible for the cleanliness, setup, and breakdown before and after his/her shift. HIRING SAME-DAY, ON THE SPOT! Kitchen Steward: A kitchen steward is primarily responsible for operating and maintaining cleaning equipment and tools. Also responsible for washing dishes, china ware, flatware, cutlery, crockery, kitchen vessels, sweeping  and mopping floors, cleaning and detailing equipment's etc. Maintains the kitchen, dish washing and back area in an inspection-ready condition in accordance with hotel guidelines.

Food and Beverage Supervisor, Front of House: A food & beverage supervisor oversees the operations of the dining areas, manages staff, and ensures excellent customer service.

Food and Beverage Supervisor, Back of House: A food & beverage supervisor/kitchen supervisor supervises kitchen staff, ensures food safety and quality, and maintains a clean and organized kitchen environment.

Public Area Attendant: A public area attendant is responsible for maintaining the overall look of cleanliness for all guest areas.

Room Attendant: A room attendant cleans and maintains guest rooms, replenishes supplies, and ensures a comfortable and welcoming environment for guests.

Houseperson: A houseperson supports the housekeeping team by assisting with cleaning tasks, delivering supplies, and maintaining common areas.

Front Office Supervisor: A front desk supervisor manages front desk operations, supervises staff, and provides exceptional customer service to guests.

Bellstaff: Bellstaff assists guests with their luggage, provides directions and information, and offers personalized service to enhance the guest experience.

Activities Coordinator: An activities coordinator plans and organizes recreational activities and events for guests, ensuring an enjoyable and engaging experience.

Maintenance Supervisor: A maintenance supervisor is responsible for coordinating daily operations of the facilities and maintenance department as directed by the Chief Engineer. Ensures the total comfort, safety, and satisfaction for all resort guests and staff. Maintains equipment, buildings, and property.

Maintenance Engineer (Evening Shift): A maintenance engineer performs maintenance and repairs on equipment, systems, and facilities to ensure their proper functioning.

Chief Engineer: A chief engineer oversees the maintenance department, manages repairs and projects, and ensures the safety and functionality of the property.

Part-Time Receiving Clerk: A receiving clerk receives, inspects, and documents incoming shipments, ensuring accuracy and proper storage.

  • Aruba

    Home to the most pristine beaches in the world with a unique desert landscape, this Dutch island is full of fun for the whole family. Sparkling beaches offer the perfect place to relax while the island’s award-winning restaurants and endless shopping keeps adults entertained. The island is home to three resorts: Divi Aruba Phoenix Divi Dutch Village Divi Village Golf

  • Barbados

    Barbados features stunning beaches, colorful coral reefs, a wide range of activities, and a one-of-a-kind British heritage. This sunny paradise is the perfect year-round vacation spot for scuba divers, snorkelers, and those looking for an authentic island experience.

    Divi Southwinds

  • Bonaire

    Bonaire is known for its world-class scuba diving and snorkeling, and the island's desert terrain and azure waters create the perfect backdrop for your vacation. With over 80+ dive sites, plenty of adventures, exhilarating water sports, and pristine natural environments, you’ll feel rejuvenated in no time.

    Divi Flamingo

  • St. Croix

    Featuring spectacular cerulean waters, a diverse culture, and exciting adventures, St. Croix offers the perfect place to escape without needing a passport. Divi Carina Bay Oceans

  • St. Maarten

    Known as “The Friendly Island," this half-French, half-Dutch island boasts lush mountains, blue waters, impeccable beaches, and top-notch cuisine. Whether you want to shop, dine, relax, explore new adventures, or hit the hottest clubs, you'll find what you’re looking for on St. Maarten. Divi Little Bay Oceans
