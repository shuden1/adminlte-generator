Brentwood Bank, an innovative, customer-focused community bank exceeding $950 million in assets, has served the Pittsburgh region for over 100 years and has offices in Brentwood, Beaver, Bethel Park, South Park, South Fayette and Wexford. We believe banking is a people business and offer a variety of financial products and services all with a personal touch.

We are looking for exceptional people with a positive attitude, willingness to help our Bank continue to be successful, and be part of a great team.
