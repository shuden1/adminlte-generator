What We Value


Relationship Builders We care personally and treat people as people. When we prioritize building strong relationships through empathy, compassion, and honest communication, growth and success follow suit. Action Owners We check our egos, acknowledge mistakes, take ownership of our failures, and develop a plan to win. Every employee is expected, and encouraged, to lead from their role, no matter their tenure or circumstance. Candor Seekers As candor seekers, we understand that candid conversations are critical to achieving objectives and to growing personally and collectively. Storytellers We know our customers are the heroes, and our role is to be a guide on their journey. We strive to truly listen and identify the needs and wants of our customers, empowering them to avoid failure and achieve success.

As a global leader of digital transformation services, with a focus on hiring passionate people, Vasion is a great place to work. Unfortunately, being a coveted workplace means we are vulnerable to recruitment phishing scams. We urge all job seekers and candidates to be wary of potential scams. Most of these have individuals pose as representatives of prominent companies, including Vasion, with the aim of obtaining personal, sensitive, or financial information from applicants. These scams prey upon an individuals desire to obtain a job and can sometimes feel like a genuine recruitment process. Some red flags are identified below. Should you encounter a recruitment process that claims to be for Vasion but is not consistent with the following, please do not provide any personal or financial information. Legitimate Vasion recruitment processes include communication with candidates through our applicant tracking system powered by Workable. Emails from this system will be sent from noreply@candidates.workablemail.com. Other legitimate communications from Vasion will always be through an official Vasion.com email address (e.g., firstname.lastname@vasion.com) Vasion will not solicit personal data from candidates during the application phase including, but not limited to, date of birth, social security numbers, or bank account information. Legitimate Vasion interviews may be conducted over the phone, in person, or via zoom. They will never be conducted via text or email. Legitimate Vasion offers of employment are based on merit and only extended once a candidate has interviewed with members of the hiring team. Offers will be extended both verbally and in written form only through our official Vasion product. If you think that you have been a victim of fraud, contact the following authorities FBI - Internet Crime Complaint Center
