A Little Bit Of Who We Are

Dircks Moving & Logistics is Arizona’s largest moving company with a major presence in the Phoenix area. With over 30 years of experience and connections to Mayflower and UniGroup, our company strives to facilitate best-in-class residential moves, commercial moves, and logistics services.

Open Position Pathway

Here at Dircks we’re proud to boast an incredible career path no matter what your ideal role is. If you feel more comfortable in an office, our corporate and executive teams are our backbone keeping the logistics and growth of our team moving. If you prefer a job that keeps you moving and, on the road, our driver team is made up of world class, incredible workers that get our team and the client’s products everywhere they need to go, keeping our business busy and forward thinking. Or perhaps you prefer a job that really lets you work with your body and keeps you moving and energized all day? If so, then you may be the best fit to join our warehouse team!

We’re always looking for experienced, trustworthy individuals to add to our team. Our hiring process includes background checks and pre-employment drug testing to ensure only the best candidates come to work with us.
