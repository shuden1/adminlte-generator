Chassi isn’t for everyone. But maybe it’s for you.

It takes a special blend of attributes to thrive on this team and push us forward. But if you’ve got them, we need you. This is what you can expect in return.

CURRENT OPENINGS

Committed ownership

Each of us is responsible for our own successes and failures here. And there will be plenty of both. We have the autonomy to do what we think is best for Chassi and own the results.

Unique creation

When we say we’re building this company, we don’t mean like a Lego spaceship. The pieces to scale and support Chassi aren’t lying in a box somewhere. We’re creating each and every one of them.

Relentless improvement

We’re never fully satisfied with our own contributions to the team. We harbor a healthy discontent that motivates us to continually level up to make our team proud and do right by Chassi.

Persistent support

We lean on our teammates unapologetically and take joy in each other’s success. Building something new is hard, and we’re all humans. None of us could do this alone. We never forget that.

Benefits

We know investing in our team is the best thing we can do for the company. So we make it a priority to take care of you.

Health insurance

Your and your family’s health should always be a top priority. We offer comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance for your care.

Remote-friendly

We’re based in Scottsdale, AZ, but our team is dispersed across the world. If there’s a reason for you to be in Scottsdale, we’ll get you there.

Unlimited PTO

Work is a part of life, and we strongly believe in finding balance between the two. So take the time you need to show up as your best self here.

Continuous learning

Continually learning and bettering yourself is one of Chassi’s core pillars. If there’s a conference, course, or training you want to attend, we’ll cover it.
