Careers

At SFCD you will work with a passionate team to create beautiful products and solve real-world challenges for innovative startups and leading brands.

UX Designer, Miami

The UX Designer will collaborate with the UX Director, project managers, graphic designers and developers to design, launch, and iterate on mobile applications and websites. We are looking for someone with a solid understanding of user-centric design principles and current web and mobile technologies and 2 – 3 years of experience.

Responsibilities will include the creation of site maps, functional specifications, workflow diagrams, user scenarios, and wireframes, as well as working with the design and development teams to ensure that all UX requirements have been fulfilled and participating in the QA process. Visual design skills are a plus but not required. Please send us your resume and links to your portfolio.

Apply

Designer, Miami

We are looking for accomplished and young designers with a great eye for design and experience bringing successful products to life. You will be part of a small, focused team led by one of our partners. Please send us links to projects you helped launch and your portfolio.

Apply

Engineer, Miami

Engineers are involved in every project from the first meeting with a client to release and beyond, working in concert with our design team. We are looking for iOS, Android and web developers who have a mastery of technology and a love of design. Please send us your resume, GitHub handle, and links to notable projects you have worked on.
