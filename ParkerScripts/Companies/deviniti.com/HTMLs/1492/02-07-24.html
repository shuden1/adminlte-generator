Niniejsza strona korzysta z plików cookie

Wykorzystujemy pliki cookie do spersonalizowania treści i reklam, aby oferować funkcje społecznościowe i analizować ruch w naszej witrynie. Informacje o tym, jak korzystasz z naszej witryny, udostępniamy partnerom społecznościowym, reklamowym i analitycznym. Partnerzy mogą połączyć te informacje z innymi danymi otrzymanymi od Ciebie lub uzyskanymi podczas korzystania z ich usług.

FAQ

‘Should I apply to Deviniti?’ Read the FAQ

  • How do I know that Deviniti is the right place for me?

    Our astronauts gladly recommend working at Deviniti to others – as evidenced not only by the high eNPS, but also by the thriving referral program. Every day, we strive to make Deviniti a place that ensures stability and awareness that your needs are being taken into account:

    • We care about communication in the company among the employees themselves and between employees and managers – for this purpose, we use the Officevibe tool, thanks to which we can better understand what we need from each other and, based on this information, shape the work environment,
    • We exchange feedback  – we use it on many levels. It is a condition for development and the basis for partnership cooperation,
    • We appreciate each other – according to our employees, the relationships with their teams and leaders are the greatest advantage of working with Deviniti,
    • We support employees’ mental and physical health by providing access to the Mindgram platform and engaging the company in many sports activities (e.g., running group).

  • What is the recruitment process like at Deviniti?

    The recruitment process consists of several stages:

    • 1st step – a phone interview with a recruiter who will get to know your experience, career goals, and expectations for the new job.
    • 2nd step – a successful phone interview will result in an invitation to an online meeting with the team leader, who will discuss the details of the position and projects with you. During the meeting, there will also be a skills verification.
    • 3rd step – regardless of the outcome of the recruitment meeting, the recruiter will get back to you with feedback within 2 weeks.

  • How can I check the status of my application?

    Within minutes of submitting your application, you will receive confirmation that we have received it. If you want to check the status of your application, we encourage you to contact the recruiter indicated in the specific advertisement for which you applied.

    If your application meets our criteria and you are selected as one of the candidates for the initial stage of the recruitment process, we will reach out to you to schedule a phone interview.

  • Will my application be considered during other recruitment processes for various positions at Deviniti?

    If, during the application, you agreed to participate in future recruitment processes, recruiters will be able to consider your candidacy in various recruitments.

    We encourage you to apply for these job offers for which you have the appropriate qualifications and experience level, and to carefully read the job description and requirements that are listed in the advertisement.

  • Is it worth applying even if I do not find an offer that matches my experience and skills?

    Of course, we encourage you to submit your application even if we currently don’t have a job opening that matches your experience and skills. Simply leave us a spontaneous application by clicking the ‘Contact us’ button below the list of current recruitments

    In case a job opportunity aligning with your experience and skills arises, we will reach out to discuss further steps. Additionally, we recommend keeping an eye on our career section where we post current job openings

  • How to prepare for a recruitment meeting?

    The recruiter will guide you on what to focus on during your preparations for the interview for a specific position. However, regardless of the recruitment process, it’s always worth:

    • Getting to know Deviniti – our blog is a treasure trove of information about us in an accessible format. Our Instagram and LinkedIn profiles inform you about what’s happening in our Software Home.
    • Preparing questions for us – we value mutual understanding.
    • Take care of yourself – get enough sleep and relax, and we will support you in presenting your potential in the best possible way during the interview.

  • What is the cooperation with Deviniti like during the trial period?
    • Full support – remote onboarding opportunity and a stellar starter pack to welcome you; a 3-month probationary period that allows you to learn at your own pace (with the help of the team and your manager); a set of tools for work (laptop, headphones, phone – depending on the position), 
    • Choice – cooperation based on an employment contract (UoP) or B2B; remote or hybrid work (offices in Wrocław and Warsaw); flexible working hours – tailored to meeting schedules, 
    • Feedback – summary meetings with your manager during the onboarding process – you won’t get stuck in one place for sure.

  • What is the development perspective at Deviniti?

    We care about your satisfaction we want to enable you to develop in many ways. Belong to them:

    • Career paths – they function in most teams. You will undergo mapping that will allow you to determine your strengths and weaknesses as an employee and how to strengthen/work on them,
    • Udemy, team training – do you feel hungry for knowledge? Tell your supervisor! He will find a course for you on the Udemy platform or inform you about planned training for your team,
    • Training and development strategy (L&D) – we introduce it in subsequent units. It helps you and the leader plan the direction of your development in line with the company’s business goals.

  • Do you offer internships/internship programs for students/graduates?

    Currently, we do not run student internships or internship programs – but sometimes we open recruitment for junior positions.

    You can now send us your CV by e-mail at praca@deviniti.com and write a few words about what professional experience you want to gain, and we will save your application for future recruitment.

  • What else is worth remembering when applying to Deviniti?

    The partnership is one of our values, and we conduct recruitment processes at Deviniti following it. So don’t worry if:

    • you have concerns about whether you will be suitable for a given role – let’s talk about them only by transparent communication will allow us to find a solution that is good for both you and Deviniti,
    • due to unforeseen circumstances, if you cannot take part in the scheduled conversation/meeting, let us know, and we will find a new date,
    • you want to resign from participation in the recruitment – let us know both parties must be interested for the cooperation to start, and perhaps in the future, we will meet as part of another recruitment.
