Senior Human Resources Generalist

Aqfer, one of the fastest growing technology companies in Central Florida is looking for an amazing candidate to join our team as we scale our business.

Role:

The Senior Human Resource Generalist will work with Aqfer’s team of skilled technology professionals and run the daily functions of the Human Resource (HR) Department including: administering pay adjustments in our PEO’s HRIS, applying company policies and practices to meet operational and organizational goals, HR consulting, training, compliance, employee relations, disciplinary processes, performance management and administration of other programs, assigned projects, and reporting while maintaining individual productivity, quality and service levels.

The person in this role will also be responsible for managing our recruiting efforts as we rapidly grow our team. In the short term this position will be a hybrid position that will also provide administrative support to our sales executives. We expect to hire a dedicated administrative assistant in the next 6 months, at which time this role will be solely focused on Human Resources and recruiting.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

Service, Support and Training

  • Develops positive, productive partnership with all operations and sales leadership/management and staff personnel
  • Proactively engages and effectively communicates with leadership and management personnel, inquiring about any HR related issues and service, training, or employee needs and providing ongoing support and consultation regarding all HR activities
  • Reviews, tracks, and documents compliance with mandatory and non-mandatory training including: HR policies, processes, legal requirements, best practices and anti-harassment training
  • Participates in employee performance management activities including:
    • Ensuring all annual performance reviews are conducted within scheduled timeframes
    • Ensuring company procedures regarding performance review preparation and delivery are followed
    • Reviewing all performance documentation for accuracy and thoroughness
    • Participating in any performance review meetings with employees as requested by management

Recruiting

Lead recruiting efforts including:

  • Ensuring that job descriptions are complete and posted to relevant job boards
  • Managing third-party recruiting firms and ensuring they understand the candidate requirements
  • Scheduling interviews and managing the recruitment process
  • Ensures the company post-interview questionnaires and processes are completed
  • Briefing candidates on benefits and company policies
  • Reviewing new job descriptions with our HR attorney to ensure that offer letters reflect legal requirements and whether exemptions apply

Administration and Data Entry

  • Properly executes all HR-related administrative tasks including:
    • Compensation adjustments and employee changes
    • Employee on-boarding and off-boarding
    • Ensures all employee files for staff are complete and current, and that all required data is accurately and timely entered into the PEO’s HRIS and other software
    • Develops and maintains mastery of PEO’s HRIS, inputs any required data accurately, completely, and in a timely manner, and is able to instruct others on proper use
  • Understands and follows all assigned work and data flow processes, and works effectively with personnel from other departments (i.e. Payroll) to ensure timely and accurate completion of mission critical activities.
  • Prepares and distributes any assigned reports, ensuring accuracy, completeness, and that all deadlines are met

Corporate Responsibilities

  • Completes any assigned HR-related corporate tasks and projects accurately, completely and within required timeframes, including:
    • Worker’s Compensation
    • Unemployment & COBRA
    • All assigned special projects are completed
  • Continually seeks to improve individual and team performance, including identifying, communicating, and adopting best practices

Executive Administration Support Responsibilities

Provide administrative support to specified executives when requested or assigned, including:

  • Scheduling meetings
  • Data entry of sales activity information
  • Other administrative tasks as assigned

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Demonstrated HR functional knowledge and skills
  • 3+ years recent Human Resources experience with generalist experience preferred
  • Must have recent experience working in the private sector with employees who are senior technology professionals, both exempt and non-exempt.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • SHRM preferred
  • Ability to act with integrity, professionalism, and confidentiality
  • Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to prioritize tasks and to delegate them when appropriate
  • Computer proficiency including Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides

This position is remote, ideally in the Eastern or Central Time Zones, but would prefer a candidate who can occasionally visit the Orlando office for meetings.

BENEFITS:

  • Medical, Dental, and Vision Insurance
  • 401K with Matching
  • Flexible Spending Account
  • Flexible Schedule
  • Parental Leave
  • Paid Holidays
  • Unlimited Paid Time Off (with Supervisor Approval)
  • Stock Options
