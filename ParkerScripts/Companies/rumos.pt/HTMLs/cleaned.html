What we offer

We offer an inclusive, collaborative, and stimulating work environment, where everyone has the opportunity to grow professionally. We like to share our success with our collaborators, and we take a fully transparent approach when it comes to individual performance and growth. 

Come be part of a team passionate about innovation and excellence, constantly seeking to challenge the status quo, and exceed expectations when it comes to providing Information Technology Training and Consulting services.

People-centric culture

Integration in a corporate group made by people for people, which believes in and promotes human potential. 

Training and Certification Plan

Access to a continuous and personalized Training and Certification plan that offers the opportunity to continue to grow professionally. 

Collaborator Wellness Program

Access to Rumos Beside You, a health and psychological support program for all collaborators.

Network of partners

Access to exclusive benefits on many services and products from our network of partners, including banks, gyms, pharmacies, and optical stores, among others. 

Work and communication equipment

Collaborators are provided with a cell phone and a computer for work and personal use.

Health Insurance

Access to a health insurance plan.

What we look for

We are looking for outstanding professionals who can add value to our teams and who want to invest in their personal and professional growth. 

If you are a proactive and active person, who is always looking for new challenges, join our team and help us transform Portugal's Information Technology Training and Consulting sector.

Alignment with the company’s vision

We are looking for collaborators who align with the purpose and culture of our company. 

Ambition

We are looking for curious people who want to challenge their skills and who put no limits to their will to learn and evolve.  

Ability to surpass oneself

We are looking for dedicated professionals with different ideas and the motivation to put them into practice.  

Dynamism

We are looking for dynamic people to join our teams, who have a sense of responsibility, team spirit, and a problem-solving approach.

Passion for technology

We are looking for collaborators who are passionate about technology, who exceed themselves, and are focused on customer satisfaction.

Vision for the future

We are looking for people who are motivated to build the future with us.
