BE A PART OF SOMETHING GR8

Gain new knowledge and experience while working side by side with the brightest talents. Together, we will build and develop global innovative solutions and reach new heights.

Digital first approach

Gr8 talent has no borders. You can work wherever you are — that’s up to you! We hire and collabor8 with talent worldwide.

Learning environment

You can grow with the team and gain new skills with a personal development plan, Opinov8 Academy, Mentoring program, English enhancement, and workshops. We can cover education expenses and support you in obtaining professional certifications.

Health care

We offer insurance coverage, paid sick leave, workshops with psychologists, and offline & online sports activities, including our football team and fitness & yoga trainer.

Referral program

We encourage our team to refer their friends and are always grateful for all recommendations. Op8rs receive a referral bonus for each successful hire.

Workplace

We offer an equal and transparent working environment, providing comfortable and flexible working conditions. Feel free to work from any corner of the world or base yourself in one of our cozy offices in London (UK), Kyiv (UA), Cairo (EG), Chicago (US), and Barranquilla (CO).
