This is Feedzai

How We Hire

What Benefits does Feedzai Offer?

We offer a wide variety of benefits for our employees globally. Here you can see the benefits all Feedzaians enjoy.

Is Feedzai a remote-friendly company?

Yes!  At Feedzai, we offer a Flex/Remote work policy. If you are near an office location, we have three options for you to choose the work style that best suits your needs:

> Primarily Office Based: You are in the office regularly 4 – 5 days per week.

> Flexible: You are in the office 2-3 days per week.

> Primarily Remote: You are in the office 1-2 days per month, as business requires.

We also have full remote options for employees who are not near one of our global offices.

I have signed the Offer letter. What will happen next?

Congratulations, you have just become a Feedzaian! After signing the Offer Letter, you will receive an email/call from the Talent Acquisition Team guiding you through the process and transition to the HR Team. The Talent Acquisition Team will advise you on who in the HR Team will be reaching out to you to help facilitate the final paperwork and process. As you move closer to your start date and begin the onboarding process, our HR Team will support you.

I have applied for a role. When can I expect to hear back?

Our Talent Acquisition Team at Feedzai is committed to providing a best-in-class candidate experience. We keep in close contact with each candidate throughout the hiring steps, and its duration depends on various factors, such as the number of applicants, the complexity of the position, and the company’s internal processes.

Can I apply for multiple jobs?

Yes, you can generally apply for multiple positions at Feedzai. However, you must meet the qualifications and requirements for each role you’re applying for. Submitting tailored applications for each role you’re interested in can increase your chances of being considered. Please, note that you will only be considered for one position: the one that best matches your skills, experience, and motivation.

How do I apply for a job at your company?

You can apply for a job by navigating to All Jobs. There, you will find our current job openings.

Can I submit a general application or resume for future consideration?

Yes, you can submit a general application on our Talent Community Page.

Are there opportunities for career growth and advancement within the company?

At Feedzai, one of our pillars is a culture of learning. We believe in internal mobility and growth and provide broad opportunities for career advancement. We offer an annual training budget for each Feedzaian. The specific career growth prospects can vary based on the role and individual performance. You can ask us about our commitment to professional development and potential career paths in your interview.

What is the interview process like?

Check the How We Hire Page to learn more about our interview process.
