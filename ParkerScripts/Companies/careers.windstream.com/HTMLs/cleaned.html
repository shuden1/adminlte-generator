IT Jobs

If you’re searching for a career in Information Technology, Windstream is the place to be. Whether you’re an expert in your field or just starting to get your feet wet, we’ve invested heavily in our IT Department to ensure our team has access to the latest technology.

CIO Stephen C. Farkouh believes “the opportunity to work on next-generation platforms and newer technologies” is what sets our IT department apart. In this business unit, you’ll have the opportunity to work on database platforms, information services, information solutions and so much more.

If working with cutting-edge technology and being a part of a WINning team motivates you, you’ve come to the right place. The best part of being on our team with access to the latest technology is we’re firm believers in investing back into our team by providing the necessary tools and training.
