Our values

  • We are a Community

    We believe our diverse community is a competitive advantage; we support all ideas, strengths and cultural backgrounds.

  • Improve & Learn Relentlessly

    Inspect and adapt is in our DNA, we continuously try new things, reflect and improve. We seek feedback, we learn from our mistakes and successes. We always strive for personal growth.

  • Think user driven

    Our end users are the core of our business. We carefully listen to and respect their needs to help us deliver an outstanding experience.

  • Drive Trust and Empowerment

    We coach our people. We foster empowerment. We rely on and trust the expertise of our colleagues and teams.

  • Innovate MBitiously

    We act with courage and innovate through experimentation. We challenge the status quo and strive to find new ways to improve.

  • Keep Your Word

    We are transparent and communicate without fear to build trust. We keep our promises and are committed to doing business the right way.

  • Win as a team

    All for one, and one for all. We strive for a common vision, between teams, departments and even companies. We win together.
