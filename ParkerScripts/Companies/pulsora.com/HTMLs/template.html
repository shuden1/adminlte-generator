What we value

Integrity

We trust our teammates to do the right thing and we value sincere and honest dialog.

Respect for all

We value and respect individuals from diverse backgrounds and strive to create an inclusive environment.

Constructive candor

We value open feedback that empowers growth, fostering culture where honesty is paired with kindness and understanding.

Intelligence

We value the ability to acquire new knowledge and skills and are constantly seeking improvement.

Passion for excellence

We strive for the highest quality in everything we do and constantly push ourselves to improve.

Teamwork

Teamwork is a powerful force that can help a company achieve its goals faster.

Benefits and perks

Health insurance

We offer competitive medical, dental, vision insurance for employees and dependents.

Flexible time off

We want you to take time off to rest and rejuvenate. Pulsora offers flexible paid vacation as well as 10+ observed holidays by country.

Employee equity

We believe in sharing success. As part of our team, you’ll have the opportunity to own a piece of Pulsora through our employee equity program.

Retirement savings

Pulsora makes it easy to save money for retirement. There’s also employer matching!

Paid parental leave

We offer biological, adoptive, and foster parents paid time off to spend quality time with family.

Monthly stipend

We offer everyone a monthly stipend to cover the cost of internet and phone.

Remote friendly

Set your own schedule and be flexible about where you work: from our offices in the Bay Area and New York, from home, or a bit of both!

Exact benefits vary depending on the country you’re hired in.

At Pulsora, we value and respect people of all backgrounds—it’s one of our core values. We are committed to creating an environment that encourages our employees to show up authentically, reach their highest potential, and have an equal opportunity to thrive.
