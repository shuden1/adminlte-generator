Careers

We firmly believe that our employees drive the success of our organization.

Our goal is to provide effective representation, innovative resources and educational programs for our members while creating an environment that offers challenging and rewarding opportunities for our employees.

The American Trucking Associations accepts applications for employment from all persons without regard to race, color, sex, religion, national origin, disability, age, sexual orientation, political affiliation, covered veteran status or any other criterion prohibited by law. Each applicant or employee will be screened on the basis of their individual abilities and qualifications; non-job related criteria will not be considered. Applicants have rights under Federal Employment Laws. Please see below for more information or contact humanresources@trucking.org with any questions.
