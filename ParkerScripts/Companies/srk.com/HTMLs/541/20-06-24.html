    Career Opportunities

    SRK is a leading provider of consulting services to the global resource industry. We are currently seeking qualified professionals for positions in our offices around the world and invite you to join us and develop your professional skills within our organisation.

    Our employees are technical specialists from a broad range of disciplines, such as mineral exploration, geology, resource and reserve estimation, geotechnical engineering, hydrology, hydrogeology, mine engineering, mineral economics, environmental and social services, mineral processing, and waste and tailings disposal.
    We are a “learning organisation” that invites professional growth in a self-directed environment. Our employees demonstrate leadership within their technical fields, effective problem-solving skills, and a strong ability to communicate commercial outcomes to clients.
