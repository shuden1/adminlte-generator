Recruitment Practices at MacroHealth

MacroHealth’s global recruitment and hiring process is centralized and managed by our Talent Acquisition team in our People Operations Department. Although MacroHealth has internal recruiting staff that successfully fill most positions, we may occasionally seek assistance from Recruiters/Staffing Agencies, and truly value the relationships we build and maintain with trusted staffing partners. If you are a professional recruiter interested in working with MacroHealth, please read our Message to Recruiters for additional information.
