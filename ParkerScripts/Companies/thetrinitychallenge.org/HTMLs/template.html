We are currently seeking an experienced, dynamic and organised Project Coordinator. This is an exciting opportunity to play a crucial role in supporting the effective coordination and implementation for various projects within the charity.

Working closely with the Project Manager, you will support functions across all departments of the small team, with a particular focus on stakeholder management. The Trinity Challenge is a charity that endeavours to solve complex global health problems through a programme of incentives and accelerated innovation. A coalition of partners is at the heart of the charity, united by the common aim of better protecting the world against health emergencies, using data-driven research and analytics.

Our vision is that a smarter, more cross-sectoral approach to data and analytics is vital to building effective, affordable and scalable surveillance and response systems around the threat of infectious diseases. We have sought to leverage the advances in data tools and technology in new ways that are more agile, more effective, less expensive and benefit more people and communities across the world.
