SMITE 2 - Motion Graphics Artist
Alpharetta,GA
Full Time
Copied to clipboard!

Titan Forge Studios is looking to hire an experienced Motion Graphics Artist to work at our studio in Alpharetta, Georgia.At Hi-Rez, you will have an opportunity to be part of an innovative environment that embraces new and different ideas. We are a passionate and enthusiastic bunch who love video games. We have incredibly talented individuals who are empowered to create and challenged to learn. All of this means more opportunities for you to unleash your creativity and dedication.

Come join the creator of the critically acclaimed SMITE, SMITE 2, and as we embark on exciting new game projects!

Responsibilities:

  • Generate motion graphic animations and broadcast assets for use on our videos, streams, games, and social channels.
  • Capture cinematic in-game/editor footage on Unreal Engine 3 and Unreal Engine 5.
  • Implement post production onto live-action footage.
  • Estimates how long a task will require to hit a specific quality bar, and provides estimates for long-term scheduling of animation tasks.

Requirements:

  • 3-5 years of industry experience.
  • Demonstrable knowledge of animating in After Effects
  • Delivers high-quality animation assets.
  • Expertise in animation principles, anatomy, design, typography, and video.
  • Can show attention to detail, spacing of assets, and readability of assets.

Preferred Candidates:

  • Geeks out over the new Unreal Motion Graphics tools & AI
  • Degree in Design/Communication field.
  • Traditional/Digital illustration and typography skills.
  • Knowledge of the games industry as a whole.
  • Knowledge of trends in Social Media and Advertising as a whole.
Salary Range: $65,400 to $90,900/annually

SMITE 2 - Sound Designer
Alpharetta,GA
Full Time
Copied to clipboard!
Hi-Rez Studios is looking to hire a Sound Designer to join our SMITE 2 team, headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.At Hi-Rez, you will have an opportunity to be part of an innovative environment that embraces new and different ideas. We are a passionate and enthusiastic bunch who love video games. We have incredibly talented individuals who are empowered to create and challenged to learn. All of this means more opportunities for you to unleash your creativity and dedication. Sound design assets and implement sound effects in-game.Come join the creators of the critically acclaimed games SMITE, Paladins, Tribes: Ascend, and Global Agenda as we embark on exciting new game projects!

What our Sound Designers do:

  • Sound design assets and implement sound effects in-game.
  • Proficient with UE4/UE5 and Wwise.
  • Work with the Audio Director to establish the audio aesthetic for the game.
  • Work with the Audio Director and designers to explore new audio features, design for gods, skins and implementation processes for the game.
  • Interface with producers leads, and team members on audio needs and asset lists.

Who we are looking for:

  • A self-motivated individual who works well when left on his/her own.
  • 3+ years of experience in audio for AAA games.
  • Solid sound design and implementation skills.
  • Ability to work quickly and go through iterations.
  • Ability to work well with feedback.
Salary range:$71,900 - $99,900/annually
