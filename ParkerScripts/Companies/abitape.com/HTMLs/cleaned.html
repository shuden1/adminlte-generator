What We're About

With over 115 years of experience in the business, the ABI and Ideal Tape teams share a passion for producing high-quality tape products for a wealth of markets. We are a tight knit tribe whose values surround Health & Safety, Drive to Serve Our Customers, Integrity, Teamwork, and Social Responsibility. If that sounds like a company culture you can get on board with, we are always looking for extraordinary people to join our team.

 

PLEASE NOTE: Resumes will not be accepted for plant/factory positions. In-person applications only are accepted at the Lowell, MA, and Moorestown,  NJ sites.

 

EHS Generalist - Operations

Posted on: 2023-08-31 | Location: Lowell, MA

SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES • Leads programs for compliance with environmental, health, safety & security regulations and requirements and provides compliance specifications to other areas of function • Develop, implement, and lead the facility’s EHS assessment process • Ensures accurate and timely environmental and safety recordkeeping and document management including the submission of periodic reports to regulatory agencies • Leads and/or assists with emergency response activities • Interface with regulatory and public sector safety authorities as appropriate • Participate in Safety Audits, PHA’s and other elements of Plant/Process Safety Management integral to sites’ safety performance • Develop, review, and revise site safety plan in collaboration with site leadership teams • Prioritize major hazards and risks across the site, and ensure actions are taken to mitigate or eliminate risks • Lead incident investigations for safety and environmental incidents and participate in management review of all serious incidents • Participate in formal HSE reviews of existing equipment modifications, new equipment purchases, and changes in site processes • Lead, support, and guide ABI safety committees and activities • Participate in annual budget development process and drive cost reduction opportunities • Track, report, and analyze safety performance and implement corrective actions as necessary • Leads the development, review, and revision of safety training programs; facilitates train-the-trainer sessions. • Must be independently driven to seek improvement opportunities in the safety review process. • Help manage sites’ security programs and systems • Work with Sites’ Engineering & Maintenance Resources to resolve issues identified during Loss Prevention/Risk Management inspections • Promote incident prevention for the benefit of employees and visitors • Assist in the development and presentation of relevant HSE training • Ability to oversee Hazardous Waste Storage area and ensure regulatory agency compliance • Achieve annual targets for incident frequency, severity, and related cost reductions
