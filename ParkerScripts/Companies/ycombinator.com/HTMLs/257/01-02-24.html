Why you should join Pirros

Pirros is the Github for Construction. Architects and engineers spend most of their time sketching blueprints and then have no way to store them. As a first step, we have built a content management system for all their blueprints with version control. The vision includes automatically suggesting and generating final blueprints based on a series of input parameters.
