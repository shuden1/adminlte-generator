      Stephanie is a parent CNA for her son, Giovanni. Her role in caring for her son began in September of 2023 when Giovanni suffered a traumatic brain injury, retinal hemorrhaging behind both eyes, a subdural hematoma and other injuries at just two years old. Giovanni had to learn how to eat, walk and talk again.

      While Giovanni was in the hospital, Stephanie found out about Aveanna and our parent CNA program. Using her years of experience as a caregiver for dementia and Alzheimer's patients, Stephanie began caring for Giovanni full-time, which could ensure that he received personal, individualized care.

      "Being a parent CNA is so hard but also so rewarding," said Stephanie. "I get to see my son improve every day knowing he’s getting the best care provided by me. I’ve always thought and said that everyone should be taken care of the same way their family member would take care of them. Providing care to someone else who needs it is by far one of the most rewarding feelings in the world, and I will continue to provide care for anyone in need that crosses my path.”

      Thank you for the compassionate care that you provide, Stephanie! You make a difference every day.
