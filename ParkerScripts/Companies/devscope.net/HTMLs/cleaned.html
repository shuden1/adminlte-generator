Room to grow — and to change

People at DevScope are in charge of their future. We give all our fellow collaborators the tools and the chance to learn from some of the best tech professionals in Portugal, including access to several current and former Microsoft MVPs, always available to pass on their knowledge. IT is an ever-changing landscape, but thanks to our partnership with Microsoft, DevScope collaborators have the chance to enroll in certification programs throughout the year and help the company grow while future-proofing their careers.

Learn and teach at DevScope

Every few weeks, all our employees have the chance to learn about the technology that drives their colleagues at our internal knowledge sharing sessions. Aptly named “CSI: Conhecimento sob Investigação”, these internal conferences give employees a platform to share the technology they love to work with and are a great opportunity to practice their presentation and teaching skills. Anyone at DevScope can deliver a CSI; all it takes is a subject you’re passionate about, a little preparation, and the will to share it with everyone.
