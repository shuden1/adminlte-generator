      Tips & Tricks

    Search Modifier

    Either terms
    Manager Supervisor retrieves an opportunity for a Manager or a Supervisor when searching for an opportunity.

    Exact phrase
    "Customer Service Manager""Customer Service Supervisor" retrieves an opportunity for a Customer Service Manager or a Customer Service Supervisor.

    Include terms
    Manager + Accounting returns opportunities for Manager and Accounting.

    Exclude terms
    -Sales returns all opportunities that do not have the term Sales associated to them.

    Either OR
    Developer + (apache | weblogic | websphere) returns Developer opportunities with at least one of the 3 relevant terms.

    Combining terms
    Manager + -Sales returns all opportunities that have Manager in them that do NOT have Sales associated to them.
    Tips & Tricks
    Search ModifierDescriptionExample
    Either terms Either search term value. Manager Supervisor retrieves an opportunity for a Manager or a Supervisor when searching for an opportunity.
    Exact phrase Returns the exact phrase match within quotes."Customer Service Manager""Customer Service Supervisor" retrieves an opportunity for a Customer Service Manager or a Customer Service Supervisor.
    Include terms The '+' indicates that both terms must be present. Manager + Accounting returns opportunities for Manager and Accounting.
    Exclude terms The '-' indicates that the term must not be present.-Sales returns all opportunities that do not have the term Sales associated to them.
    Either OR Offers flexible inclusion, and typically broadens your search results.You may use parentheses to encapsulate OR statements.Developer + (apache | weblogic | websphere) returns Developer opportunities with at least one of the 3 relevant terms.
    Combining terms Retrieves records that include the first term if they do not have the second term.Manager + -Sales returns all opportunities that have Manager in them that do NOT have Sales associated to them.
