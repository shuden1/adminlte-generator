Learn MoreAccounting Manager

The Accounting Manager will be responsible for performing a variety of accounting duties, including but not limited to journal entries, reconciliations, preparation and analysis of financial statements, ad hoc reporting, and ensuring compliance with accounting principles and regulations. The ideal candidate possesses leadership capabilities with a solid technical and operational accounting background with excellent analytical skills, and the ability to meet month-end deadlines.

Target Base annual salary range: $115,000 to $125,000 based on experience

Remote work with days in office as business needs require.

Learn MoreTSP Consolidation Specialist

The TSP Consolidation Specialist will assist the participant in all facets of the consolidation process through inbound contacts and outbound follow up. They will utilize a consultative approach to determine the needs of the participant and utilize a course of action that best suits those needs. This position will be responsible for owning the entire consolidation process from start to finish through effective communication, follow through, and case management.
