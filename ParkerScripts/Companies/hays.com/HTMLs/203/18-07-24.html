Service Desk Specialist – Permanent – Oxnard, CA – $60,000 - $65,000.

The final salary or hourly wage, as applicable, paid to each candidate/applicant for this position is ultimately dependent on a variety of factors, including, but not limited to, the candidate’s/applicant’s qualifications, skills, and level of experience as well as the geographical location of the position.

Applicants must be legally authorized to work in the United States. Visa sponsorship not available.

Our client is seeking a Service Desk Specialist in Oxnard, CA.

Role Description

• Provide operational leadership to ensure networks, systems, and processes and procedures are clearly documented and to further share operational insight with all team members.
• Act as a liaison between local business units and corporate service desk for IT service management and infrastructure initiatives to ensure needs, requirements, and project delivery meet operational expectations
• Work with the IT security team to ensure technical and operational security controls are implemented and maintained into new and existing systems and applications. This is done by ensuring that corporate standards are applied for all equipment connected to the client network.
• Participate in ongoing maintenance, testing, and governance of business continuity and disaster recovery plans.
• Manage project implementation with the help of the IT project management team as assigned by corporate IT or self-identified to ensure alignment of resource and project dates.
• Provide in-person and remote escalated technical support, troubleshooting, training, and resolution of IT related issues to ensure exemplary customer support standards are met.
• Frequent travel to regional sites will be required initially with overnight stay likely. Use of personal vehicle with reimbursement according to IRS guidelines is required. 1-2 trips per year to headquarters in Florida may be required.

Skills & Requirements

• Proven customer service and organizational skills
• Working experience troubleshooting software and hardware issues
• Ability to operate tools, components and peripheral accessories
• A+, Server +, Network+ knowledge (certification preferred)
• Knowledge of ITIL foundation (preferably ITIL certified)
• Working knowledge of Microsoft Suite of application and remote control tools
• Degree in Information Technology or related field preferred along with 5 years of experience
• Microsoft, Cisco, service management certifications (preferred)
• 3-5 years of relevant experience in a technology support role required
• Basic knowledge of enterprise infrastructure and technology
• Excellent interpersonal and communication skills along with demonstrated ability to partner with organizational leadership
