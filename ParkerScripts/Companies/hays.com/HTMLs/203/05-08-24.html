Local DC based General Contractor hiring a Senior Project Manager

Your new company:
Seeking to hire a Senior Project Manager to work on an excellent variety of mixed commercial interior construction projects in the DC area. This general contractor has an incredibly strong pipeline of Government and mixed Commercial in within the DC beltway and are looking to hire Senior Project Managers with new and renovation construction commercial experience to join them on this journey! This a permanent, long-term career position with a genuine career path to a senior leadership position on offer. If you are looking to join a company that can offer you the chance to work on high-profile projects providing job security, this is a position for you to apply to!


Your new role:
As a Senior Project Manager in their construction department, you will be site-based working alongside an established and successful field and project management. You will be reporting into a Project Executive. If you are passionate about people leadership, there is the opportunity here to lead a team.


You will be responsible for running the project management program for your assigned projects, working closely with the field management team. Project budget and financial tracking, scheduling, construction documentation, quality control and client POC will all fall under your/PM teams’ responsibility. You will have the support of APMs and be tasked with developing their skill set into a Project Manager as you help develop the department.


What you need to succeed:
  • 8-14 years of Construction experience working for a commercial construction general contractor.
  • Ideally you will have experience in working on local government, public construction projects including education but any commercial construction experience will be considered.
  • A construction related degree such as engineering, architecture or construction management is desired but not essential.
  • This is an on-site position on a construction site, likely in the Northern Virginia area so candidates will need to be local and be comfortable to a daily commute.


What you'll get in return:
  • Base salary in the range of $135k - $150k
  • Yearly bonus in the 10%-40% range based on performance.
  • Vehicle allowance
  • Enrolment into the company Profit Sharing Plan.
  • Company paid healthcare, dental and vision plans including family.
  • 401k Plan with a company match
  • Professional Development Assistance through company paid courses and tuition support.
  • Genuine career paths available to a Project Executive position.


What you need to do now:
If you're interested in this Construction Senior Project Manager position and meet the requirements above, click 'apply now' to forward an up-to-date copy of your CV, or call Scott Kinson now on 2024700641. If this job isn't quite right for you but you are looking for a new position, please contact us for a confidential discussion on your career.
