Job Duties

Working closely with the business management analyst and project managers in the development team to research, design and develop customized software applications for event planning and logistics services according to client needs in the life science industry which includes pharmaceutical, biotech, and medical device companies. Analyze user needs to build user friendly enterprise level front end layers, middle layers and data access layers for web and mobile applications. Design, develop, and prepare software models and diagrams to provide the core functions of software requirements. Identify and investigate defects in functionality of existing software applications to determine alternative models or applications. Research and utilize cutting edge technologies and emerging programming languages for software development to implement and enhance application features. Collaborate with other internal teams to determine feasibility and cost benefit analysis in order to implement application development objectives and corresponding metrics, such as project costs and timeline schedules based on estimates for new projects.
