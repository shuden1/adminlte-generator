why work with cityswift

Our Benefits

Flexible Working

Our employees have the opportunity of working remotely or in our office as well as flexi-time during our core business hours.

Paid Time Off

Employees are entitled to 25 days annual leave, paid sick leave plus additional discretionary company days. There is 100% paid time off for maternity, paternity, and adoptive leave, as well as paid force majeure, compassionate and special leave.

Financial Benefits

We ensure our employees are well compensated by offering a number of financial benefits from bike to work and bonus schemes to matching pension contributions.

Career Development

We support our employees’ growth by offering personal career paths and continuous learning and development opportunities.

Health & Wellbeing

At CitySwift, we provide comprehensive healthcare insurance and free VDU eye tests, as well as employee assistance programs and mental health resources.

Our People

“I moved from Dublin to join CitySwift in Galway – and I haven’t looked back! Everyone here is keen to learn and lend a hand, creating a great environment for people to excel both personally and collectively.
