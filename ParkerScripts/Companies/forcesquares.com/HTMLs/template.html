Career

Why work with us?

Here at Forcesquares we consider ourselves not just a business but a family, and we believe every single professional within our growing brand is an asset.

Our employees like working here because of the great teamwork and the chance to solve challenging problems in the best interests of our clients. Through integrity and accountability, our employees appreciate the responsibility and opportunity that is given to them.

Salesforce Consultant - 10 positions

Job Description

  • Customer Support, liaising directly with the clients.
  • Expertise with any of the Sales/Service/Marketing cloud
  • Analyse, document and review business, functional, and technical requirements.
  • Salesforce admin/developer certified required and Sales/Service/Marketing consultant certification preferred.

Salesforce Developer- 20 positions

Job Description

  • Deployment using complex enterprise Force.com release management and knowledge of Force.com Metadata API.
  • Apex & Visual Force Development/SFDC Configuration/Force.com
  • 3+Years strong Salesforce development background and Salesforce.com Certifications (PD1, PD2 and Sales/Marketing/Service consultant certification )
  • Preferred- Competency in administering, configuring, and securing Einstein Analytics

Salesforce Architect- 3 Positions

Job Description

  • Design & develop solutions primarily on the Force.com platform using Apex programming language and Visual force.
  • Design, Configure and Develop solution that integrate and automate data interfaces between Salesforce and other systems.
  • 2+ years of experience in Salesforce Architecture or Salesforce Architecture certification; And 6+ years of Salesforce application development and Salesforce.com Certifications (Salesforce 3x AND Application Architect System Architect/Technical Architect/B2C Solution Architect/B2C Commerce Architect)

Salesforce Project Manager- 5 Positions

Job Description

  • Assists clients in the design, business architect, process development, functional implementation, and support of Salesforce CRM Applications.
  • Provide experience in designing workflow rules to enforce business processes
  • 5+ years of experience as a Project Manager. 3+ years of Salesforce experience and Salesforce.com Certifications (Admin, Consultant Sales / Service and any project management certification preferred)
