Perks & Benefits

Healthy means happy all Schibsted employees have access to great wellbeing choices →

Healthy means happy

Schibsted provides opportunities for employees to participate in health and well-being activities at the workplace and at home using Schibsted Life. Examples of offers can be running clubs, morning yoga sessions on the roof terrace, christmas workshops or inspirational talks from external or internal speakers and much more.

We always aim to promote healthy lifestyle behaviors that have been found to optimize and improve overall health and well-being

We also offer emotional and mental support, and preventative measures.

Flexible workmodel – the Schibsted workplace is more than just an office – it is anywhere we work together →

Flexible workmodel

Whether you work from the office or remotely, we will support future work styles by providing a sustainable environment that nurtures collaboration, creativity and innovation.

Employee Share Saving Plan – take part in Schibsted’s potential value-creation by saving in the Schibsted B-share →

Employee Share Saving Plan

All employees can take part in Schibsted’s potential value-creation by saving in the Schibsted B-share. You get the opportunity to earn 1 free Schibsted B-share for every 2 Schibsted B-shares you own for more than 2 years. Participation is voluntary and the intention is to motivate employee ownership and strengthen our corporate culture.

Lifelong learning we facilitates further skills development through internal and external courses. →

Lifelong learning

Internal development programs are there to make sure employees both have regular quality talks with their leader and access to education and courses that are relevant to them.

Our Learning Lab prepares our people and teams for the future by offering customized learning experiences, development programs, and leader support.

Learning and development

Regardless of how you prefer to learn, we strive to be enablers of our employees' growth aspirations. The reward of carefully tending to, nurturing and cultivating this growth is making sure everyone at Schibsted is in full bloom.
