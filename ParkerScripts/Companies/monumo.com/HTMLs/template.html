Our values

Responsible

We are driven by our purpose: to move the world to a sustainable future by using deeptech to create the world’s best electric motors. When it comes to the work each of us does - its contribution and impact - we encourage autonomy, and that comes with obligations too. Responsibility, accountability, and a duty of care are pre-requisites to the contribution we make and the impact we have, collectively and individually.

Determined

Fuelled by our desire to break new ground and motivated by the relevance and complexity of the challenge ahead, there is a palpable determination in our growing team. It’s the rare combination of talent, creativity and meaningful purpose that makes Monumo a special place to work.

Curious

Monumo’s culture is both open-minded and inquisitive, striving to remove bias from every aspect of our business, our thinking and our technology. Have we asked the right questions? How much better could it be? Is there another way to look at this? What if…?

Different

Monumo celebrates diversity in all things… Every individual here makes up the core strength of Monumo – a diverse approach, a diversity of skills, expertise, backgrounds, outlooks, ways of working and personalities. The Monumo team are all different, but we are driven by our common purpose; to uncover solutions to the challenge of decarbonisation and building a sustainable future.

How we will reward you

  • Holidays & Flexibility
    • Hybrid working with 3 core days a week in the office. We are flexible about where you work
    • 30 days holiday and time off between Christmas and New Year
    • A month’s paid sabbatical after every 5th year at Monumo
  • Financial Benefits
    • Competitive compensation
    • Everyone who joins Monumo will receive a meaningful share of the business in the form of options attached to their salary
    • Pension plan with employer contributions
    • Cycle to work scheme
    • Salary sacrifice Workplace Nursery Scheme
    • We pay a referral bonus for new joiners you introduce to us as a thank you. (subject to them completing their probationary period)
  • Health & Wellbeing
    • Private healthcare
    • Life assurance
    • Shared full pay for maternity, paternity or adoption leave (subject to length of service) 
    • Support to take care of your mental and physical wellbeing
    • We encourage ongoing training and development for everyone

Monumo celebrates diversity in all its forms. Our core value of Different is not just a statement, but a guiding principle that permeates everything we do.

We embrace the richness of varied experiences, knowledge, heritage and perspectives within our team; harnessing them to drive innovation and make a lasting impact.

Our team is as diverse as the challenges we tackle, but we're united by a shared purpose: to lead the charge in decarbonisation and pave the way for a better world. Each individual brings a unique set of skills, backgrounds and personalities; contributing to the strength of our collective whole, learning from one another, fostering an atmosphere where everyone can thrive and grow.
