    FAQ

    How is remote work organized at Playrix?

    Our employees live in different cities and countries, so we have a distributed team. This means that everyone works remotely from home or from our offices in Ukraine, Armenia, Kazakhstan, Cyprus, and Serbia.

    We use Slack, Asana, Zoom, and other tools to communicate with one another. On their first day of work, each employee receives all the equipment they need for their job. We also reimburse the cost of office furniture if you choose to work from home.

    We have a flexible work schedule, but we try to hold most meetings between 8 AM and 1 PM GMT.

    Learn more about remote work at Playrix:

    • The founders of Playrix about managing a distributed team
    • Podcast on remote work with the company founders
    • How remote work is organized at Playrix
    When will I get an answer to my application?

    You should receive an email that confirms we received your application immediately after you sent it. Don't forget to check your spam folder.

    We try to respond to every application within 3-7 business days. If you have not received a response from us by then, it’s likely that we’re not ready to offer you a position at this time.

    What are the stages of selection for a job opening?

    Usually, the selection process consists of several stages: a questionnaire, a test assignment, and an interview. But depending on the position, the stages themselves and their number may vary.

    What terms do you offer?

    We take care of our employees and offer them highly comfortable working conditions: health insurance, 100% payment for sick leave and vacations, hot meals in our offices, online therapy sessions, and sports and training compensation.

    When will I get an answer after passing the interview/completing the test assignment?

    After each stage, we need some time to make a decision. The response time may vary depending on the position and the project. The recruiter will tell you more about what happens next.
