Director of Compliance

eternalHealth – Your Forever Partner in Healthcare

Healthcare is confusing, but it doesn’t need to be. As a consumer-centric HMO based in Boston, MA, we are a tech-focused start-up that is committed to creating long-lasting partnerships with our members, our providers, and you!

About the role: As the Director of Compliance with eternalHealth you will work to support our Chief Compliance Officer in the creation and maintenance of eternalHealth’s compliance program, to ensure compliance with internal quality standards as well as applicable regulatory requirements. You will have the opportunity to work across workstreams and engage your fellow teammates to ensure a culture of compliance at eternalHealth. You will also help identify and remediate any instances of non-compliance.

We provide a unique opportunity to be a part of a health plan in its beginning stages, by taking advantage of this rare opportunity, you will be able to have insight into all the operations and expertise that is required to run a successful and sustainable plan, as well as have a hand in the development of a culture of compliance. Our team members are flexible and able to play different roles, while staying committed to teamwork and collaboration, and passionate about sustainable change.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement compliance policies, procedures, and practices that comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Monitor changes to regulations to ensure that the organization remains up-to-date and compliant writing and revisions policies and procedures, developing, and conducting training, and engaging with internal and external auditors.
  • Lead the compliance program training and education program.
  • Monitor the organization’s exclusions and monitoring program.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate risk.
  • Five (5) years of directly applicable experience in the healthcare delivery system.
  • Bachelor’s degree in business, Healthcare Administration, Legal, Finance/Accounting or related field of study from an accredited college or university.
  • Excellent written and communication skills.
  • Creative, strategic, and continuously striving for improvement.
  • Flexible and able to ramp-up quickly on different projects to support the team as needed in a fast-paced start-up environment.
  • Professional certifications such as Certification in Healthcare Compliance (CHC), Certified Information Privacy Professional (CIPP), Certified Compliance and Ethics Professional (CCEP), Certified in Healthcare Privacy Compliance (CHPC) or similar are preferred.
