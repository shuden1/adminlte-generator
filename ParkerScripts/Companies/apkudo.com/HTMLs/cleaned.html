Our Core Values

Built on a foundation of respect and connection, our core values are the guiding principles that shape our culture, drive our decisions, and define who we are as a team.

Be you.

We bring our whole selves to our work, our teams, and our customers. The collective “we” will shine bright for all to see.

Proudly accountable.

We follow through because the alternative is not an option. Every word, every action, and every result reflects the improvements we seek to make.

I have your back.

We trust and respect each other to do the right thing when someone needs an extra hand. We go farther together than we do alone.

We communicate confidently and with generosity.

We not only spread ideas, but we also spread understanding to help every team succeed.

Hooked on customers.

Our passion for innovation propels our customers forward. We thrive on solving our customers’ problems, big and small.

We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including:

Paid Time Off

We encourage employees to take time off and provide four weeks of PTO and 12 company-paid holidays, including Juneteenth and the day after Thanksgiving.

Volunteer Time Off

We grant every full-time employee 16 hours each year to use for volunteering. We proudly support all the generous ways Apkudo employees volunteer their time and talents to their communities and causes.

Flexible Work

While some positions require employees to be on-site or on-call, we encourage most employees to manage and flex their own time.

Retirement Planning

We offer a 401(k) with Apkudo matching 100% of contributions for the first 3% and 50% of contributions for the next 2% of each full-time employee's annual salary.

Tech Stipend

Each employee may choose a company-paid Google Pixel phone or a monthly credit to apply toward their tech needs (including upgrading existing network bandwidth).
