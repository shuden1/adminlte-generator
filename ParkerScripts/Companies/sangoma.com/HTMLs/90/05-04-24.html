Careers at Sangoma

At Sangoma, we value the contributions that each of our employees make towards achieving our corporate goals. We strive to ensure that each member of our team is given the tools and support they need to make valuable contributions. Sangoma is committed to providing a stimulating and rewarding work environment, including competitive salaries and benefits.

As part of the Sangoma team, you will have the opportunity to work with globally recognized market leaders who take pride in delivering quality services and market-leading innovations.

Sangoma is always looking for bright, enthusiastic people, and we hire co-op students and recent graduates from our local universities and colleges. Our work environment is casual yet fast-paced and exciting — and we’re supporters of life-long learning to nurture developing skills and careers.

We’re always looking for outstanding candidates to join our team. For information on available positions or to be considered for a career opportunity at Sangoma, check out our current career openings by clicking the ‘Find a Job’ icon above.

Life at Sangoma

We believe every person matters and can make a difference. We’re so passionate about that idea that it’s at the core of our Mission and Values statement.

Here, you’re joining a team that is passionate about helping change the way the world communicates through our innovative products and services. But we’re equally passionate about having fun and being decent, caring people who make a difference in our communities and our world.

About Sangoma

Sangoma (TSX Venture: STC) is the leading provider of enterprise grade, value-based communications solutions. With Sangoma, businesses of all sizes can find affordable cloud and on-premise Unified Communications (UC) systems with advanced functionality.

Sangoma’s global footprint extends to millions of customers in over 150 countries who rely on Sangoma technology for their mission critical communications infrastructure.

Sangoma offers a complete portfolio of next-generation UC solutions, delivering industry-leading quality at price points that maximize customers’ return on investment. Sangoma products and services are developed by the best engineers in the industry and backed by a professional services team that is second to none, offering as much support and assistance as any business needs, up to a fully managed solution.

Core Values

Trust
The willingness to rely on others to get things done and others rely on you to get things done. We are open and transparent communicators. The components of trust are Credibility and Reliability. Credibility is believing what is being said and reliability is believing we live up to our commitments. 
Teamwork
Each team member is committed to working towards the accomplishment of  shared goals and is willing to help colleagues and is trustworthy. Effective Teamwork requires open transparent communication and willingly seeks feedback from team members and provides feedback to team members.
Mutual Respect
Mutual respect occurs when leaders set the example by seeking the opinions of and listening to our employees, customers, and  shareholders. By providing timely and meaningful feedback and coaching to our employees and seeking feedback from our employees demonstrates respect.
Integrity
“Doing the right thing even when no one is looking.” 

Having strong ethical operating  principles  that are  demonstrated by our behaviors. A person with integrity communicates  what they plan to do and then do what they have said they would do.
Customer Centric
Customer centricity is the ability of people in an organization to understand customers' situations, perceptions, and expectations. Customer centricity demands that the customer is the focal point of all decisions related to delivering products, services and experiences to create customer satisfaction, loyalty and advocacy.
ACCOUNTABILITY
Embracing and integrating accountability into everything we do, individually and collectively, to honor our commitments. We take ownership of our work, adhere to deadlines, and strive to deliver excellence, the first time.

Fitness

Cafe

Fun
