Careers & Culture

When you come to work for Cambridge Systematics, you are joining a community of professionals working towards a common goal: make transportation better for future generations.

Join our mission-driven team and experience a culture of collaboration, mutual respect, accountability and learning where individualities are celebrated.

As a Cambridge Systematics employee, you do meaningful work that impacts communities, mobility, safety, operations, livability, economic development and accessibility. And here’s what you will gain:

Inclusivity

Culture is everyone’s responsibility, and it’s always dynamic. Our Culture Charter helps keep our staff grounded in their commitment to upholding our firm’s values and to fostering a culture of collaboration, mutual respect, accountability and learning.

As part of our commitment to culture, CS’ Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Council was formed to create and implement a strategic plan to help our firm become a more equitable and inclusive place to work.

Professional Development

In addition to attending industry conferences, our staff have support in exploring courses and participating in trainings to further their professional development. CS leadership host in-house trainings, like our Associates Boot Camp, for staff at career milestones. As an employee-owned company, we provide a community of peers who are invested in your success.

Rewards & Benefits

Cambridge Systematics demonstrates its commitment to employees with first-in-class benefits, like premium-free dental and medical coverage, that support health, welfare and work-life balance. From your first day on the job, when you arrive to find your office ready for you, we strive to give you all the tools, technology and resources to excel. And for employees who build their careers at Cambridge Systematics, working at an employee-owned company means the ultimate reward—the option to own a stake in the business itself.

  • Generous paid vacation and personal time

  • Health, medical, dental, and vision coverage including premium-free options

  • 401(k), disability and life insurance plans

  • Flexible spending accounts for health and dependent care costs

  • Company stock for eligible employees
