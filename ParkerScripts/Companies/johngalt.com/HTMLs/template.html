    Careers @ John Galt Solutions

    Join the team that makes a difference

    It’s time to love your job again.

    Everyone at John Galt makes an impact, contributes to our customers’ success and our growth. The inclusion of multiple viewpoints contributes to a rich and vibrant workplace.

    When you join the John Galt team, your efforts matter. You help determine our direction. You get to see your ideas and hard work transform into real life actions.

    Why John Galt?

    Smart, Motivated Team

    Join a team of supply chain enthusiasts who have a passion for what they do, a passion to help make an impact on this vital industry.

    Kiss Boredom Goodbye

    It’s time to join a fast-paced, agile environment that is always innovating. Across the organization, we are always on the move, shaping the future of supply chain.

    Work with a Purpose

    We respect your knowledge, abilities, effort and time. Celebrate success, respect each other’s input and hard work, and be rewarded.

    Learn and Grow

    We want to help you expand your capabilities, take on new challenges and succeed. As we each grow, so too does the company.

    Benefits Overview

    Enjoy a generous benefits package as part of the John Galt team. Here are some of the highlights:

    • Health Insurance: John Galt pays 100% toward employee-only coverage and 25% of family coverage.
    • 401(k) Retirement Savings: Company contributed 401(k). Rollovers are accepted immediately.
    • Paid Time Off: 3 weeks of PTO to start.
    • Extended Weekends: An extra paid day off is provided surrounding Spring Holiday, Memorial Day, Fourth of July, and Labor Day.
    • Employee Referral Program: Employees who refer a candidate who is hired will receive a bonus.
    • Work Environment: Extremely casual dress and remote work options
    • Anniversary Bonus: A bonus is awarded for completion of the first year of service.
