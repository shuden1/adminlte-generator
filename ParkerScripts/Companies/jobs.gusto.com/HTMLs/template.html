Curacubby, Inc

Curacubby is a complete online software system designed to help education programs automate registration, enrollment, attendance, demographic tracking, time tracking, communication, billing and payment processing, reporting, safety screening, account management, and business operations– without the integration requirements and complexity found in other solutions. Curacubby provides advanced data analytics and business intelligence to help organizations better manage their programs by exposing transaction data at every level.
