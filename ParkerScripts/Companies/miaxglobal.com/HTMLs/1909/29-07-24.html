    Careers

    Join the MIAX® team.

    MIAX has built a multi-disciplinary team of professionals with deep expertise in the trading industry, working to bring enhanced market structure, technological innovation and superior customer service to the exchange space.  We offer a challenging and rewarding work environment with an entrepreneurial spirit and culture that emphasizes collaboration and teamwork while maintaining commitment to professional ethics.

    MIAX continues to look for talented professionals to fill select roles throughout the company.

    If you are interested in applying for a position, please send your resume, cover letter and salary requirements to hr@miaxglobal.com.

    Current Job Openings

    Minneapolis, MN

    The Minneapolis Grain Exchange, LLC (MGEX), is seeking a Clearing Operations Analyst in its Clearing Division for its Minneapolis, Minnesota offices. The Business Operations Analyst position will be responsible for all execution of critical Clearing activities.

    Roles & Responsibilities

    • Monitoring, reviewing, and reporting on various market activities.
    • Developing and listing new futures and options contracts.
    • Utilizing MGEX market data and trading rules in determining daily intra and end-of-day settlement processes.
    • Reviewing and accounting for cash and Treasury bill collateral calculations and the balancing of all accounts daily.
    • May be involved with various auditing of Clearing Member and Futures Commission Merchants' internal processes and procedures.
    • Involvement with various Risk Management procedures and projects.
    • Communicating with corporate offices, various industry groups, outside regulatory bodies, and member firms of MGEX.   
       

    Education, Experience and Other Qualifications

    • Four-year degree in Accounting, Business Administration, Finance, Economics, similar field or equivalent.
    • Prior audit experience is helpful, but not required.
    • Reliable and organized with a strong attention to detail.
    • Ability to handle multiple tasks on an on-going basis and work in a team environment or independently.
    • Strong written and oral communication skills

    If you are interested in applying for a position, please send your resume, cover letter, and salary requirements to hr@miaxglobal.com.

    Major responsibilities include:

    • Designing and developing high quality, high performance trading systems software written in C++ to meet deliverable timelines and requirements.
    • Adhering to MIAX’s software development life cycle process/methodology.
    • Building business level subject matter expertise in trading systems functionality and processing.
    • Provide second level support for production on an ad hoc basis when necessary.

    Organizational Structure
    The developer will be an integral part of a core development team and report to the Trading System Development management team.

    Qualifications

    • Full software development life cycle experience in a mission critical trading systems environment a must: Options, Equities, Futures, etc.
    • Must possess excellent software design skills and knowledge of advanced data structures.
    • Must have exceptionally strong C++ knowledge and debugging skills in a Linux environment.
    • Solid knowledge of Object Oriented Programming concepts a must.
    • Strong knowledge of TCP/IP multicast and socket programming required.
    • Knowledge of the BOOST libraries and STL required.
    • Must have experience in developing real-time applications in a distributed processing architecture.
    • Must have excellent organizational and communication skills.
    • Must be able to work effectively in a team environment.
    • Strong knowledge of the logical business domain in Options or Equities trading systems a big plus.
    • Experience coding interface solutions for FIX, OPRA, CTA or UTP a big plus.
    • Knowledge of scripting languages such as Python, Shell, and Perl a plus.

    Education and Experience

    • Minimum of a Bachelor's degree or equivalent in IT/Computer Science.
    • 7+ years of experience in C++ development.
    • 5+ years of demonstrated experience in delivering software solutions in a trading systems environment for an Exchange or a Wall Street firm.

    Chicago, IL or Minnesota, MN

    The MGEX Regulatory Department is looking for a Financial Surveillance Lead. 
    The Financial Surveillance Lead position is responsible for protecting the integrity of MGEX futures and options markets by monitoring financial activity for compliance with MGEX and Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) rules. There will be interaction with other commodity markets and regulatory bodies, as well as with market participants.  

    Main responsibilities include

    • Monitor the financial health of MGEX market participants.
    • Analysis of daily financial reports and notices.
    • Financial statement analysis (monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements).
    • Collect and ensure timely submission of statements.
    • Evaluate market risk exposures of market participants.
    • Assist in onboarding new market participants, including by review of required financial statements.

    Organizational Structure
    The Financial Surveillance Lead will be an integral part of the regulatory department of MGEX and report to the Chief Regulatory Officer. 

    Qualifications

    • Attention to detail.
    • Exceptional analytical skills.
    • Strong written and oral communication skills.
    • High level of confidentiality and professionalism.

    Education and Experience

    • Bachelors’ degree in finance, mathematics, accounting or similar field, or equivalent. 
    • Exposure to commodities markets.

    If you are interested in applying for a position, please send your resume, cover letter, and salary requirements to hr@miaxglobal.com.

    Princeton, New Jersey

    If you are a marketing professional who thrives on data-driven decision-making and has a passion for optimizing marketing strategies, especially within the financial sector, with a strong background in marketing technology, we encourage you to apply for the position of Marketing Analytics Manager.

     As a Marketing Analytics Manager at MIAX, you will be a key player in using data to enhance our marketing strategies, improve customer acquisition, and maximize ROI. You will collaborate with various teams to extract insights from data and inform marketing decisions. This position will report directly to the Chief Marketing Officer.

    Main responsibilities include

    • Data Analysis: Analyze marketing data to assess the performance of campaigns, customer behavior, and ROI. Utilize statistical models and data visualization tools to present findings. 
    • Team Leadership: Manage and mentor those in the marketing team to ensure the delivery of actionable insights and reports. Encourage a culture of data-driven decision-making and innovation. 
    • Marketing Technology: Proficiency in marketing technology platforms, such as CRM systems, marketing automation tools, and digital analytics tools. Leverage these platforms for data collection, segmentation, and campaign execution.
    • Campaign Optimization: Collaborate with the marketing team to refine campaign strategies, targeting, and messaging based on data insights. Optimize ad spend and conversion rates.
    • KPI Reporting: Develop and maintain regular reports and dashboards for marketing KPIs, providing senior management with a clear view of marketing performance. 
    • Customer Segmentation: Utilize data to segment and profile customers, tailoring marketing efforts to specific audience groups for improved engagement. 
    • A/B Testing: Plan and oversee A/B tests to evaluate the effectiveness of marketing strategies and identify opportunities for improvement.
    • Market Research: Stay updated on industry trends, competitive analysis, and regulatory changes to adapt marketing strategies accordingly. 
    • Collaboration: Work closely with marketing, product development, and sales teams to align marketing strategies with the overall business goals. 

    Qualifications

    • Bachelor's or Master's degree in marketing, statistics, economics, or a related field. 
    • 5+ years of professional experience. 
    • Proven experience in marketing analytics and data management, preferably within the financial sector.
    • Proficiency in marketing technology platforms such as CRM, marketing automation, and analytics tools, specifically Salesforce. 
    • Familiarity with data analysis tools such as SQL, Excel, Google suite of products (Analytics, Ads, Search Console), and statistical software.
    • Leadership and team management skills.
    • Excellent communication and presentation abilities. 
    • Financial services and regulatory compliance knowledge are a plus. 
    • Ability to think critically and make data-driven marketing decisions.

    If you are interested in applying for a position, please send your resume, cover letter, and salary requirements to hr@miaxglobal.com.

    • Ability to perform real-time reviews involving complex trading issues. 
    • Demonstrate excellent organizational, written and oral communication skills.

    • Familiarity with LiveVol or other market data vendor platforms.

    Education and Experience

    • Working knowledge of equity, options and/or futures exchange trading procedures and operations.
    • Expert-level professional focused on sharpening skills and working under limited guidance and supervision.

    • Regulatory experience at an SRO strongly desired.
