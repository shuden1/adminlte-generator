Our mission

Connect the world to the future of healthcare

“We founded doxy.me to remove cost and complexity as a barrier to telemedicine”.
— Brandon M. Welch, Founder & CEO

We believe the future of healthcare should be available to all, and that cost and complexity should never be a barrier. Every day around the world, millions of minutes of healthcare are delivered through doxy.me.

Designing for the future while supporting the complexity, scale, and volume of care that we enable isn’t easy. We need authentic, smart, and experienced people to help us create the future of healthcare.

At doxy.me, you will have an amazing opportunity to apply your skills and knowledge to overcome important problems in healthcare, work alongside a team of great people, and do something that would make your grandmother proud.

Our values
Our values are part of everything we do
01
Everything starts and ends with the provider
We design, build and maintain doxy.me to benefit and improve the lives of providers. We exist to support providers and put it at the center of every decision we make.
02
Maintain a growth mindset
We push ourselves to expand the reach of what is possible in healthcare through professional development and innovation. We want to get 1% better every single day.
03
Collective wins are better than individual accomplishments
We believe good ideas can come from anywhere. We’re willing to try new things and take data driven approaches to decision-making.
04
Foster an honest and candid environment
We are open, honest, and direct about problems and seek the truth. We assume the good in others and give feedback directly but in a supportive way.
Life at doxy.me

Remote by design

Doxy.me is a remote-first company with hubs in the US and Europe. We seek talent from across the globe, and hubs allow teams to work in the same time zone and organize in-person meetups.

Connected by choice

We are a team during work but also enjoy spending time together outside of the office. Work is better when you’re surrounded by fun people who support you. We find time to connect and build relationships through unique and interesting experiences.

Benefits

Helping our employees thrive

Work your way
Want to come into the office to collaborate in-person, great! Want to work from home and get into a flow state, awesome! We want you to work in the environment you’re most comfortable in.
Time for living
We launched unlimited PTO in 2021 with the belief that our employees and teams can manage their workload without us reviewing vacation balances. We have a high performance culture and time to unwind is crucial to our long term success.
Grow your skills
Company sponsored trainings, certificate programs, tuition reimbursement and learning stipends. Doxy.me employees are in control of their career progression and we give you the tools and support to achieve your goals.
Finding balance
Employee wellness is so important to us, we’ll pay you to take it seriously! Gym reimbursement, mental health benefits, fitness groups, and team lunch stipends are some of the ways we can help you achieve it.
Give back to your community
We are community minded in spirit and doxy.me employees have contributed time and money to charitable causes around the world. We want to leave the world better than we found it and support time off to make that a reality in addition to company matching of charitable donations.
Health and financial wellness
We offer market competitive salaries, a 401k match, and employee profit sharing. Comprehensive medical, dental, & vision insurance are also available for full-time employees.
Please note that benefits vary by country, the ones described on this page are for our US based employees. Benefit information for non-US based positions will be provided to individuals who interview for those roles.
